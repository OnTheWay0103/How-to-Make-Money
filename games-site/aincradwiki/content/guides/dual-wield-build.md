## Why Play Dual-Wield?

Dual-Wield is the **fastest attack-speed weapon** in Echoes of Aincrad. You trade raw single-hit damage for relentless pressure, high combo counts, and the ability to stun-lock many enemies. If you enjoy weaving through attacks while landing ten hits per second, this build is for you.

## Core Mechanics

### Dual Blades Stance

You equip two one-handed swords simultaneously. Each weapon contributes its damage independently, but your attack animations are faster and your combo meter builds twice as fast as any other weapon type.

### Combo Meter

Every landed hit fills your Combo Meter. At full meter:
- Sword Skills deal **+35% damage**
- Each consecutive hit extends combo duration
- Dropping the combo (being hit or not attacking for 3 seconds) resets the meter

The Dual-Wield playstyle is about **maintaining the offensive**. Every dodge, reposition, or defensive action risks resetting your meter.

### Stun-Lock Potential

Fast attacks cause hit-stun on most non-boss enemies. You can keep small-to-medium enemies locked in place until they die — they simply cannot act between your rapid strikes.

## Best Sword Skills

| Skill | Type | When to Use |
|-------|------|------------|
| **Sonic Leap** | Gap closer + multi-hit | Open engagements, chase runners |
| **Double Circular** | AoE spin (dual version) | Farming mob packs |
| **Cross Slash** | Burst damage | Combo finisher, stagger windows |
| **Raging Blows** | Sustained DPS | During boss damage phases |
| **Blade Dance** | Evasive attack | Dodge through attacks while dealing damage |

## Stat Priority

| Stat | Investment | Why |
|------|-----------|-----|
| Dexterity | 40% | Crit rate + attack speed scaling |
| Strength | 25% | Each weapon benefits from raw damage |
| Endurance | 25% | SP regen is critical — you attack constantly |
| Vitality | 10% | You dodge more than you get hit |

**Hybrid stat investment** makes Dual-Wield unique. You need Dexterity for speed and crit, but Strength still contributes a meaningful damage bonus since each hand attacks independently.

## Combo Rotations

### Standard Opening
Sonic Leap (gap close) → 4 light attacks → Cross Slash → back off

### Boss Damage Phase
Blade Dance (evade through attack) → 6 light attacks → Raging Blows (full channel) → Cross Slash

### AoE Farming
Double Circular → 3 light attacks → Double Circular → repeat

## Boss Matchups

| Boss | Dual-Wield Strategy |
|------|--------------------|
| Violet Fencer | Blade Dance through her thrust combos. Stay glued to her — do not let her create distance. |
| Ark Guardian | Risky matchup. The beam attack one-shots you. Stay behind the boss at all times. |
| Twin Wardens | Focus Blades first. Your speed lets you attack between his flurry hits. Dodge Flames' AoE. |
| Golem Kings | Easy matchup. Stun-lock them with constant pressure. Their slow swings cannot hit you. |

## Pros and Cons

**Pros:**
- Highest sustained DPS in the game
- Excellent stun-lock potential
- Fun, fast-paced playstyle
- Scales well into endgame

**Cons:**
- Very SP-hungry (you must manage stamina carefully)
- Combo meter reset is punishing
- Struggles against enemies that knock you back
- Requires good positioning — you must stay close

## Quick Tips

- **SP management is your core skill** — watch your stamina bar like a hawk. Back off and regen at 30%.
- **Do not use Cross Slash at low SP** — it costs 35 SP. You will be stuck with no stamina for 2 seconds afterward.
- **Sonic Leap closes distance AND deals damage** — use it to catch runners, but save it for when terrain is clear.
- **Blade Dance has i-frames** — use it proactively, not reactively. Predict the attack and dance through it.
- **Your combo meter is your damage multiplier** — losing it costs you 35% damage. Play aggressive but smart.
