This tier list ranks every EX-Mod in Echoes of Aincrad based on **general usefulness, build specificity, power gain per slot, and farming difficulty**. Each weapon holds up to 4 EX-Mods (5 with 100% familiarity).

## EX-Mod Tier Rankings

### S-Tier — God Tier, Farm These First

Universally useful EX-Mods that provide massive power spikes for any build.

| EX-Mod | Effect (Max) | Best For | Why S-Tier |
|--------|-------------|----------|------------|
| **Reduced Stamina Cost** | -20% SP cost | All builds | Every build needs SP. No build skips this |
| **Improved Critical Rate** | +12% crit chance | DEX builds, DPS | Doubles damage on crit. ~10-12% overall DPS increase |
| **Counter Damage Up** | +20% counter damage | Parry builds (Rapier, S&S) | Rare separate multiplier bucket — massive gains per slot |
| **Improved Normal Attack** | +16% basic attack | All builds | Basic attacks are 40-60% of your total damage |

**God Roll**: Reduced Stamina Cost + Improved Critical Rate + Counter Damage Up + Improved Normal Attack.

---

### A-Tier — Strong, Build-Specific Powerhouses

These EX-Mods are as strong as S-Tier options within their specific builds, but weaker or useless outside them.

| EX-Mod | Effect (Max) | Best For | Why A-Tier |
|--------|-------------|----------|------------|
| **Improved Charge Attack** | +20% charge damage | Greatsword, Axe | Core damage for heavy weapons |
| **Attack Speed Up** | +12% attack speed | DEX builds, Dagger | More crit rolls per second, better uptime |
| **Stamina Recovery Up** | +20% SP regen | Heavy weapons | Combines with Stamina Cost for infinite SP |
| **Guard Break Up** | +20% stagger buildup | Mace, Greatsword, Axe | Essential for stagger builds |
| **HP Recovery on Kill** | 8% HP per kill | Farming builds | Infinite farming sustain, useless vs bosses |
| **Improved Block** | -20% block damage | Sword & Shield | Near-unkillable tank setup |

---

### B-Tier — Situational or Stat-Stacking Fillers

These EX-Mods are useful in specific situations or as stat-stacking filler when you cannot get S/A-Tier options.

| EX-Mod | Effect (Max) | Best For | Viability |
|--------|-------------|----------|-----------|
| **HP Up** | +16% max HP | All builds, beginners | Safety net, falls off as you improve |
| **Improved Skill Damage** | +16% skill damage | Burst builds | Skills are only 30-40% of total damage |
| **Ranged Damage Up** | +16% ranged damage | Dagger, Magic hybrids | Very situational |
| **Elemental Damage Up** | +16% elemental | Elemental builds | Strong on weakness, weak otherwise |
| **Drop Rate Up** | +10% drop rate | Farming | Zero combat value |
| **Vitality Up** | +8% Vitality | Tank builds | Worse than HP Up (diminishing returns) |
| **Movement Speed Up** | +10% move speed | Kiting builds | Minor combat benefit |
| **Parry Window Extension** | +15% parry window | Beginners learning | Training wheels, swap out later |

## Best EX-Mod Combinations by Build

### Rapier (Dex Crit) — God Roll
**Slot order**: Reduced Stamina Cost > Improved Critical Rate > Counter Damage Up > Improved Normal Attack. Alternate: swap Counter Damage Up for Attack Speed Up.

### Sword & Shield (Balanced)
**Slot order**: Reduced Stamina Cost > Improved Block > Improved Normal Attack > HP Up. Alternate: HP Up for Counter Damage Up if confident in parry timing.

### Greatsword / Two-Handed Axe (Heavy Strength)
**Slot order**: Improved Charge Attack > Reduced Stamina Cost > Stamina Recovery Up > HP Recovery on Kill. Farming setup: replace Reduced Stamina Cost with Guard Break Up.

### Dual-Wield (Burst)
**Slot order**: Reduced Stamina Cost > Attack Speed Up > Improved Critical Rate > Improved Normal Attack.

### Mace (Stagger)
**Slot order**: Guard Break Up > Reduced Stamina Cost > Improved Normal Attack > HP Up.

### Dagger (Speed Crit)
**Slot order**: Improved Critical Rate > Attack Speed Up > Reduced Stamina Cost > Improved Normal Attack.

## How to Farm the Best EX-Mods

**1. Weapon Combining (Cheapest)**: Farm weapon drops (Floor 1 goblin camps), combine same-type weapons to pool EX-Mods, keep S/A-Tier donors.

| Method | Cost per S-Tier EX-Mod |
|--------|----------------------|
| Weapon combining | ~500 G + materials |
| Direct farming | Free |
| Vendor purchase | 2,000-5,000 G |
| EX-Mod transfer | 800-3,000 G |

**2. Boss Farming (Fastest)**: Bosses drop higher-rarity weapons. Violet Fencer (Uncommon), Ark Guardian (Rare), Twin Wardens (Rare), Floor 3+ (Epic). Kill → check → transfer → repeat.

**3. Vendor Scouting**: Save-quit-reload to cycle inventory. Buy weapons with Reduced Stamina Cost (under 1,500 G), Improved Critical Rate (under 3,000 G), or 3+ EX-Mod slots (under 2,000 G).

**4. Material Crafting (Endgame)**: Reduced Stamina Cost = Iron Ore x5 + Beast Hide x3. Improved Critical Rate = Crystal Shard x2 + Refined Steel x1. Counter Damage Up = Boss Essence x1 + Crystal Shard x3. Improved Normal Attack = Iron Ore x3 + Wood Scraps x5.

**Pro tip**: Do not craft common-rarity EX-Mods. The material cost matches Uncommon+, but stat values are 40-60% lower.

## Common EX-Mod Mistakes

- **Stacking duplicates**: Same-type bonuses do not stack. The second slot is wasted.
- **Over-investing in early weapons**: Transfer costs for low-rarity EX-Mods are not worth it.
- **Skipping Reduced Stamina Cost**: The single best EX-Mod in the game. Do not ignore it.
- **Crafting common rarity**: The stat difference between Common and Uncommon is 40-60%. Save materials.
- **Ignoring the 5th hidden slot**: Unlocked at 100% weapon familiarity. Plan your best EX-Mod around it.
