## Weapon Overview

Echoes of Aincrad has 6 weapon types. Your weapon is your class — there are no spellcasters. Each weapon has unique Sword Skills, attack patterns, and playstyles.

| Weapon | Style | Shield? | Speed | Difficulty |
|--------|-------|---------|-------|------------|
| Sword & Shield | Balanced | Yes | Medium | Easy |
| Two-Handed Axe | AoE burst | No | Slow | Medium |
| Dagger | Speed/crit | No | Very Fast | Hard |
| Rapier | Reach/counter | Yes | Fast | Medium |
| Greatsword | Heavy/charge | No | Slow | Medium |
| Mace | Stagger/break | Yes | Medium | Hard |

## 1. Sword & Shield — Best for Beginners

The most forgiving weapon. Block reduces incoming damage and creates counter windows.

- **Pros**: Tanky, simple combos, block saves you from mistakes
- **Cons**: Lower DPS ceiling compared to Rapier or Dagger
- **Best Sword Skills**: Horizontal Arc (AoE slash), Shield Bash (stun)
- **Ideal for**: Learning the game, surviving tough boss fights

## 2. Rapier — The Meta Pick

Fast thrusting weapon with parry counters. Blue flash on enemy attacks signals the parry window — successful parry creates a massive damage opportunity.

- **Pros**: Highest solo DPS ceiling, counter windows trivialize 1v1 fights
- **Cons**: Requires good timing, punished for missed parries
- **Best Sword Skills**: Linear (thrust combo), Star Splash (multi-hit)
- **Ideal for**: Solo players, experienced action RPG fans

## 3. Dagger — Speed Demon

Fastest weapon with high crit rate. Can throw daggers for ranged poke. Fragile but deadly in the right hands.

- **Pros**: Highest attack speed and crit rate, throw for ranged options
- **Cons**: Shortest range, very fragile, unforgiving
- **Best Sword Skills**: Rapid Bite (fast multi-stab), Fad Edge (throw)
- **Ideal for**: High-risk/high-reward players, Dex builds

## 4. Greatsword — Heavy Hitter

Slow, devastating charge attacks. Each swing is a commitment — but landing one changes the fight.

- **Pros**: Highest single-hit damage, charge attacks break shields
- **Cons**: Very slow, dodge becomes a roll (less safe than block)
- **Best Sword Skills**: Avalanche (heavy slam), Cyclone (spin AoE)
- **Ideal for**: Strength builds, farming mob packs, patient players

## 5. Two-Handed Axe — AoE King

Wide sweeping attacks that hit multiple enemies. The best weapon for clearing groups of mobs.

- **Pros**: Best AoE damage, strong guard breaks
- **Cons**: Slow recovery, vulnerable during long animations
- **Best Sword Skills**: Whirlwind (360 AoE), Boulder Breaker (slam)
- **Ideal for**: Mob farming, area control, group encounters

## 6. Mace — Stagger Specialist

Blunt weapon focused on breaking enemy defenses. Builds up stagger on enemies — when the gauge fills, they become vulnerable.

- **Pros**: Staggers and guard-breaks, strong vs shielded enemies
- **Cons**: Lower raw DPS, situational effectiveness
- **Best Sword Skills**: Iron Crusher (guard break), Steel Slam (stagger build)
- **Ideal for**: Support role with partners, fighting shielded enemies

## Which Weapon Should You Pick?

| Your Goal | Pick |
|-----------|------|
| Learn the game | Sword & Shield |
| Max DPS / solo play | Rapier |
| Fast and risky | Dagger |
| Big numbers | Greatsword |
| Farm mobs efficiently | Two-Handed Axe |
| Break tough enemies | Mace |

Remember: you can switch weapons between quests at the Town of Beginnings. Try them all.
