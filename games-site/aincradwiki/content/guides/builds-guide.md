## Build Philosophy

Your "build" in Echoes of Aincrad is defined by three choices:
1. **Weapon** — determines combat style and Sword Skills
2. **Stats** — Growth Point allocation at the Inn
3. **EX-Mods** — weapon modifiers from the Smithy

## Build 1: Dexterity Critical (S-Tier for Solo)

**Best weapons**: Rapier, Dagger
**Playstyle**: Fast, high crit, parry-focused

### Stat Priority
| Stat | Investment | Reason |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Dexterity | Primary (40-50%) | Crit rate and light weapon scaling |
| Vitality | Secondary (20-25%) | Survivability for a fragile build |
| Endurance | Secondary (20-25%) | SP for dodging and parrying |
| Strength | Minimal (5-10%) | Small bonus for Rapier/Dagger scaling |
| Mind | Ignore | Not needed for this build |

### Best EX-Mods
1. **Improved Critical Rate** — +crit chance (priority #1)
2. **Reduced Stamina Cost** — more dodges and parries
3. **Improved Normal Attack** — bread-and-butter damage
4. **Attack Speed Up** — faster combos = more crits

### Combat Flow
Parry (blue flash) → Counter window → Sword Skill combo → Repeat.

## Build 2: Heavy Strength (A-Tier for Farming)

**Best weapons**: Greatsword, Two-Handed Axe
**Playstyle**: Slow, devastating, AoE-focused

### Stat Priority
| Stat | Investment | Reason |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Strength | Primary (50%) | Heavy weapon damage scaling |
| Vitality | Secondary (25%) | You will take hits during long animations |
| Endurance | Secondary (20%) | Charge attacks cost SP |
| Dexterity | Minimal (5%) | Minor benefit |
| Mind | Ignore | Not needed |

### Best EX-Mods
1. **Improved Charge Attack** — core damage source
2. **Stamina Recovery Up** — sustain charge-heavy playstyle
3. **Guard Break Up** — shatter enemy defenses faster
4. **HP Recovery on Kill** — sustain while farming mobs

### Combat Flow
Charge attack → Heavy swing → Guard break → Sword Skill finisher.

## Build 3: Balanced Hybrid (Best for Beginners)

**Best weapons**: Sword & Shield
**Playstyle**: Flexible, survivable, all-rounder

### Stat Priority
| Stat | Investment | Reason |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Vitality | 30% | Survivability first |
| Endurance | 25% | Blocking costs SP |
| Strength | 25% | Sword & Shield scaling |
| Dexterity | 15% | Some crit bonus |
| Mind | 5% | Minor support boost |

### Best EX-Mods
1. **Reduced Stamina Cost** — block more, die less
2. **Improved Block** — further reduce damage on block
3. **Improved Normal Attack** — reliable DPS
4. **HP Up** — more HP pool

### Combat Flow
Block → Counter-attack → Shield Bash (stun) → Sword Skill → Block.

## Build FAQ

**Can I respec?** Growth Point allocation is permanent. Plan ahead.

**Which build is best?** Dexterity Critical (Rapier) has the highest ceiling. Balanced (S&S) is most forgiving.

**When should I specialize?** Start with Balanced for the first 5 levels. Once you understand enemy patterns, respec into Dexterity Critical or Heavy Strength.
