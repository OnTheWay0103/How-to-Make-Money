## S-Tier Builds

### 1. Rapier (Dexterity Critical) — Best for Solo DPS

The undisputed king of 1v1 combat. The parry-counter system creates massive damage windows that no other weapon matches.

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Why S-Tier** | Highest raw DPS, parries trivialize bosses, +40% counter damage |
| **Weakness** | Fragile, punished hard for missed parries |
| **Stats** | Dexterity 50%, Vitality 25%, Endurance 25% |
| **Must-have EX-Mods** | Improved Critical Rate, Counter Damage Up, Reduced Stamina Cost |
| **Best Sword Skills** | Linear (starter), Star Splash (counter), Guillotine (finisher) |

### 2. Dual-Wield — Best for Burst Damage

A late-game unlock trading precision for raw burst and crowd control.

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Why S-Tier** | Insane burst combos, flexible, animation cancel potential |
| **Weakness** | High SP cost, requires good resource management |
| **Stats** | Strength 30%, Dexterity 30%, Endurance 20%, Vitality 20% |
| **Must-have EX-Mods** | Reduced Stamina Cost, Attack Speed Up, Improved Critical Rate |
| **Best Sword Skills** | Sonic Leap (gap closer), Cross Slash (burst), Starburst Stream (ultimate) |

## A-Tier Builds

### 3. Sword & Shield — Best for Beginners and Teams

The most forgiving build. Sacrifices top DPS for unmatched survivability and team utility.

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Why A-Tier** | Block is the safest defensive tool, Shield Bash stuns, easy to play |
| **Weakness** | Lower DPS ceiling, no flashy burst combos |
| **Stats** | Vitality 30%, Endurance 25%, Strength 25%, Dexterity 15%, Mind 5% |
| **Must-have EX-Mods** | Reduced Stamina Cost, Improved Block, Improved Normal Attack |
| **Best Sword Skills** | Horizontal Arc (AoE), Shield Bash (stun), Cross Shield (team buff) |

### 4. Greatsword (Strength) — Best for Farming

The heavy hitter. Clears mobs faster than any other build, but struggles against fast bosses.

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Why A-Tier** | Highest single-hit damage, charge attacks break guards, strong AoE |
| **Weakness** | Very slow, vulnerable during charge, dodge is a roll (worse than block) |
| **Stats** | Strength 50%, Vitality 25%, Endurance 20%, Dexterity 5% |
| **Must-have EX-Mods** | Improved Charge Attack, Stamina Recovery Up, HP Recovery on Kill |
| **Best Sword Skills** | Avalanche (heavy slam), Cyclone (spin AoE), Earth Shaker (shockwave) |

## B-Tier Builds

### 5. Dagger — High Risk, High Reward

Fastest weapon with the highest crit rate. Can rival Rapier DPS on paper, but fragility and short range hold it back.

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Why B-Tier** | Highest attack speed and crit rate, throwing daggers for ranged poke |
| **Weakness** | Short range, very fragile, no block, unforgiving |
| **Stats** | Dexterity 50%, Agility 20%, Endurance 20%, Vitality 10% |
| **Must-have EX-Mods** | Improved Critical Rate, Attack Speed Up, Reduced Stamina Cost |
| **Best Sword Skills** | Rapid Bite (multi-stab), Fad Edge (throw), Shadow Stitch (DoT) |

### 6. Two-Handed Axe — AoE King

The best mob-clearing weapon, but suffers against single targets and bosses.

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Why B-Tier** | Best AoE in the game, excellent stagger damage |
| **Weakness** | Slow, low single-target DPS, long recovery animations |
| **Stats** | Strength 50%, Endurance 25%, Vitality 20%, Dexterity 5% |
| **Must-have EX-Mods** | Guard Break Up, Improved Charge Attack, HP Recovery on Kill |
| **Best Sword Skills** | Whirlwind (360 AoE), Boulder Breaker (slam), War Cry (self-buff) |

### 7. Mace — Stagger Specialist

Built around stagger: fill the stagger bar and enemies become helpless. Powerful in theory but situational.

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Why B-Tier** | Best stagger/guard break, excellent against shielded enemies |
| **Weakness** | Low raw DPS, stagger is worthless on some enemies |
| **Stats** | Strength 35%, Vitality 25%, Endurance 20%, Mind 10%, Dexterity 10% |
| **Must-have EX-Mods** | Guard Break Up, Reduced Stamina Cost, HP Up |
| **Best Sword Skills** | Iron Crusher (guard break), Steel Slam (stagger), Holy Strike (support) |

## Which Build Should You Pick?

| Your Situation | Recommended Build |
|----------------|-------------------|
| New to the game | Sword & Shield |
| Solo, highest DPS | Rapier (Dex Crit) |
| Team/co-op | Sword & Shield or Mace |
| Farming mobs | Greatsword or Two-Handed Axe |
| High-skill challenge | Dagger |
| Best endgame | Dual-Wield (late-game) |

## Tier List Changes by Progression Stage

| Stage | S-Tier | A-Tier |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Floors 1-3 | Sword & Shield | Greatsword, Rapier |
| Floors 4-6 | Rapier | Sword & Shield, Greatsword |
| Floors 7-10 | Rapier, Dual-Wield | Greatsword, Sword & Shield |
| Floor 10+ | Dual-Wield | Rapier, Mace (team) |

The meta shifts as you unlock more EX-Mod slots and Sword Skills. Do not judge a build by its early-game performance alone.
