## What Are EX-Mods?

EX-Mods are weapon modifiers that significantly enhance your combat capabilities. Each weapon can hold up to 4 EX-Mods. They are crafted, combined, and transferred at the Smithy in the Town of Beginnings.

## Unlocking the Smithy

The Smithy unlocks after you reserve a room at the Inn. This happens early in the main quest — usually within the first 1-2 hours.

## Core Systems

### 1. Improve Base Parameters

Use materials and spare weapons to boost a weapon's base stats (attack power, durability). This is the simplest upgrade path — good for early game when you lack EX-Mods.

### 2. Combine Weapons (EX-Mod Pooling)

Combine two weapons of the same type to pool their EX-Mods onto one weapon. The resulting weapon keeps up to 4 EX-Mods (the highest-rank ones are prioritized).

**Example**: Weapon A has EX-Mods [Attack +5%, HP +10]. Weapon B has EX-Mods [Crit +3%, Stamina -8%]. Combining A + B at the Smithy creates a weapon with [Attack +5%, HP +10, Crit +3%, Stamina -8%].

### 3. EX-Mod Transfer

Move an EX-Mod from one weapon to another. This costs materials and Gyldenblood (in-game currency). Use this to:
- Move a rare EX-Mod from a weak weapon to your main weapon
- Preserve EX-Mods when upgrading to a higher-rarity weapon

**Transfer cost increases with EX-Mod rarity**. Epic/Legendary EX-Mods are expensive to move — plan transfers carefully.

## Material Farming

### Common Materials
- **Iron Ore** — dropped by goblins and mined from Floor 1 caves
- **Beast Hide** — dropped by Dire Wolves (Forest area, Floor 1)
- **Wood Scraps** — from wooden crates and barrel objects

### Rare Materials
- **Crystal Shards** — rare drop from Ark bosses
- **Refined Steel** — Smithy crafting using Iron Ore × 5
- **Boss Essence** — guaranteed drop from floor bosses

### Best Farming Route (Early Game)
1. Start at Floor 1 Forest checkpoint
2. Clear Dire Wolf packs (Beast Hide + XP)
3. Clear goblin camps (Iron Ore + weapon drops)
4. Fast travel back to checkpoint
5. Repeat

This loop yields enough materials for 2-3 EX-Mod upgrades per hour.

## EX-Mod Rarity

| Rarity | Max EX-Mod Slots | Effect Range | Transfer Cost |
|--------|-----------------|--------------|---------------|
| Common | 2 | Low (+5-8%) | Low |
| Uncommon | 3 | Medium (+10-12%) | Medium |
| Rare | 3 | High (+15-18%) | High |
| Epic | 4 | Very High (+20%) | Very High |

## Quick Tips

- **Combine before transferring** — pooling EX-Mods via combination is cheaper than individual transfers
- **Keep a "donor" weapon** — store a weapon with good EX-Mods to transfer onto future upgrades
- **Don't over-invest in early weapons** — you will replace them. Save rare EX-Mods for weapons you will use for 5+ hours
- **Check the Smithy after every boss** — bosses drop materials needed for high-tier EX-Mod crafting
