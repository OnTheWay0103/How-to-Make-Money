## Floor Overview

Aincrad has 10 cleared floors in the current version of Echoes of Aincrad. Each floor has a unique theme, enemy roster, field boss, and dungeon culminating in a floor boss. Completing a floor boss unlocks the next floor's teleport gate.

### Recommended Order

Always complete floors in numerical order. You can technically skip ahead using teleport gates unlocked by other players, but you will be underleveled and poorly geared.

## Floor 1 — Town of Beginnings

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Recommended Level** | 1-10 |
| **Theme** | Green fields, forests, starter town |
| **Enemies** | Dire Wolves, Goblins, Giant Bats |
| **Field Boss** | Goblin Chieftain (Lv8) |
| **Floor Boss** | Illfang the Kobold Lord (Lv10) |

**Key Quests**:
- "Welcome to Aincrad" — tutorial quest, rewards starter weapon
- "Goblin Problem" — kill 20 goblins, rewards healing potions recipe
- "The Foreboding Fortress" — unlocks Floor 1 dungeon

**Notable Rewards**:
- First weapon from tutorial quest (choose wisely — it is your main weapon for 5+ levels)
- Smithy unlock after reserving Inn room
- Basic crafting recipes from shop vendors

**Tips**:
- Complete every side quest before attempting the floor boss
- Farm goblin camp for Iron Ore (your first upgrade materials)
- The Floor 1 dungeon has a hidden chest with an Uncommon weapon

## Floor 2 — Crystal Caverns

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Recommended Level** | 10-15 |
| **Theme** | Underground caves, crystal formations |
| **Enemies** | Crystal Golems, Cave Bats, Rock Spiders |
| **Field Boss** | Crystal Guardian (Lv13) |
| **Floor Boss** | Wyvern of the Deep (Lv15) |

**Key Quests**:
- "Into the Depths" — enter the Crystal Caverns
- "Crystal Clear" — collect 10 Crystal Shards
- "Wyvern Hunt" — floor boss unlock quest

**Notable Rewards**:
- Crystal Shards (crafting material for upgrade tier 2)
- Uncommon armor pieces from Crystal Guardian
- First accessory drop from chests

**Tips**:
- Bring a torch item (bought from Floor 1 general store) — some caves are pitch black
- Crystal Golems are weak to blunt damage (Shield builds have an advantage here)
- The Wyvern's breath attack has a 2-second wind-up — easy to dodge once you learn it

## Floor 3 — Flooded City

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Recommended Level** | 15-20 |
| **Theme** | Sunken city, water canals |
| **Enemies** | Water Spirits, Drowned Soldiers, Giant Leeches |
| **Field Boss** | Leviathan Spawn (Lv18) |
| **Floor Boss** | Kraken Tentacle (Lv20) |

**Key Quests**:
- "Rising Tide" — explore the flooded district
- "Ghosts of the Past" — defeat Drowned Soldiers (story quest)
- "Kraken's Reach" — floor boss unlock

**Notable Rewards**:
- Water resistance accessories (useful for future water zones)
- Uncommon-rare weapons from underwater chests
- Leviathan Spawn drops rare ring (one of the first good accessories)

**Tips**:
- Some areas require swimming — SP drains faster in water. Plan your route.
- Water Spirits are immune to Fire damage. Switch your Mage to Ice or Arcane.
- The floor boss does not move — it is a stationary tentacle. Attack from the side, not the front.

## Floor 4 — Plains of War

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Recommended Level** | 20-25 |
| **Theme** | War-torn plains, ruined fortresses |
| **Enemies** | Harpies, War Golems, Deserters |
| **Field Boss** | War Golem Commander (Lv23) |
| **Floor Boss** | General Aldrich (Lv25) |

**Key Quests**:
- "Call to Arms" — report to the war camp
- "Golem Research" — collect 5 Golem Cores
- "The General's Downfall" — floor boss unlock

**Notable Rewards**:
- First Rare weapons from War Golem Commander
- Refined Steel in bulk drops
- War Camp vendor sells Uncommon armor sets

**Tips**:
- This is the first difficulty spike. Ensure your gear is upgraded to at least Uncommon tier.
- Harpies fly — use ranged attacks or wait for them to dive.
- General Aldrich has a 3-hit combo. Parry the third hit (it has a blue flash).

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Recommended Level** | 25-30 |
| **Theme** | Grand library, arcane halls |
| **Enemies** | Animated Armor, Floating Tomes, Arcane Wisps |
| **Field Boss** | Arcane Construct (Lv28) |
| **Floor Boss** | The Archivist (Lv30) |

**Key Quests**:
- "Knowledge Seeker" — enter the Library
- "Tome Collection" — collect 10 Spell Tomes
- "Silence the Archivist" — floor boss unlock

**Notable Rewards**:
- Best mage weapons in the game at this tier
- Spell Tomes for EX-Mod crafting
- Rare accessories with Intelligence bonuses

**Tips**:
- This floor strongly favors ranged builds. Melee users will struggle with Floating Tomes.
- Animated Armor is weak to Electric damage.
- The Archivist teleports frequently — do not chase. Wait for it to reappear.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Recommended Level** | 30-33 |
| **Theme** | Swampland, toxic mist, ruins |
| **Enemies** | Giant Frogs, Poison Slimes, Bog Zombies |
| **Field Boss** | Swamp Wyrm (Lv32) |
| **Floor Boss** | Corrupted Treant (Lv33) |

**Key Quests**:
- "Through the Mire" — cross the swamp
- "Cleanse the Corruption" — story quest
- "The Treant's Heart" — floor boss unlock

- Poison resistance accessories
- Rare armor set with HP regeneration
- Unique EX-Mod: Poison Damage on Hit

**Tips**:
- The mist reduces visibility. Learn the path before engaging enemies.
- Poison Slimes split on death — kill them one at a time, not in groups.
- Corrupted Treant has a healing phase. Interrupt it with Shield Bash or a heavy hit.

## Floor 7 — Lava Fields

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Recommended Level** | 33-38 |
| **Theme** | Volcanic landscape, lava rivers |
| **Enemies** | Lizardmen, Fire Demons, Magma Elementals |
| **Field Boss** | Flame Demon Lord (Lv36) |
| **Floor Boss** | Ifrit, Core of the Mountain (Lv38) |

**Key Quests**:
- "Into the Fire" — enter the Lava Fields
- "Demon Slayer" — kill 15 Fire Demons
- "Ifrit's Prison" — floor boss unlock

**Notable Rewards**:
- Epic weapons from Flame Demon Lord
- Fire resistance armor (mandatory for this floor)
- Boss Essence for Legendary crafting

**Tips**:
- Lava deals damage over time. Do not stand in it. Do not fall in.
- Lizardmen are aggressive and patrol in packs. Pull them with ranged attacks.
- Ifrit has a floor-wide AoE at 50% HP. Save your Iron Fortress or Mana Shield for this.

| Aspect | Details |

| **Recommended Level** | 38-42 |

| **Enemies** | Ice Golems, Frost Wolves, Yetis |
| **Field Boss** | Frost Giant King (Lv40) |
| **Floor Boss** | Permafrost Dragon (Lv42) |

**Key Quests**:
- "The Frozen Wastes" — enter the Tundra
- "Warmth in the Cold" — collect 10 Fire Essence for a camp upgrade
- "Dragon's Lair" — floor boss unlock

**Notable Rewards**:
- Ice resistance accessories
- Epic armor set with defense bonuses
- Legendary crafting component: Dragon Scale

**Tips**:
- Cold status slows your movement. Bring fire-based food buffs to counter it.
- Yetis have a grab attack that one-shots low-Vitality builds. Do not get grabbed.
- Permafrost Dragon flies — bring ranged options or wait for it to land.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Recommended Level** | 42-46 |
| **Theme** | Roman-style arena, gladiator challenges |
| **Enemies** | Gladiator NPCs, War Beasts, Champion Spirits |
| **Field Boss** | Arena Champion (Lv44) |
| **Floor Boss** | Emperor Gladiator (Lv46) |

**Key Quests**:
- "Enter the Arena" — win your first match
- "Gladiator's Rise" — win 10 matches
- "The Emperor Falls" — floor boss unlock

**Notable Rewards**:
- Unique weapons with PvP-oriented EX-Mods
- Colosseum Champion title (cosmetic)
- Rare accessories with multi-stat bonuses

**Tips**:
- The Colosseum features humanoid enemies that parry and dodge. Treat them like PvP opponents.
- Attack patterns are faster here than anywhere else. Prioritize Dexterity and attack speed.
- The floor boss has 2 phases — human form and beast form. Both must be learned separately.
- **Only floor with PvP-enabled zones**. Watch for player invaders in the arena outskirts.

## Floor 10 — Sky Fortress

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Recommended Level** | 46-50 |
| **Theme** | Floating castle, clouds, celestial halls |
| **Enemies** | Angelic Knights, Sky Serpents, Celestial Wisps |
| **Field Boss** | Celestial Warden (Lv48) |
| **Floor Boss** | Angel of the Sundered Skies (Lv50) |

**Key Quests**:
- "Ascension" — unlock the Sky Fortress teleport
- "Celestial Purge" — clear all enemy types in the fortress
- "Fallen Angel" — final floor boss unlock

**Notable Rewards**:
- Legendary weapons and armor (endgame gear)
- Legendary Shards for ultimate upgrades
- Exclusive EX-Mod: Celestial Blessing (party-wide buff)

**Tips**:
- This is the current endgame content. Bring max-level gear, full EX-Mods, and plenty of potions.
- Angelic Knights have a party-wipe attack at low HP. Spread out when they glow.
- The floor boss is the hardest fight in the game. Study its pattern for at least 5 attempts before trying to win.
- **Celebrate** — clearing Floor 10 is the current endgame achievement.

## Quick Tips

- **Complete every quest on each floor** — side quests award skill points, EX-Mod slots, and permanent stat bonuses.
- **Always unlock the teleport gate** — the first thing you should do on any new floor is find and activate the gate.
- **Check floor vendors** — each floor's vendor sells unique items. Floor 3 sells water-walking potions. Floor 7 sells fire resistance gear.
- **Return to earlier floors** — some quests and secrets require high-level stats or skills to access on low floors.
- **The difficulty jump between floors is not linear** — Floors 4 and 7 are notable spikes. Prepare accordingly.
