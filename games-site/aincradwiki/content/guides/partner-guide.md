## Partner System Overview

You never fight alone in Echoes of Aincrad. AI partners join every quest with their own:
- Fighting style (aggressive, defensive, balanced)
- Weapon preference
- Support Skills (healing, buffs, debuffs)
- Combination Skills (duo attacks)

## How Partners Work

### Combat Behavior
Partners fight autonomously but respond to your commands:
- **Free Mode**: Partner attacks your target or the nearest enemy
- **Switch Mode**: Partner draws aggro while you reposition, heal, or set up combos

### Support Skills
Assigned to your action wheel. Partners use them on cooldown or when conditions are met (e.g., you drop below 50% HP).

### Combination Skills
Powerful duo attacks triggered after both you and your partner build enough damage on the same target. A gauge fills during combat — when full, unleash the Combination Skill for massive damage.

## Partner Types

### Asuna (Balanced Fighter)
- **Weapon**: Rapier
- **Style**: Aggressive, high DPS
- **Support Skill**: Healing (restores 30% HP)
- **Best for**: General use, speedrunning content

### Klein (Tank)
- **Weapon**: Sword & Shield
- **Style**: Defensive, draws aggro
- **Support Skill**: Defense Up (20% damage reduction for 30s)
- **Best for**: Difficult bosses, survival-focused runs

### Agil (Heavy Hitter)
- **Weapon**: Two-Handed Axe
- **Style**: Aggressive, AoE focus
- **Support Skill**: Attack Up (15% damage increase for 30s)
- **Best for**: Farming, clearing mobs quickly

### Lisbeth (Support Specialist)
- **Weapon**: Mace
- **Style**: Supportive, stagger-focused
- **Support Skill**: Party Heal (restores 20% HP to both)
- **Best for**: Long boss fights, dungeon runs

## Best Partner by Situation

| Situation | Best Partner | Why |
|-----------|-------------|-----|

| Boss fights | Lisbeth | Party heal sustains long fights |
| Speed farming | Agil | Attack Up buff + AoE clears packs fast |

## Switch Mode Tactics

Switch Mode is activated from the command wheel. Your partner becomes the focus of enemy attention for 15 seconds.

### When to Switch
- **You need to heal** — Switch, pop healing items, switch back
- **You are low on SP** — Switch, let SP recover, switch back
- **Setting up a combo** — Switch, position behind the enemy, unleash your full combo
- **Partner is low HP** — Do NOT switch. Switch Mode on a dying partner gets them killed.

### Switch Mode Cooldown
30 seconds. Use it strategically — do not waste it on trash mobs.

## Building Combination Skill Gauge

The gauge fills faster when:
- Both you and your partner attack the same target
- You land consecutive hits without taking damage
- You trigger counter windows (parries)

Once full, the gauge stays full until used. **Save it for bosses** — do not waste Combination Skills on normal enemies.

## Partner Progression

### How Partners Improve
Partners scale with your Cardinal Rank. At higher ranks:
- They learn new Support Skills
- Their stats increase
- Combination Skill damage increases
- New dialogue and story interactions unlock

### Partner-Specific Quests
Each partner has optional side quests that improve their abilities. Complete these when available — the upgrades are significant.

## Quick Tips

- **Healing Support Skill is the best** — always have at least one heal-equipped partner
- **Partners cannot die permanently** — but if they fall in combat, you fight alone until the next checkpoint
- **Switch Mode is not a panic button** — use it proactively, not reactively
- **Combination Skill gauge persists between fights** — build it on trash, spend it on the boss
- **Experiment with all partners** — their playstyles complement different weapon choices
