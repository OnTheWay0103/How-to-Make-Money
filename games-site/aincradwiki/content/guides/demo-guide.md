## What Is the Echoes of Aincrad Demo?

The Echoes of Aincrad demo is a free, limited version of the full game. It lets you experience the opening hours of Aincrad, try multiple weapons, and face the first floor boss — all without spending a dime. Progress carries over to the full game, making it a risk-free way to start your journey.

## How to Download the Demo

### Steam (PC)
1. Open Steam and search for "Echoes of Aincrad"
2. Scroll down to the **Downloadable Content / Demo** section
3. Click **"Download Demo"** (approximately 15 GB)
4. Install and launch

**Tip**: Add the game to your wishlist from the demo page — you will receive a notification when the full game launches or goes on sale.

### PlayStation 5
1. Open the PlayStation Store
2. Search for "Echoes of Aincrad"
3. Select the game tile and choose **"Demo"** from the options
4. Download (approximately 18 GB with 4K textures)

### Xbox Series X|S
1. Open the Microsoft Store on your console
2. Search for "Echoes of Aincrad"
3. Select **"Free Demo"** under the game listing
4. Download and install

## Demo Content Scope

The demo covers the **first floor** of Aincrad, which is roughly the opening 4-6 hours of gameplay depending on your pace. Here is exactly what is included:

| Feature | Available in Demo? | Notes |
|---------|-------------------|-------|
| Floor 1 main quests | Yes | Full campaign up to the Violet Fencer boss |
| Side quests | Yes | ~8 side quests available |
| All 6 weapon types | Yes | You can try every weapon before committing |
| Smithy / EX-Mod system | Yes | Up to 3 EX-Mod slots (reduced from 4) |
| Partner system | Yes | Partner available, affinity capped at 50 |
| Inn / Growth Points | Yes | Full stat system, respec not available |
| Floor 1 Violet Fencer boss | Yes | Can be fought repeatedly for practice |
| Floor 2+ content | No | Full game required |
| Multiplayer | No | Demo is single-player only |
| Floor 1 optional bosses | Yes | Ark Guardian is included |
| Achievements / Trophies | Partial | Demo achievements carry to full game |
| Photo Mode | Yes | Fully functional |

**Level cap in demo**: Your character cannot exceed Cardinal Rank 25. Growth Points earned beyond this are saved and applied when you transfer to the full game.

## Best Demo Builds

Since the demo caps your options at Floor 1, certain builds perform noticeably better than others.

### Sword & Shield — Best for Demo (S-Tier)

The safest choice. Block makes early learning forgiving. **Stats**: Vitality (30%) > Endurance (25%) > Strength (25%). **EX-Mods**: Reduced Stamina Cost, Improved Block, Improved Normal Attack. **Boss**: Parry the Violet Fencer's lunge, block everything else.

### Rapier — Best for Experienced Players (A-Tier)

Fastest boss kills in the demo. **Stats**: Dexterity (50%) > Vitality (25%) > Endurance (25%). **EX-Mods**: Improved Critical Rate, Counter Damage Up, Reduced Stamina Cost. **Boss**: Parry every lunge for massive counter damage.

### Greatsword — Best for Farming (A-Tier)

One-shots goblins and Dire Wolves. **Stats**: Strength (50%) > Vitality (25%) > Endurance (25%). **EX-Mods**: Improved Charge Attack, Guard Break Up, HP Recovery on Kill. **Boss**: Charge attack during recovery windows.

### Builds to Avoid

| Build | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Dagger | Too fragile, limited EX-Mod slots hurt scaling |
| Two-Handed Axe | AoE is overkill for demo mob density |
| Mace | Stagger is less valuable against demo enemies |

## Save Transfer: Everything You Need to Know

**Yes, demo progress carries over to the full game.** This includes:

| Transfers | Does Not Transfer |
|-----------|------------------|
| Character level and Growth Points | Multiplayer progress (new save on full game) |
| All equipped weapons and gear | Shop/vendor refresh timers |
| EX-Mod progress on weapons | Partner affinity over 50 (capped at 50 in demo) |
| Quest completion status | Tutorial quest checklist (auto-completed) |
| Floor 1 boss clear status | Photo Mode screenshots (saved locally) |
| Cardinal Rank | Settings and control configurations |

### How to Transfer
1. Complete the demo and exit to the main menu
2. Purchase and install the full game
3. Launch the full game — it will detect your demo save automatically
4. Confirm the import prompt
5. Continue from where you left off

**Warning**: Do not delete your demo save file after purchasing the full game. The transfer happens on first launch only. If you skip the prompt, you can trigger it manually from Settings > Save Data > Import Demo Save.

## Why Play the Demo?

The demo is the same build as the first 10% of the full game. Everything you earn carries over — levels, gear, and progress. Try all 6 weapons, practice the Violet Fencer boss, and decide if Aincrad is for you with zero commitment. If you enjoy the demo, you will enjoy the full game.
