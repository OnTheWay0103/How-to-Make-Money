## Why Play Rapier?

Rapier is the **highest DPS weapon** in Echoes of Aincrad when mastered. It combines long reach, fast attacks, and a parry counter system that creates massive damage windows. In the right hands, no other weapon matches its solo potential.

## Core Mechanics

### Parry Counter

The Rapier's defining feature. When an enemy attack flashes **blue**, you are in the parry window. A successful parry:
- Negates all damage
- Staggers the enemy briefly
- Opens a **counter window** (enemy takes +40% damage for 3 seconds)

The flow: **Watch for blue flash → Parry → Counter window → Sword Skill combo.**

### Linear Thrust Combo

Your bread-and-butter attack chain. Fast, long reach, builds combo meter.

### Star Splash

Multi-hit Sword Skill. Use during counter windows for maximum damage. Each hit can crit independently, making it devastating with high Dexterity.

## Best Sword Skills

| Skill | Type | When to Use |
|-------|------|------------|
| **Linear** | Combo starter | Open every engagement |
| **Star Splash** | Multi-hit DPS | During counter windows |
| **Guillotine** | Finisher | When enemy is below 30% HP |
| **Piercing Ray** | Ranged poke | Safe damage, kite enemies |

## Stat Priority

| Stat | Investment | Why |
|------|-----------|-----|
| Dexterity | 50% | Crit rate + rapier scaling = your damage |
| Vitality | 25% | Survive missed parries |
| Endurance | 25% | SP for parries and dodges |

**Do not invest in Strength.** Rapier scales almost entirely with Dexterity.

## Parry Timing Guide

### Visual Cue
The blue flash appears about 0.3 seconds before the attack lands. Press parry when you see it — not before, not after.

### Enemy-Specific Timings

| Enemy Type | Parry Window | Tip |
|-----------|-------------|-----|
| Goblins | Long, generous | Good for practice |
| Dire Wolves | Medium | Watch the leap animation |
| Violet Fencer | Short | Parry the Lunge only (hardest move to dodge) |
| Twin Warden Blades | Very short | Parry the third hit of Flurry — it has a blue flash |
| Ark Guardian | None | Beam cannot be parried — dodge only |

### Practice Routine
1. Go to Floor 1 Forest
2. Find a lone goblin
3. Do not attack — only parry
4. Parry 10 times in a row without taking damage
5. Now add attacks after each parry

## Solo Boss Strategy

The Rapier excels in 1v1 boss fights:

1. **Learn the pattern** — spend the first 30 seconds only dodging and observing
2. **Parry the telegraphed moves** — most bosses have 1-2 attacks with generous parry windows
3. **Counter window → Star Splash** — this is your damage phase
4. **When the boss enrages** — play safer. Parry windows shrink but the patterns are the same.
5. **Guillotine to finish** — below 30% HP, your finisher deals bonus damage

## Quick Tips

- **Patience wins** — a successful parry into counter combo does more damage than 10 reckless attacks
- **Do not spam attacks** — Rapier thrives on rhythm, not button mashing
- **Learn the sound cue** — the blue flash has an accompanying audio ping. Use it.
- **Star Splash has end lag** — do not use it if you might need to parry immediately after
