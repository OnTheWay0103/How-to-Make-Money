## Boss Mechanics Overview

Bosses in Echoes of Aincrad are **gate-locked behind barriers**. Once the fight starts, it ends only when one side dies — no retreat, no escape.

### Pre-Boss Checklist
Before every boss fight:
- [ ] Weapon tuned with EX-Mods (Stamina reduction + Attack boost minimum)
- [ ] Partner with healing or defensive Support Skills equipped
- [ ] Full stock of healing items
- [ ] Cardinal Rank, weapon upgrades, and stats aligned
- [ ] Recent save (use checkpoint before boss room)

### Universal Boss Tips
- Open with light attacks and parries to learn patterns
- Save heavy/special attacks for confirmed openings
- Use Switch Mode to have your partner draw aggro
- Fire Combination Skills once damage is built up
- Watch for the blue flash — it signals a parry window

## Floor 1: The Violet Fencer

**Location**: Floor 1 — End of the Forest Dungeon
**Difficulty**: Medium
**Recommended**: Vitality 3+, Endurance 3+, Damage stat 3+, Uncommon weapon with 2 EX-Mods

### Attack Patterns
- **Thrust Combo** (3 hits): Dodge sideways on the third thrust
- **AoE Slash** (360°): Backstep immediately — large radius
- **Lunge** (gap closer): Parry the blue flash for a counter window
- **Enrage** (below 30% HP): Attacks become 30% faster

### Strategy
1. **Phase 1 (100-50%)**: Play defensively. Block/dodge, learn the rhythm. Attack only after her Thrust Combo finishes.
2. **Phase 2 (50-30%)**: She adds the AoE Slash. Stay close — the AoE is easier to dodge at close range (smaller effective radius).
3. **Phase 3 (30-0%)**: Enrage. Her combos are faster but the patterns are the same. Do not panic-dodge. Save Combination Skill for the finish.

### Best Weapons
- **Sword & Shield**: Block everything, punish after combos
- **Rapier**: Parry the Lunge for massive counter damage

### Partner Setup
Bring a partner with healing Support Skill. Use Switch Mode to let them tank the Enrage phase while you heal.

## Floor 1: Ark Guardian (Optional)

**Location**: Floor 1 — Sealed Chamber (requires key from side quest)
**Difficulty**: Hard
**Recommended**: Vitality 5+, weapon with 3 EX-Mods

### Attack Patterns
- **Ground Slam** (AoE shockwave): Jump or dodge
- **Charge** (linear): Sidestep — do NOT block (guard break)
- **Summon Adds**: At 50% HP, summons 3 goblins — kill them first
- **Beam Attack** (linear, telegraphed): Easy dodge but one-shots if it hits

### Strategy
Kill adds immediately when they spawn. The Guardian's beam attack has a long wind-up — use the time to heal and rebuff. Mace is surprisingly effective — stagger buildup interrupts the beam charge.

## Floor 2: Twin Wardens

**Location**: Floor 2 — The Split Gate
**Difficulty**: Very Hard
**Recommended**: Vitality 8+, Damage stat 8+, Rare weapon with 4 EX-Mods

### Attack Patterns
Two bosses fight simultaneously:
- **Warden of Blades** (fast, melee): Flurry attacks, bleed debuff
- **Warden of Flames** (ranged, magic): Fire projectiles, AoE flame pools

### Strategy
1. **Kill Blades first** — his bleed will drain your healing items if left unchecked
2. Use Switch Mode to have your partner occupy Flames while you fight Blades
3. **Parry Blades' Flurry** — the third hit has a blue flash window
4. After Blades dies, Flames enrages (faster projectiles, larger AoE). Close the gap and stay aggressive.
5. **Dodge, don't block** — Flames' attacks cannot be blocked

### Best Weapons
- **Dagger**: Speed to match Blades, throw to poke Flames at range
- **Greatsword**: Charge attacks during Flames' long cast animations
