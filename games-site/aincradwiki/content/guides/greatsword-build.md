## Why Play Greatsword?

Greatsword is the **hardest-hitting weapon** in Echoes of Aincrad. Each swing is a commitment — but a single charged attack can end a fight. If you enjoy big numbers, patient combat, and devastating finishers, this is your weapon.

## Core Mechanics

### Charge Attack

Hold the attack button to charge. Release at max charge for:
- +100% damage
- Guard break (cannot be blocked)
- Wide AoE (hits multiple enemies)

Charge attacks are slow. You are vulnerable while charging. Timing is everything.

### Cyclone

Spin-to-win AoE Sword Skill. Covers 360° around you. Your best farming tool.

### Avalanche

Heavy overhead slam. Highest single-hit damage in the game. Use against staggered or slow enemies.

### Dodge Roll

Unlike shield weapons, Greatsword users dodge-roll instead of blocking. This is less safe but more mobile — use it to reposition for charge attacks.

## Best Sword Skills

| Skill | Type | When to Use |
|-------|------|------------|
| **Cyclone** | AoE spin | Farming mobs, clearing packs |
| **Avalanche** | Single-target slam | Boss damage phase, stunned enemies |
| **Helm Breaker** | Gap closer | Close distance on ranged enemies |
| **Earth Splitter** | Line AoE | Enemies in a row, corridor fights |

## Stat Priority

| Stat | Investment | Why |
|------|-----------|-----|
| Strength | 50% | Greatsword scaling — your damage |
| Vitality | 25% | You WILL take hits during charge animations |
| Endurance | 25% | Charge attacks cost heavy SP |

**Do not invest in Dexterity.** Greatsword scales with Strength and has low base crit rate.

## Combat Flow

### Vs. Mobs (Farming)
Charge attack → walk into pack → release → Cyclone to clean up survivors. HP Recovery on Kill keeps you topped off.

### Vs. Bosses
1. Dodge their attacks, stay at mid-range
2. Charge attack during their recovery animations
3. Helm Breaker to close gaps after dodging
4. Avalanche when the boss is staggered
5. Repeat

### Vs. Fast Enemies (Goblins, Wolves)
Do NOT charge. Use light attacks instead. Charge only when they are staggered or recovering from an attack.

## Farming Strategy

Greatsword is one of the best farming weapons thanks to AoE:

1. Find a mob-dense area (Floor 1 Forest goblin camp is ideal)
2. Run in circles to group enemies together
3. Charge attack into the pack
4. Cyclone to finish
5. Collect drops, move to next pack

**With HP Recovery on Kill EX-Mod**, you can farm indefinitely without healing items.

| Twin Wardens | Focus Blades. Charge attacks interrupt his Flurry. Dodge Flames' projectiles. |

## Quick Tips

- **Patience > aggression** — one charged hit is worth five light attacks
- **Learn enemy recovery windows** — that is when you charge
- **Dodge roll has i-frames** — use them to roll through attacks, not away from them
- **Do not charge against fast enemies** — you will get hit every time
- **Weapon weight matters** — Greatsword is heavy. You cannot cancel animations. Commit to each swing.
