## What is Grain Rot?

Grain Rot is a horror co-op extraction builder developed by Beck & Branch Games and published by Neem Interactive. You play as a **Living Spark** — a fragment of soul possessing fragile wooden vessels. You and up to 3 friends descend via a cursed elevator into procedurally shifting underground ruins to scavenge loot, fight Corrupted enemies, and extract before the Rot consumes you.

Between runs, you rebuild your Outpost — unlocking new vessels, constructs, stat upgrades, and rescued survivors. If your vessel is destroyed, your Spark escapes to possess a new body. But if everyone dies, all unextracted loot is lost.

- **20+ Vessels** — From Ironbark Guardian to Whisper-Wood Scout, each with unique abilities
- **20+ Constructs** — Weapons, utilities, and consumables to bring into runs

- **3+ Biomes** — The Shallows, The Warrens, The Depths, and more at launch
- **Outpost Base Building** — Workshop, Forge, Alchemy, and Survivor Quarters
- **Body Corruption** — The deeper you go, the more the Rot changes you
- **Tactical Carpentry** — Smash furniture, build barricades, grind for gold
