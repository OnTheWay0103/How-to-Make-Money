# Release Date, Platforms & FAQ — Everything We Know

Grain Rot is one of the most anticipated indie games of summer 2026, with nearly 200,000 Steam wishlists and over 350,000 demo downloads. Here's everything confirmed about the game's launch.

## Release Date

**August 7, 2026** — confirmed by developers Beck & Branch Games and publisher Neem Interactive.

The game will launch on Steam as a full release (not Early Access). The demo will remain available post-launch.

## Platforms

| Platform | Status |
|----------|--------|
| **PC (Steam)** | ✅ Confirmed — August 7, 2026 |
| **PS5** | ❓ Under discussion — based on player feedback and wishlist data |
| **Xbox Series X\|S** | ❓ Under discussion — same as PS5 |
| **Nintendo Switch** | ❌ Not planned |

The developers have stated they are "evaluating console ports based on launch performance and player demand." The game's controls and physics systems would need adaptation for controllers.

## Price

The exact price has not been announced, but based on similar indie co-op games:

- **Expected**: $14.99 - $19.99 USD
- The free demo gives a substantial amount of content (3 biomes, 9 enemies, 20+ constructs)
- The developers have confirmed there will be **no microtransactions** — buy once, play forever
- All post-launch content updates will be free

## Demo

A **free demo** is available now on Steam. It includes:

- 3 underground biomes (The Shallows, The Warrens, The Depths)
- 9 enemy types
- 20+ unlockable constructs, weapons, and utilities
- Upgradeable Outpost rooms
- Dungeon modifiers
- Cosmetics (hats and vessel paints)
- Full co-op support (1-4 players)

**Note**: Demo progress does **not** carry over to the full game. The demo is intended as a standalone experience to showcase the core loop.

## FAQ

### When does Grain Rot come out?
August 7, 2026 on Steam.

### Is Grain Rot on PS5 or Xbox?
Not at launch. Console versions are under discussion based on player demand.

### How much does Grain Rot cost?
The price hasn't been announced yet. Expect $14.99-$19.99 USD based on comparable indie co-op games.

### Is there a Grain Rot demo?
Yes! A free demo is available now on Steam with 3 biomes, 9 enemies, and 20+ constructs.

### Does demo progress carry over?
No. The demo is standalone. Progress resets for the full game.

### Is Grain Rot multiplayer only?
No. You can play solo, but the game is designed around 1-4 player co-op. Solo play emphasizes stealth and careful resource management.

### How many players in co-op?
Up to 4 players online.

### Is there matchmaking?
The game supports friend invites. Public matchmaking details haven't been confirmed.

### Is there voice chat?
Yes, with a twist — the Rot distorts your voice as corruption builds.

### Will there be mod support?
Not at launch. The developers have said they're "open to it" but focused on the base game first.

### How long is a typical run?
15-30 minutes for a standard extraction run. Deep runs can take 45+ minutes.

### Is there a story?
Yes. The game has environmental storytelling and lore about the First Spark, the origin of the Rot, and the cursed elevator system. There are two confirmed endings in the full game.

### Will there be post-launch content?
Yes. The developers have confirmed free content updates after launch, including new biomes, enemies, bosses, and constructs.
