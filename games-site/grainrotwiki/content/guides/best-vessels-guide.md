# Best Vessels & Constructs Guide

Your choice of **Vessel** (the wooden body your Spark inhabits) and **Constructs** (unlockable tools and weapons) defines your playstyle in Grain Rot. This guide ranks everything and recommends the best builds for solo and co-op play.

## Vessel System Explained

Vessels are the wooden bodies your Living Spark possesses. When a vessel is destroyed, your Spark escapes and can possess a new one — but you'll need to find or bring a replacement.

Vessels have three core stats:
- **Durability**: Total HP before breaking
- **Speed**: Movement and sprint speed
- **Ability**: A unique skill or passive

### Starting Vessel (Default)
- **Durability**: 3/5 | **Speed**: 3/5
- **Ability**: None (basic melee and sprint only)
- **Best for**: Learning the game. Replace ASAP.

## Best Builds

### Solo Survival Build
**Vessel**: Rot-Touched Vessel
**Constructs**: Splinter-Bow + Rot Purge Flask + Loot Magnet
**Strategy**: Stay quiet, pick off enemies from range, manage corruption carefully, extract with maximum loot.

### Co-op Tank Build
**Vessel**: Ironbark Guardian
**Constructs**: Reinforced Mallet + Barricade Kit + Healing Salve
**Strategy**: Draw enemy aggro, block corridors with barricades, let teammates deal damage from behind you.

### Speed Runner Build
**Vessel**: Whisper-Wood Scout
**Constructs**: Spark Beacon + Noise Maker + Grappling Hook
**Strategy**: Scout ahead silently, mark extraction, distract enemies away from the team, grab high-value loot and run.

### Loot Goblin Build
**Vessel**: Reinforced Hauler
**Constructs**: Loot Magnet + Barricade Kit + Fire Bomb
**Strategy**: Carry everything. Prioritize high-value items, use barricades to create safe looting windows, Fire Bomb for emergency escapes.
