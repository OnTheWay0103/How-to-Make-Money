# Co-op Guide — Multiplayer Tips & Team Strategies

Grain Rot is designed around cooperative chaos. Up to 4 Living Sparks descend together into the ruins — and while the game encourages "friendly accidents," coordinated teams survive longer and extract richer. This guide covers everything you need for effective co-op play.

## Why Co-op Matters

The underground ruins scale with player count:
- **More loot spawns** with more players
- **Tougher enemies** (more HP, higher density)
- **More complex puzzles** (some require multiple players)
- **Shared extraction** — all extract or all lose

Solo play is viable, but co-op unlocks the game's full strategic depth.

## Team Roles

In a 4-player team, assign these roles:

### 1. The Vanguard (Tank)
- **Best Vessel**: Ironbark Guardian
- **Constructs**: Reinforced Mallet, Barricade Kit, Healing Salve
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Enter rooms first to draw aggro
  - Block doorways with barricades
  - Soak damage while teammates deal DPS
  - Call out enemy positions and types

### 2. The Scout (Recon)
- **Best Vessel**: Whisper-Wood Scout
- **Constructs**: Spark Beacon, Noise Maker, Grappling Hook
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Move ahead silently to map the area
  - Mark the extraction point with Spark Beacon
  - Lure enemies away from the team with Noise Makers
  - Find hidden loot rooms and shortcuts

### 3. The Striker (DPS)
- **Best Vessel**: Ember-Kissed Vessel or Splinter-Skin Runner
- **Constructs**: Splinter-Bow, Fire Bomb, Rusty Blade
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Focus down priority targets (Screamers, Knights)
  - Deal damage from safe angles while Vanguard tanks
  - Clear Husk Drones and other swarm enemies
  - Use Fire Bombs for area denial

### 4. The Hauler (Support)
- **Best Vessel**: Reinforced Hauler
- **Constructs**: Loot Magnet, Rot Purge Flask, Healing Salve
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Carry the bulk of the team's loot
  - Distribute Rot Purge Flasks when corruption gets high
  - Heal damaged vessels with Healing Salves
  - Manage inventory — decide what to keep and what to Grind

### 3-Player Adaptation
Combine Scout + Striker into one role. The Hauler takes on additional support duties.

### 2-Player Adaptation
One player runs Vanguard + Hauler, the other runs Scout + Striker. Prioritize survival over greed.

## Communication Tips

### Voice Chat Essentials
- **Call enemy types immediately**: "Screamer, left corridor!" is faster than "There's an enemy over there"
- **Announce Rot levels**: Warn teammates when your corruption crosses 50% and 75%
- **Coordinate extraction**: "Heading to elevator, 45 seconds left" keeps everyone synchronized
- **Share loot discoveries**: "Gold vein in the southwest room" helps the Hauler prioritize

### Non-Verbal Communication
- **Ping system**: Use the in-game ping to mark enemies, loot, and threats
- **Emotes**: Cursed emotes degrade with corruption — use them to signal your corruption level
- **Dropkick a teammate**: Yes, it's trolling — but it can also knock them out of a grab attack

## Team Compositions

### Balanced Squad (Recommended for Beginners)
- 1 Ironbark Guardian, 1 Whisper-Wood Scout, 1 Ember-Kissed Vessel, 1 Reinforced Hauler
- Covers all roles, forgiving for new players

### Speed Clear (Experienced Teams)
- 2 Whisper-Wood Scouts, 1 Splinter-Skin Runner, 1 Reinforced Hauler
- Maximum mobility, minimal combat — loot fast, extract faster

### Combat Focus (Boss Runs)
- 2 Ironbark Guardians, 2 Ember-Kissed Vessels
- Maximum damage output and survivability for boss encounters

### Loot Maximization (Resource Runs)
- 4 Reinforced Haulers
- Everyone carries maximum loot, avoid combat entirely

## Shared Resources & Loot Etiquette

### Gold
Gold is shared among all surviving players after extraction. There's no need to fight over gold pickups — grab everything.

### Construction Materials
Shared to the Outpost pool. More materials = faster base progression for everyone.

### Weapons & Utilities
These are **per-player inventory**. Discuss who needs what:
- Give Splinter-Bows to the Striker and Scout
- Give Barricade Kits to the Vanguard
- Give Rot Purge Flasks to the Hauler for distribution
- Extra Healing Salves go to whoever has the lowest durability vessel

### The Grinder
Only one player needs to interact with the Grinder — the gold is shared. Let the Hauler handle Grinder operations while others provide security.

## Revival Mechanics

When a player's vessel is destroyed:
1. Their Spark escapes and becomes a floating orb
2. The Spark can slowly float toward teammates
3. A teammate must interact with the Spark to guide it to a new vessel
4. The revived player possesses the nearest available vessel

**Revival priority**: Hauler > Scout > Striker > Vanguard. The Vanguard can survive longest alone; the Hauler carries everyone's loot.

## Common Co-op Mistakes

1. **Everyone splitting up** — solo players die fast. Stay in pairs at minimum.
2. **No role assignment** — without a Vanguard, everyone takes turns getting hit
3. **Greedy extraction timing** — one player's greed wipes the entire team
4. **Ignoring corruption** — a fully corrupted teammate becomes a liability
5. **No communication on enemy types** — fighting a Screamer without focus fire is a team wipe
6. **Forgetting the elevator timer** — designate one player (usually Scout) to track time
