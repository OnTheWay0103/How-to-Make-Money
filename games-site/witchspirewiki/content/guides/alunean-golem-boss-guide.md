# Al'uunian Golem Boss Guide

> **Applies to EA v0.1.1d / Updated: June 29, 2026**

The Al'uunian Golem is the first major boss in Witchspire and your gateway to the second region, Timberline Basin. It is a stone construct awakened by the player's actions in the Eastern Ruins. This guide covers every attack pattern, the best positioning, and both solo and co-op strategies.

---

## Boss Info

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Region | Vyrs — Eastern Ruins Arena |
| Level | 8 |
| HP | Approximately 4,500 |
| Type | Construct |
| Weakness | Crystal damage (shatter effect) |
| Resist | Physical damage (reduced 30%) |
| Recommended Level | 8+ |
| Recommended Gear | Iron weapon, Fiber armor (min) |
| Recommended Hearth | Tier 3 |

### Gear Check

| Item | Recommendation | Why |
|-----|---------------|-----|
| Weapon | Iron Spellblade or Iron Wand | Stone tools deal reduced damage |
| Armor | Full Fiber set (at minimum) | Avoid one-shot from slam |
| Familiar | Level 5+ Sproutling or Pebbleback | Tank the golem's aggro |
| Potions | 5+ Minor Healing Potions | You will get hit |

### Luminary Nodes

These nodes help significantly:
- **Quick Recovery** (2 points) — healing potions restore 50% more
- **Extra Familiar Slot** (3 points) — two familiars means double tanking
- **Tough Hide** (2 points) — 10% damage reduction

## Attack Patterns

The Al'uunian Golem has 5 attacks. Learn the telegraphs:

### 1. Ground Slam (AoE)
- **Telegraph**: Golem raises both arms for 2 seconds, ground glows orange
- **Damage**: High (one-shot potential without armor)
- **Range**: Medium (4m radius around golem)
- **Counter**: Double dodge roll away. Do not block — it breaks guard
- **Safe spot**: Behind the golem (it only slams forward)

### 2. Rock Throw (Ranged)
- **Telegraph**: Golem reaches into its chest, pulls out a boulder
- **Damage**: Medium-high
- **Range**: Long (across entire arena)
- **Counter**: Sidestep when the boulder is thrown. Do not run backward
- **Safe spot**: Stay close — the boulder cannot hit you at point-blank

### 3. Rock Armor (Buff)
- **Telegraph**: Golem crouches, stone skin glows
- **Effect**: +50% damage reduction for 15 seconds
- **Counter**: Stop attacking and focus on dodging. Wait it out
- **Note**: This is a damage check — if your gear is too low, you will time out

### 4. Stomp Combo (Melee)
- **Telegraph**: Golem stomps 3 times in a row, each stomp creates a shockwave
- **Damage**: Medium per hit
- **Counter**: Dodge the first shockwave, then run in a circle around the golem
- **Safe spot**: Behind the golem after the first stomp

### 5. Slam + Rock Shower (Enrage — below 20% HP)
- **Telegraph**: Golem roars (audible cue), slams both arms, sends rock fragments across the arena
- **Damage**: High (multi-hit)
- **Counter**: Broom flight — get airborne immediately when you hear the roar
- **Safe spot**: None on ground. Must fly.

## Solo Strategy

### Spellblade (Recommended: Cloudpiercer)
1. Let your familiar engage first to draw aggro
2. Circle-strafe behind the golem
3. Use light x3 → heavy combo for max damage (beam hits weak points)
4. Dodge backward when you see the arm raise (Ground Slam telegraph)
5. Save mana for emergency beam attacks during Rock Armor phase
6. At 20% HP -> mount broom and stay airborne until enrage ends

### Wand (Recommended: Wildroot or Tomekeeper)
1. Keep maximum range at all times
2. Use your familiar to tank
3. Cast and move — never stand still
4. Nature Wand: maintain heal-over-time on yourself
5. Nox Wand: stack poison, refresh before it expires
6. When golem turns to you, sprint to the opposite side of the arena
7. At 20% HP -> same as above, mount broom and kite from air

## Co-op Strategy (2-4 Players)

| Role | Player Count | Responsibilities |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| Tank | 1 (Wildroot / Cloudpiercer) | Face golem, hold aggro, dodge slams |
| DPS | 1-2 (Any) | Stay behind golem, maximize damage |
| Healer | 0-1 (Wildroot) | Maintain heals on tank, cleanse debuffs |

### Positioning
- Tank holds golem at arena center, facing away from group
- DPS and healer stay at golem's back/sides
- If golem turns, DPS rotate to new back position
- When Rock Armor activates, all players stop attacking and spread out

### Co-op Tips
- Designate one player to call out telegraphs — it helps the team react
- If you have 2+ Wands, stagger your spells so mana regen is constant
- Drop a Hearth flag in the arena before starting (respawn point)
- If someone dies, the tank must hold aggro while they run back

## Post-Boss Checklist

After defeating Al'uunian Golem:
1. Collect Golem Core from the arena floor
2. Return to Hearth and interact with the crystal
3. Equip the Region Unlock Token in your inventory
4. Head to Vyrs South Passage
5. Open the gate to Timberline Basin
