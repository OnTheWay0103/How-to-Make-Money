# Potions Guide — Brewing, Effects, and Ingredient Sourcing

> **Applies to EA v0.1.1d / Updated: July 23, 2026**

Potions and consumables are essential for surviving Witchspire's increasingly dangerous regions. While basic healing can be managed with food and rest, advanced zones like Timberline Basin and the Shuddersands demand proper alchemical support. This guide covers every brewable potion, where to find ingredients, and which potions matter at each stage of the game.

---

## Brewing Station: The Witchcraft Circle

Potions are crafted at the **Witchcraft Circle**, unlocked at **Hearth Tier 4**. The Circle has three crafting modes:

| Mode | Function | Unlock |
|------|----------|--------|
| **Brewing** | Standard potions | Hearth 4 |
| **Infusion** | Enchanted materials (Enchanted Fiber, Enchanted Steel) | Hearth 4 |
| **Advanced Alchemy** | Endgame elixirs and revival tonics | Hearth 5 |

Each potion requires **Nectar** as a base solvent, plus plant reagents grown in your **Garden Plot** or foraged in the wild.

### Garden Plot Requirements

Most potion ingredients must be grown or foraged. The Garden Plot (Hearth Tier 2) is your primary source:

| Plant | Seed Location | Growth Time | Primary Use |
|-------|---------------|-------------|-------------|
| Sunbloom | Vyrs vendors | 10 min | Healing potions |
| Thornberry | Vyrs bushes (forage) | 10 min | Damage/reflect potions |
| Moonleaf | Timberline Basin | 10 min | Mana potions |
| Crystal Bud | Timberline Basin caves | 10 min | Defense buffs |

**Tip:** Plant a rotating harvest of 3-4 Sunbloom plots and 1-2 of each other plant. Healing potions are consumed fastest and their ingredients are always in demand.

## Complete Potion Catalog

### Healing Potions

| Potion | Recipe | Effect | Crafting Time |
|--------|--------|--------|---------------|
| Minor Healing Potion | 5 Nectar, 2 Sunbloom | Restore 100 HP | 10s |
| Major Healing Potion | 5 Nectar, 4 Sunbloom, 1 Moonleaf | Restore 300 HP | 15s |
| Greater Healing Elixir | 5 Nectar, 6 Sunbloom, 2 Moonleaf, 1 Ethereal Dust | Restore 500 HP over 5s | 30s |
| Regeneration Tonic | 5 Nectar, 4 Sunbloom, 2 Crystal Bud, 1 Astral Essence | Restore 3 HP/sec for 60s | 45s |

**Strategy:** Minor Healing Potions are cost-effective for general exploration. Major potions are for boss fights. Greater Healing Elixirs should be reserved for the Shuddersands and late-game expeditions.

### Mana Potions

| Potion | Recipe | Effect | Crafting Time |
|--------|--------|--------|---------------|
| Minor Mana Potion | 3 Nectar, 2 Moonleaf | Restore 50 Mana | 10s |

| Arcane Infusion | 5 Nectar, 4 Moonleaf, 2 Crystal Bud, 1 Ethereal Dust | Restore 250 Mana + 20% spell damage for 30s | 30s |

**Strategy:** Spellblade and Wand users should carry 3-4 Minor Mana Potions per expedition. Arcane Infusion is particularly effective before boss engagements — time it so the damage buff overlaps with your burst window.

|--------|--------|--------|----------|

## Ingredient Sourcing Strategy

### Early Game (Vyrs / Hearth 1-3)

| Ingredient | How to Get | Priority |
|------------|------------|----------|
| Nectar | Garden Plot (Sunbloom harvest), foraging flowers | High — base for all potions |
| Sunbloom | Buy seeds from Vyrs vendors, grow in plots | High — healing staple |
| Thornberry | Forage from bushes around Vyrs | Medium — combat prep |
| Crystal Shards | Timberline Basin Crystal Caverns | Low — not yet accessible |

**Goal:** Stockpile 50+ Nectar and 30+ Sunbloom before moving to Timberline Basin.

### Mid Game (Timberline Basin / Hearth 3-4)

| Ingredient | How to Get | Priority |
|------------|------------|----------|
| Moonleaf | Forage in Timberline Basin forest, grow in plots | High — mana potions |
| Crystal Bud | Timberline Basin caves, grow in plots | High — defense potions |
| Coal | Timberline Basin dark stone deposits | Medium — Emberbrew |
| Iron Ore | Timberline Basin mines | Low — crafting, not potions |

**Goal:** Unlock the Witchcraft Circle and mass-produce Major Healing and Major Mana Potions.

### Late Game (Mira Isles / Shuddersands / Hearth 5)

| Ingredient | How to Get | Priority |
|------------|------------|----------|
| Ethereal Dust | Ethereal enemies (Timberline Basin), special events | High — advanced alchemy |
| Astral Essence | Night events (Shuddersands), legendary familiar drops | High — endgame elixirs |
| Thornberry (bulk) | Multiple garden plots | Medium — Berserker Draught |
| Crystal Shards (bulk) | Timberline Basin Crystal Caverns farming runs | Medium — waterwalking/nightvision |

**Goal:** Produce Greater Healing Elixirs, Arcane Infusions, and Familiar Revival Tonics for endgame expeditions.

## Brewing Efficiency Tips

1. **Batch craft before expeditions.** The Witchcraft Circle has no bulk craft option — craft each potion individually. Set aside 10 minutes at base to produce a full inventory before heading out.

2. **Prioritize garden diversity over volume.** Three different plants producing simultaneously gives you more recipe flexibility than six plots of Sunbloom. A balanced garden supports every potion type.

3. **Moonleaf is the bottleneck.** Sunbloom grows from purchased seeds, but Moonleaf seeds must be foraged in Timberline Basin. Each foraging run should prioritize Moonleaf nodes above all else.

4. **Save Ethereal Dust for emergency tonics.** Ethereal Dust is rare until late game. Don't waste it on Greater Healing Elixirs when Major Healing Potions suffice — save it for Familiar Revival Tonics and Arcane Infusions.

5. **Use Lootsense Extract on farming runs.** Craft one Lootsense Extract before dedicated ingredient-gathering expeditions. The visibility range boost dramatically reduces search time.
