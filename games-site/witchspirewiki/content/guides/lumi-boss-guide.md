# Lumi Boss Guide — Witchspire Tower Final Encounter

> **Applies to EA v0.1.1d / Updated: July 11, 2026**

Lumi, the Tower Guardian, is the final boss of Witchspire's current Early Access content. This sentient crystalline entity guards the top of Witchspire Tower, the central structure that appears after defeating the Shudder Matriarch. The fight has three distinct phases, each demanding different positioning and tactics. This guide covers everything you need to secure your first clear.

---

## Boss Info

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Location | Witchspire Tower — Summit Chamber |
| Level | 32 |
| HP | Approximately 28,000 (across all phases) |
| Type | Ethereal (Crystal / Light) |
| Weakness | Nox damage (+30%) — corrupts the crystal form |
| Resist | Physical damage (reduced 50%), Lux damage (reduced 30%) |
| Recommended Level | 30+ |
| Recommended Gear | Matriarch Silk armor or equivalent, Steel+ weapon with Nox enchant |
| Recommended Hearth | Tier 6 (maximum in current EA) |
| Recommended Luminary | 40+ points allocated |

### Accessing the Tower

1. Defeat the **Shudder Matriarch** in the Sunken Arena
2. Collect the **Matriarch Core**
3. **Witchspire Tower** rises from the center of the Shuddersands map
4. Enter the tower via the ground-floor entrance
5. Climb 5 floors of enemies (Lv 30-32 — bring potions)
6. The Summit Chamber door is at the top — enter to trigger the Lumi fight

## Phase 1 — Crystal Sentry (100% — 65% HP)

Lumi begins as a floating crystal entity in the center of a circular chamber. The room has 4 pillars for cover.

### Attack Patterns (Phase 1)

| Attack | Telegraph | Damage | Counter |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Crystal Lance** (Ranged beam) | Lumi's core glows, fires a thin beam | High (piercing) | Hide behind a pillar or block with shield |
| **Shard Volley** (Aimed projectiles) | Lumi spins, fires 8 crystal shards | Medium per shard | Dodge roll through the gaps between shards |
| **Crystal Push** (AoE knockback) | Lumi pulses white, ring expands | Low + knockback | Jump over the ring or brace against a pillar |
| **Summon Fragments** (Adds) | Lumi splits off 3 crystal fragments | Low (fragments explode on death) | Kill fragments before they reach Lumi (they heal the boss on contact) |
| **Glare** (Blind) | Lumi flashes bright | Blind (5s) | Turn your camera away from Lumi when you see the flash charge |

### Phase 1 Strategy

**All players:**
- Use the 4 pillars as cover — always have one between you and Lumi
- Kill Crystal Fragments immediately — they heal Lumi for ~500 HP each if they reach the boss
- When Lumi casts Glare, turn your camera 180 degrees (look at the wall) to avoid the blind
- Attack windows: after Shard Volley (dodge then attack) and after Crystal Push (attack during cooldown)

**Solo:**
- Keep one familiar on aggressive (attack Lumi) and one on defensive (kill adds)
- Circle strafe around the room, using pillars as cover between attack windows
- Do not chase Lumi when it teleports — wait for it to re-engage

**Co-op:**
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Primary DPS | Focus on Lumi, time attacks to Shard Volley cooldown |
| Add Clear | Dedicated player kills Crystal Fragments before they reach Lumi |
| Pillar Keeper | Ensure someone is always behind a pillar when Crystal Lance charges |

## Phase 2 — The Gauntlet (65% — 35% HP)

Lumi shatters the floor, revealing a vertical shaft. Both Lumi and the player must now fight while falling / flying downward through the tower's central core. This is a **broom flight mandatory** phase.

### Phase 2 Mechanics

| Mechanic | Description | Counter |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Descending Arena** | You and Lumi fall through a 200m vertical shaft | Stay on broom, control descent speed |
| **Crystal Walls** | Walls of the shaft have crystal outcroppings | Dodge or destroy to clear path |
| **Beam Barrage** | Lumi fires beams while falling | Weave between the beams |
| **Floor Platforms** | Small platforms every 30m for brief rests | Land to regenerate stamina briefly |
| **Wind Gusts** | Random gusts push you sideways | Counter-steer (lean into the gust) |

### Phase 2 Strategy

**Solo:**
- **Control your descent** — do not hold the descend key continuously. Tap it to maintain height
- Focus on dodging beams rather than dealing damage — survival comes first
- Use platforms to briefly land and refresh potion buffs (you cannot drink while flying)
- Aerial Combat Luminary node (+20% damage while flying) is critical here
- Crystal Walls that block your path: destroy them with a ranged attack (1-2 hits)

**Co-op:**
- Call out "beam incoming" for vertical beams (dodge left/right)
- Call out "gust" for wind gusts (counter-steer in the opposite direction)
- DPS players focus Lumi; the support player clears crystal walls ahead of the group
- If a player gets knocked off their broom, another player can fly close and press `F` to "catch" them (grants brief hover)

## Phase 3 — Lumi's True Form (35% — 0% HP)

At the shaft's bottom, Lumi reforms into its true shape: a massive crystal humanoid with visible cracked core. The arena is now a flat stone circle. All previous abilities return, plus devastating new ones.

### New Attacks (Phase 3)

| Attack | Telegraph | Damage | Counter |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Crystal Prison** (Trap) | Ground crystals rise around a target | Locks target in place (5s), ticking damage | Other players must destroy the crystals (3 hits each) |
| **Shatter Wave** (Room-wide AoE) | Lumi's core flashes rapidly, cracks spread | Very High (lethal without evasion) | Jump + dodge roll at the moment the wave reaches you (timing window: 0.5s) |
| **Core Beam** (Channeled beam sweep) | Lumi's core opens, charges for 2s, then sweeps beam across arena | Instant kill (any hit) | Mount broom and fly behind Lumi — the beam cannot turn 180 degrees |
| **Desperation Cascade** (Below 10% HP) | Lumi's core explodes outward in 3 rings | Lethal | Time three consecutive jumps to clear each ring |

### Phase 3 Strategy

**Solo:**
1. Stay close to Lumi (mid-range) — the Shatter Wave is hardest to dodge at long range
2. When Crystal Prison traps you, immediately switch to your second familiar's active ability (many can break crystals) or rapidly spam dodge
3. Core Beam: the instant you see the core open, mount broom and fly to Lumi's back. The beam sweeps forward; you are safe behind Lumi
4. Below 30% HP: pop all remaining consumables and damage buffs
5. Desperation Cascade (10% HP): three jumps with 0.8s timing. Watch the ring visuals — jump when the white flash reaches the ring edge

**Co-op:**
| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Break Team | 2 players dedicated to breaking Crystal Prison crystals on trapped teammates |
| Bait | 1 player flies behind Lumi during Core Beam to bait the beam direction |
| Burst DPS | All players unload cooldowns below 20% HP |
| Caller | One player calls Shatter Wave timing for the team |

**Critical co-op note**: When a player is trapped in Crystal Prison, the other players have ~4 seconds to break them out or that player takes massive damage. Assign two players to handle this — one may not be enough.

## Post-Boss Content (Current EA)

After defeating Lumi:
1. Collect **Lumi's Core** — this is the current EA endpoint
2. A credits sequence plays (approx 3 min)
3. Your save is marked as **"Cleared — awaiting Chapter 2"**
4. You can continue playing: free-roam all regions, farm materials, capture all familiars, complete side quests
5. Future updates will add the next chapter (Shuddersands Gate, new regions, new bosses)
