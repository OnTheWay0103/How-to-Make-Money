# How to Unlock Flight in Witchspire

> **Applies to EA v0.1.1d / Updated: June 29, 2026**

Flight opens up Witchspire's world. Without a broom, you are stuck on the ground dodging enemies between every resource node. Here is exactly how to unlock it, what materials you need, and where to find every Flight Pillar.

---

## Step 1: Upgrade Hearth to Tier 2

Flight requires a Broom. Brooms are crafted at the Workbench, unlocked at Hearth Tier 1. However, the materials require Hearth Tier 2.

**Hearth Tier 2 requirements:**
- 30 Wood
- 25 Stone
- 10 Fiber

Build the Hearth upgrades from the Hearth menu (interact with the central crystal). Once Tier 2 is active, you unlock the Furnace and higher-tier Workbench recipes, including Brooms.

See [Hearth Upgrade Guide](/guides/hearth-upgrade-guide) for full upgrade trees.

---

## Step 2: Gather Broom Materials

### Basic Broom Recipe

| Material | Amount | Source |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Refined Wood | 8 | Furnace: 2 Wood → 1 Refined Wood |
| Iron Ingot | 4 | Furnace: 3 Iron Ore + 1 Coal → 1 Ingot |
| Sturdy Fiber | 6 | Furnace: 3 Fiber → 1 Sturdy Fiber |
| Nectar | 3 | Yellow flowers in Vyrs fields |

### Where to Find Iron Ore

Iron ore appears in several places in Vyrs:

| Location | Yield | Danger Level |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Vyrs South Cave | 3-5 per node | Low (level 3-4 enemies) |
| Vyrs East Ridge | 2-3 per node | Medium (level 5-6 enemies) |
| Timberline Basin | 4-6 per node | High (level 10+ enemies) |

Stick to Vyrs South Cave for your first batch.

---

## Step 3: Craft Your Broom

1. Open your Workbench
2. Navigate to the "Tools" tab
3. Find "Basic Broom" in the list
4. Craft (takes ~30 seconds)

**Congratulations — you can now fly.**

## Flight Controls

| Action | PC Control | Controller |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| Mount / Dismount | F | Y |
| Ascend | Space | A |
| Descend | Left Shift | Left Bumper |
| Forward | W / Forward on stick | Left stick forward |
| Boost (sprint) | Left Ctrl | Click left stick |
| Brake | S | Back on left stick |

Your broom has a stamina bar. When it runs out, you slowly descend. Land on any solid ground to regenerate stamina.

## Region-by-Region Flying Tips

### Vyrs
Open fields and low obstacles. Use boost generously. Flight Pillars are close together.

### Timberline Basin
Dense trees and elevation changes. Fly above the canopy line to avoid obstacles. The Basin Summit pillar is essential for navigating this zone.

### Mira Isles
Water landings are safe — your broom hovers above water. Use flight to reach underwater cave entrances from above.

### Shuddersands
Sandstorms reduce visibility while flying. Stay low and follow the Flight Pillar paths. Boost through storm patches to minimize stamina drain.
