# Witchspire Demo & Purchase Guide — Price, Platforms, and System Requirements

> **Applies to EA v0.1.1d / Updated: July 11, 2026**

Witchspire entered Steam Early Access on June 10, 2026. Whether you are considering the free demo or deciding which edition to buy, this guide covers everything from price tiers to console availability to minimum PC specs.

---

## Is There a Witchspire Demo?

**Yes.** A free Witchspire demo was released during Steam Next Fest (February 2026) and remains available on the Steam store page as of July 2026.

To download it:

1. Visit the [Witchspire Steam page](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2679100/Witchspire)
2. Click **"Download Demo"** below the purchase options
3. Install and play — no purchase required

The demo has no time limit. You can play as long as you like within its content boundaries.

---

## Demo Content Scope

The demo offers a slice of the Early Access experience:

| Feature | Demo | Full EA Game |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Playable Region | Vyrs (starting area) | Vyrs + Timberline Basin + Mira Isles + Shuddersands |
| Level Cap | 7 | No cap (current EA max ~30) |
| Playable Covens | Cloudpiercers, Foretellers | All 6 Covens |
| Familiars | 2 starters (Sproutling, Emberpuff) | 25+ familiars |
| Building | Basic cabin + furnishings | Full base building, automation |
| Farming | Not available | Full farming system |
| Hearth Upgrades | Tier 1 only | Up to Tier 5 |
| Co-op | Up to 4 players | Up to 4 players |
| Crafting | Basic tools + workbench | All stations and recipes |

**What you can do in the demo:**
- Explore the Vyrs region freely
- Fight the Al'uunian Golem (first boss)
- Catch and level your first familiar
- Build a starter cabin with astral projection building mode
- Play solo or co-op with up to 4 players
- Encounter Peb, a mysterious NPC

**What is NOT in the demo:**
- Farming and automation systems (added in Early Access)
- Full Luminary skill tree
- Most familiars and crafting recipes
- Higher-tier Hearth upgrades

## Does Demo Progress Carry Over?

**Demo saves do NOT transfer to the Early Access game.** When you purchase the full game, you will start a new character from scratch.

This is standard for Steam Next Fest demos. The demo build is a separate app ID on Steam, and save data is not shared between the two.

**What you keep:** Knowledge of the early game. Use the demo to decide if the gameplay loop clicks for you, then start fresh with a clear plan.

## Price and Versions

There are three purchase options on Steam:

### Standard Edition — $22.99

The game itself. Includes the full Early Access build and all future EA updates. The full 1.0 release is included at no extra cost.

- Base price: **$22.99 / ~85.56 RMB**
- Launch discount was 15% (ended June 18, 2026)
- Price may increase when the game leaves Early Access

### Founder's Edition DLC — $14.99

An add-on content pack (does NOT include the base game). Contains:

- **Digital Art Book** — concept art and behind-the-scenes visuals
- **Digital Soundtrack** — full official OST
- **Royal Decoration Pack** — throne, banners, carpets, royal-themed bed
- **Plushie Crafting Pack** — craftable plushie decorations
- **Mystic Style Pack** — unique hat and broom cosmetic skins
- **Familiar Accessory Pack** — bow ties, headbands, mini hats for your pets
- **Early Bird Cosmetic Pack** — exclusive EA-only decorations (ornate lamps, Flamewasp Dragon rug, wooden sofa, cushions)

Founder's Edition content unlocks when the game reaches 1.0.

### Founder's Pack Bundle — $37.98

Standard Edition + Founder's Edition DLC in one discounted bundle.

- Bundle price: **$37.98 / ~144.56 RMB**
- Saves roughly $5 compared to buying separately

### Which Edition Should You Buy?

| If you... | Buy |

| Love the art style and want extras | Founder's Pack Bundle ($37.98) |

## System Requirements

### Minimum (Low settings, 1080p with DLSS/FSR Performance)

| Component | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| OS | Windows 11 (64-bit) |
| CPU | Intel Core i5-10400 / AMD Ryzen 5 5600G |
| RAM | 16 GB |
| GPU | NVIDIA GTX 1070 or AMD Radeon RX 6600S |
| Storage | 12 GB (SSD strongly recommended) |
| DirectX | Version 12 |
| Network | Broadband internet required |

### Recommended (High settings, 1080p 60+ FPS)

| Component | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| OS | Windows 11 (64-bit) |
| CPU | Intel Core i7-10700K / AMD Ryzen 7 3700X |
| RAM | 16 GB |
| GPU | NVIDIA RTX 3070 / RTX 3080 Ti or AMD Radeon RX 6800 XT |
| Storage | 12 GB (SSD strongly recommended) |
| DirectX | Version 12 |
| Network | Broadband internet required |

### Notes
- The game requires a 64-bit processor and Windows 11 only
- An SSD is strongly recommended even for minimum spec
- Hosting multiplayer sessions with multiple players may impact performance
- DLSS and FSR are supported for lower-end hardware

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I play the demo after Early Access launched?**
Yes, the demo is still available on the Steam store page as of July 2026.

**Q: Will Witchspire come to PS5 or Switch?**
Envar Games has not announced console versions. Currently the game is PC-only via Steam.

**Q: Does the demo have co-op?**
Yes. Up to 4 players can play the demo together.

**Q: Will the price go up?**
The developer has stated the price may increase when the game leaves Early Access for full 1.0 release.

**Q: Do I need a powerful PC?**
The minimum spec targets an NVIDIA GTX 1070 or equivalent, with 16 GB of RAM. The game is moderately demanding for its art style due to the open world and real-time lighting.
