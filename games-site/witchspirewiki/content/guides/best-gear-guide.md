# Best Gear Guide — Brooms, Wands, and Equipment Tier List

> **Applies to EA v0.1.1d / Updated: July 18, 2026**

Gear in Witchspire determines your combat effectiveness, traversal speed, and resource gathering efficiency. This guide ranks every weapon and broom by stats, explains gear progression by game stage, and recommends optimal loadouts for each Coven.

---

## Broom Tier List

Brooms are your primary traversal tool. Higher-tier brooms fly faster, climb higher, and have better stamina efficiency.

### All Brooms Compared

| Broom | Speed | Stamina Use | Altitude | Materials | Hearth Tier |
|-------|-------|-------------|----------|-----------|-------------|
| Basic Broom | 100% | 3/s | Low | 8 Refined Wood, 4 Iron Ingot, 6 Sturdy Fiber, 3 Nectar | 2 |
| Swift Broom | 130% | 3/s | Low | 10 Refined Wood, 6 Iron Ingot, 6 Sturdy Fiber, 5 Nectar | 3 |
| Ember Broom | 140% | 4/s | Medium | 8 Steel Ingot, 4 Sturdy Planks, 2 Crystal Shards, 5 Nectar | 4 |
| Frost Broom | 120% | 2/s | High | 8 Steel Ingot, 4 Sturdy Planks, 2 Moonleaf, 5 Nectar | 4 |
| Storm Broom | 160% | 5/s | Medium | 10 Enchanted Steel, 6 Ethereal Dust, 3 Crystal Shards | 5 |
| Phantom Broom | 150% | 2/s | Very High | 10 Enchanted Steel, 8 Ethereal Dust, 5 Astral Essence | 5 |

### Broom Rankings

**S-Tier: Phantom Broom**
Best overall broom. Excellent speed combined with the lowest stamina drain and highest altitude cap. Ideal for exploration and escaping combat. The Astral Essence requirement makes it an end-game grind, but it is worth every minute.

**A-Tier: Storm Broom**
Fastest broom in the game at 160% speed. Drains stamina quickly, so pair it with stamina food or the +Stamina Luminary node. Best for speedrunning between known locations.

**B-Tier: Frost Broom**
Slower than Ember, but stamina-efficient and reaches high altitude. Excellent for navigating vertical zones like Timberline Basin cliffs. The Moonleaf material cost is reasonable.

**C-Tier: Ember Broom**
Good all-rounder available at Hearth Tier 4. Higher stamina cost than Frost but faster speed. Use if you already have Crystal Shards stockpiled.

**D-Tier: Swift Broom and Basic Broom**
Swift Broom is your first upgrade from Basic and serves well through mid-game. Skip Basic Broom entirely if you can — farm the extra resources for Swift.

## Wand Tier List

Wands determine your ranged magic damage and spell effectiveness. Each wand has a base damage stat and an element affinity.

### All Wands Compared

| Wand | Magic Damage | Element | Attack Speed | Materials | Hearth Tier |
|-----|--------------|---------|--------------|-----------|-------------|
| Stone Wand | 8-12 | None | 1.0/s | 6 Wood, 4 Stone | 1 |
| Iron Wand | 15-20 | None | 1.0/s | 4 Iron Ingots, 3 Refined Wood | 3 |
| Crystal Wand | 22-30 | Ice | 0.8/s | 6 Crystal Shards, 4 Refined Wood, 2 Iron Ingots | 4 |
| Ember Wand | 25-35 | Fire | 0.9/s | 8 Steel Ingots, 4 Sturdy Planks, 3 Ember Shards | 4 |
| Storm Wand | 30-40 | Lightning | 0.7/s | 6 Enchanted Steel, 4 Ethereal Dust, 2 Crystal Shards | 5 |
| Moonlit Wand | 35-45 | Nature | 0.8/s | 8 Enchanted Steel, 6 Moonleaf, 4 Astral Essence | 5 |
| Shadow Wand | 40-55 | Umbra | 0.6/s | 10 Enchanted Steel, 8 Ethereal Dust, 6 Dark Essence | 5 |

### Wand Rankings

**S-Tier: Shadow Wand**
Highest damage output in the game. The Umbra element deals bonus damage to most end-game enemies in Shuddersands and Timberline Basin. Slow attack speed is offset by high per-hit damage — ideal for the Nightscribe and Foreteller Coven builds.

**A-Tier: Moonlit Wand**
Excellent Nature-element damage with a reliable attack speed. The healing passive (3% HP per 5 hits) makes it invaluable for solo play. Best wand for Wildroot Coven.

**B-Tier: Storm Wand**
High Lightning-element damage with good material accessibility. Lightning damage chains to nearby enemies in groups. Worse than Moonlit for single-target fights, but excellent for crowd control.

**B-Tier: Ember Wand**
Fire-element wand available at Hearth Tier 4. Fire damage applies a burn DoT (8 damage per tick, 4 ticks). Good all-around choice for Cloudpiercer Coven players at mid-game.

**C-Tier: Crystal Wand**
Ice-element with slow attack speed. The freeze proc is useful but inconsistent. Use only if you have excess Crystal Shards from Timberline Basin farming runs.

**D-Tier: Iron and Stone Wands**
Stone Wand is your starting weapon — replace it as soon as possible. Iron Wand is serviceable through early Shuddersands content but falls off hard at Hearth Tier 4.

## Gear Progression by Game Stage

### Early Game (Hearth Tier 1-2, Vyrs Region)

| Slot | Item | How to Obtain |
|------|------|---------------|
| Wand | Stone Wand → Iron Wand | Craft at Workbench |
| Broom | Basic Broom | Craft after Hearth Tier 2 |
| Armor | Fiber Set (full) | Craft at Workbench |
| Accessory | Nectar Pendant (quest) | Vyrs starter quest chain |

**Focus**: Iron Wand + Full Fiber Armor. Do not waste resources on leather armor — save Iron Ingots for your broom unlock.

### Mid Game (Hearth Tier 3-4, Timberline Basin)

| Slot | Item | How to Obtain |
|------|------|---------------|
| Wand | Ember Wand or Crystal Wand | Timberline Basin materials |
| Broom | Swift Broom or Ember Broom | Craft at Advanced Workbench |
| Armor | Iron Set (Helm + Chest + Greaves) | Craft at Workbench |
| Accessory | Crystal Ring (+15% magic damage) | Timberline Basin treasure chests |

**Focus**: Ember Wand + Swift Broom + Iron Armor. Farm Crystal Shards for your next wand upgrade.

### Late Game (Hearth Tier 5+, Shuddersands + Mira Isles)

| Slot | Item | How to Obtain |
|------|------|---------------|
| Wand | Shadow Wand or Moonlit Wand | Advanced materials from end-game zones |
| Broom | Phantom Broom or Storm Broom | Craft at Enchantment Table |
| Armor | Enchanted Steel Set | Craft with Ethereal Dust |
| Accessory | Astral Amulet (+30% all damage, legendary) | Shuddersands world boss drop |

**Focus**: Shadow Wand + Phantom Broom + Enchanted Steel Armor. This is the current end-game loadout.

## Recommended Gear by Coven

### Cloudpiercer (Spellblade + Lux)
- **Best weapon**: Ember Spellblade (mid) → Soul Reaver (late)
- **Best broom**: Ember Broom (mid) → Storm Broom (late)
- **Playstyle**: Aggressive melee. Prioritize spellblade upgrades over wand upgrades.

### Wildroot (Wand + Nature)
- **Best weapon**: Crystal Wand (mid) → Moonlit Wand (late)
- **Best broom**: Frost Broom (mid) → Phantom Broom (late)
- **Playstyle**: Kiting and sustain. Pair Moonlit Wand with the familiar healing skill.

### Nightscribe (Wand + Umbra)
- **Best weapon**: Ember Wand (mid) → Shadow Wand (late)
- **Best broom**: Ember Broom (mid) → Phantom Broom (late)
- **Playstyle**: Glass cannon burst. Maximize magic damage with accessories and Luminary nodes.

### Foreteller (Wand + Ice)
- **Best weapon**: Crystal Wand (mid) → Shadow Wand (late)
- **Best broom**: Frost Broom (mid) → Phantom Broom (late)
- **Playstyle**: Crowd control. Ice element pairs with Frost Broom's stamina efficiency for long kiting sessions.
