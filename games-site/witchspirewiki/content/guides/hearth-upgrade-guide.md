# Hearth Upgrade Guide — All Tiers and Materials

> **Applies to EA v0.1.1d / Updated: June 29, 2026**

Your Hearth is the most important structure in Witchspire. Every upgrade tier unlocks new crafting stations, gameplay systems, and progression gates. If you feel stuck, the answer is almost always "upgrade your Hearth."

---

## Hearth Overview

The Hearth is your home base. Interact with the central crystal to:
- View upgrade requirements
- Allocate Luminary skill points
- Access Astral Projection mode (Tier 3+)
- Set your respawn point

**You can relocate your Hearth** by crafting a new Hearth Crystal at the Workbench. Place it anywhere on valid ground, then activate it.

| Tier | Materials Required | Features Unlocked |
|------|-------------------|-------------------|
| 1 (Default) | None | Basic Hearth, respawn point |

| 3 | 40 Refined Wood, 30 Iron Ingots, 15 Sturdy Fiber | Furnace, Advanced Workbench, Flight Pillar link |
| 4 | 60 Sturdy Planks, 40 Steel Ingots, 20 Enchanted Fiber | Witchcraft Circle, Astral Projection, Familiar Stable |
| 5 | 80 Steel Ingots, 10 Crystal Shards, 5 Ethereal Dust | Enchantment Table, Advanced Garden, Teleport Network |
| 6 | 100 Enchanted Steel, 20 Crystal Shards, 10 Astral Essence | Endgame crafting, Legendary familiar summoning |

## Astral Projection Mode

Unlocked at **Hearth Tier 4**. Enter it by interacting with your Hearth crystal and selecting "Astral Project."

**What Astral Projection does:**
- Separates your spirit from your body
- Free-flight building mode — place and move structures from the air
- No fall damage
- Preview structures before committing materials

**Use it to:**
- Build multi-level bases
- Place garden plots on elevated terrain
- Arrange furniture precisely
- Scout surrounding area safely

## Base Location Suggestions

| Location | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| Vyrs Meadows (Starting area) | Central, flat, close to resources | Gets boring visually |
| Vyrs East Ridge | High ground, good view | Longer trek to early resources |
| Timberline Basin (Central Grove) | Beautiful, abundant materials | Tougher enemies nearby |
| Timberline Basin (Cliffside) | Defensible, looks epic | Windy terrain, harder building |
| Mira Isles (Any island) | Endgame resources, unique vibe | Requires flight to reach easily |

**Our recommendation for first playthrough:** Build in **Vyrs Meadows** for Tiers 1-3, then relocate to **Timberline Basin Central Grove** for the rest of the game. The early access convenience of Vyrs is worth more than aesthetics.

2. **Tier 3 → Before fighting Al'uunian Golem**

4. **Tier 5 → Before Lumi boss fight**
