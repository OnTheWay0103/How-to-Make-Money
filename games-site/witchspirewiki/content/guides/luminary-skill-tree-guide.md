# Luminary Skill Tree Guide — Best Builds

> **Applies to EA v0.1.1d / Updated: June 29, 2026**

The Luminary skill tree governs your passive abilities, familiar slots, and stat boosts. **You cannot reset your points.** Every point is permanent. This guide tells you exactly where to invest for every playstyle.

---

## Critical Warning: No Respec

There is no respec option in the current build. It may be added in a future patch, but as of v0.1.1d, every point you spend is locked.

**What this means:**
- Do not spend points immediately — read the full tree first
- Do not spread points evenly — specialize
- Save at least 2-3 points for key mid-game nodes you will unlock later

---

## Skill Tree Structure

The Luminary tree has 4 branches:

| Branch | Focus | Best For |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Covenant** | Core passive, familiar capacity | Every build (rush this first) |
| **Vitality** | HP, defense, regeneration | Melee / solo builds |
| **Essence** | Mana, cooldown reduction, spell power | Caster builds |
| **Familiar** | Pet stats, extra slots, evolution | Familiar-heavy builds |

Each branch has minor nodes (+2% stats) and major nodes (unique abilities).

## Recommended Builds

### Universal Starter Build (Any Coven — 10 Points)

Spend your first 10 points in this order:

1. **Extra Familiar Slot** (Covenant, 3 points)
2. **Quick Recovery** (Vitality, 2 points)
3. **Familiar Bond** (Familiar, 2 points)
4. **Arcane Flow** (Essence, 2 points)
5. **Efficient Crafting** (Covenant, 2 points)

This gives you 2 familiars, +50% healing, +20% familiar damage, -15% cooldowns, and -10% crafting costs. It works on every Coven.

### Spellblade Build (Cloudpiercers / Starsmiths / Foretellers)

After the starter build, add:
- 2 points in **Tough Hide** (Vitality) — you are in melee range
- 2 points in **Mana Font** (Essence) — Spellblade combos cost mana
- 2 points in **Quick Recovery** upgrade (Vitality) — even more healing
- Save remaining points for future tiers

### Wand Build (Wildroots / Tomekeepers / Nightscribes)

After the starter build, add:
- 3 points in **Arcane Flow** upgrades (Essence) — maximize casting
- 2 points in **Mana Font** (Essence) — wand builds are mana-hungry
- 1 point in **Wanderer's Step** (Covenant) — kiting is easier with speed
- Save remaining points

### Familiar-Master Build (Any Coven — Advanced)

After the starter build, add:
- 2 points in **Collector's Instinct** (Familiar) — more materials
- 3 points in **Familiar Bond** upgrade (Familiar) — more pet damage
- 2 points in **Tough Hide** (Vitality) — let your pet tank
- Remaining points: save for future familiar evolution nodes

## Point Economy

| Source | Points Earned |
|--------|--------------|
| Starting | 3 points |
| Level 2-10 | 1 point per level |
| Level 11-20 | 1 point every 2 levels |
| Boss kills | 1 point each (first kill only) |
| Hearth milestone upgrades | 1 point each |

Total points available in current build: approximately **35-40 points** if you complete all content.

---

## Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Spreading points across all 4 branches** — you get no powerful major nodes
2. **Skipping Extra Familiar Slot** — two familiars is twice the power
3. **Investing in C-tier nodes** — Night Vision and Ember Touch are traps
4. **Not saving points for Hearth milestones** — check what each Hearth tier unlocks before spending everything
