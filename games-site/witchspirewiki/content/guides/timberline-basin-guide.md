# Timberline Basin Region Guide — Full Exploration

> **Applies to EA v0.1.1d / Updated: July 11, 2026**

Timberline Basin is the second region you unlock after defeating the Al'uunian Golem in Vyrs and passing through the South Passage gate. Dense forests, crystal caves, and elevated ruins define this mid-level zone (levels 8-16). This guide maps every point of interest, resource hotspot, and familiar spawn.

---

## Region Overview

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Level Range | 8-16 |
| Area Size | Large (approx 3km x 3km) |
| Flight Pillars | 6 |
| Bosses | None (quest-based encounters) |
| NPCs | 3 |
| Hidden Chests | 8 |
| Resource Caves | 3 |
| Recommended Stay | Until level 16 or before Mira Isles |

### How to Enter

1. Defeat Al'uunian Golem in Vyrs Eastern Ruins
2. Collect the **Golem Core** from the arena floor
3. Use it at the Hearth crystal to create the **Region Unlock Token**
4. Equip the Token and approach the Vyrs South Passage gate
5. The gate opens automatically — you are now in Timberline Basin

**Don't forget**: Buy the Timberline Basin map from Scholar Vex in Vyrs (500 gold) before leaving — it reveals all Flight Pillar locations.

## Key Locations

### Al'uunian Tower

The central landmark of Timberline Basin — a massive stone spire surrounded by floating crystalline shards.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Coordinates | Center-north of the map |
| Enemies | Spectral Witches (Lv 10-12), Crystal Golems (Lv 12-14) |
| Purpose | Main quest location, lore exposition, mid-boss encounter |
| Flight Pillar | On the tower's mid-level platform |
| Hidden Chests | 2 (one on the ground floor behind a bookshelf, one on the upper balcony) |

Inside the tower, you will find:
- **Scholar Vex's Journal** (lore item, starts a side quest)
- **Crystal Console** — activates the basin's Flight Pillar network
- **Mid-boss**: Corrupted Crystal Golem (Lv 14, drops Shard of Alignment)

### Shard Caverns

An underground cave system accessible from the eastern cliffside. Glowing crystal formations light the path.

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| Entrance | East side of the map, behind a waterfall |
| Enemies | Crystal Crawlers (Lv 11-13), Echo Wraiths (Lv 12-14) |
| Resources | Crystal Shards (abundant), Coal deposits, Iron Ore |
| Hidden Chests | 3 |
| Boss | Crystal Crawler Queen (Lv 15, optional) |

**Key spots inside Shard Caverns:**

- **Crystal Vein Junction** — 5+ high-density Crystal Shard nodes. Best farming spot in the region for crystal materials.
- **Echo Pool** — Underground lake. Refills mana when standing in the water. Fish for rare Moonleaf items.
- **Queen's Nest** — Deepest chamber. Crystal Crawler Queen optional boss drops **Queen's Carapace** (armor upgrade material).

### Flight Pillars (6 total)

| # | Location | Notes |
|---|----------|-------|
| 1 | Al'uunian Tower mid-level | Requires climbing the tower exterior |
| 2 | Shard Caverns entrance | Above the waterfall, easy to miss |
| 3 | Basin Crossroads | Central fork, next to the merchant |
| 4 | Western Ridge | Guarded by a Crystal Golem |
| 5 | Northern Lookout | Overlooks the entire basin |
| 6 | Southern Gate | Near the exit to Vyrs (return travel) |

## NPC Locations

| NPC | Location | Services |
|-----|----------|----------|
| Merchant Reyes | Basin Crossroads (center) | Sells Steel tools, Coal x5 stack, Basin map fragments |
| Archivist Maren | Al'uunian Tower ground floor | Lore dialogue, unlocks Crystal Shard refining recipe |

## Resource Farming Route

Optimal loop for Timberline Basin farming:

```
Southern Gate → Basin Crossroads (vendor + pillar) → Al'uunian Tower 
(chests + pillar) → Shard Caverns (crystals + coal + chests) 
→ Western Ridge (fiber + chest) → Northern Lookout (chest) 
→ Back to Crossroads
```

Total time: ~30 minutes. Total haul: ~25 Crystal Shards, ~10 Coal, ~15 Iron Ore, ~8 Moonleaf, ~15 Sturdy Fiber.

## Leaving Timberline Basin

The exit to Mira Isles is located at the **Eastern Docks** — a wooden pier guarded by a Lv 16 Shore Sentinel. You need:

- **Level 14+** (recommended)
- **Tier 4 Hearth** (recommended)
- **Shard of Alignment** (dropped by Corrupted Crystal Golem in Al'uunian Tower)
- **500 gold** (boat fare to Mira Isles)

**Completion checklist before leaving:**
- [ ] Activate all 6 Flight Pillars
- [ ] Find all 8 hidden chests
- [ ] Capture a Crystal Sprite familiar
- [ ] Defeat Crystal Crawler Queen (optional but recommended)
- [ ] Stock up on Coal from Merchant Reyes
- [ ] Upgrade to Steel tools
