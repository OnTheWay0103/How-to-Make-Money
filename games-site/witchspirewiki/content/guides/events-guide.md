# Events Guide — Night Events, Seasonal Activities, and Special Encounters

> **Applies to EA v0.1.1d / Updated: July 23, 2026**

Witchspire features a variety of dynamic events that occur as you explore, providing bonus loot, rare materials, and unique encounters. Some events are triggered by progression, others are time-based within each session, and a few rotate on a real-world schedule. This guide catalogs every known event type and how to make the most of each.

---

## Event Types Overview

| Event Category | Trigger | Frequency | Difficulty |
|----------------|---------|-----------|------------|
| **Night Events** | In-game time cycle | Per expedition (night cycle) | Medium-Hard |
| **World Boss** | Region progression | Respawns on timer | Hard |
| **Rare Encounters** | Random exploration | Low chance per zone | Variable |
| **Environmental Events** | Weather/region conditions | Per session | Easy-Medium |
| **Seasonal Festivals** | Real-world calendar | Limited-time only | Variable |

---

## Night Events

Night Events are the most frequently encountered event type. When the in-game clock reaches night in certain regions, special conditions activate.

### Shuddersands Night Events

The Shuddersands are the primary Night Event region. When night falls:

- **Ethereal enemies spawn** at 3x the daytime rate
- **Astral Essence nodes** appear in fixed locations for the duration
- **Ambient light** reduces dramatically — Nightvision Brew is strongly recommended
- **Special boss variant:** The Nightfall Lumi can spawn (enhanced version of the standard Lumi boss)

| Night Event | Duration | Rewards | Notes |
|-------------|----------|---------|-------|
| Ethereal Surge | 15 min | Ethereal Dust, Astral Essence | Enemies drop 2x materials |
| Astral Bloom | 20 min | Astral Essence nodes (3-5 per zone) | Marked on map with a purple icon |
| Nightfall Lumi | Until defeated | Legendary crafting materials, unique gear | Spawns after 10 min of night |

**Night Event Checklist:**
- [ ] Craft Nightvision Brew before entering
- [ ] Clear inventory — you will pick up rare materials
- [ ] Bring Major Healing Potions (4+) and a Familiar Revival Tonic
- [ ] If playing co-op, assign one player as loot collector and others as bodyguards
- [ ] Mark Astral Essence nodes on your map as you find them — they despawn at dawn

### Timberline Basin Night Events

Night in Timberline Basin is less intense but still profitable:

- **Crystal Bud nodes** glow visibly, making them easier to spot
- **Coal deposits** have a higher yield per node
- **Berserk Timber Wolves** spawn with increased drop rates for hides and bones

## World Boss Events

World bosses appear after meeting specific progression milestones in each region. They operate on a respawn timer (approximately 2-4 hours real-time per boss).

| Boss | Region | Recommended Level | Key Drops |
|------|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| Alunean Golem | Vyrs (South) | Hearth 3+ | Steel recipe, Golem Core (building material) |
| Shudder Matriarch | Timberline Basin | Hearth 4+ | Ethereal Dust (guaranteed), unique wand schematic |
| Lumi (Nightfall) | Shuddersands | Hearth 5+ | Astral Essence, legendary cape pattern |
| Mira Guardian | Mira Isles | Hearth 5+ | Waterwalking schematic, legendary ring |

### Boss Event Strategy

1. **Check respawn status** before leaving base. Bosses have a regional announcement when they respawn.
2. **Bring a full potion loadout.** Boss fights last 3-8 minutes depending on group size and gear.
3. **Co-op is strongly recommended.** Solo bossing is possible but requires near-perfect play and endgame gear.
4. **Loot is shared.** All players in the boss area who contributed damage receive independent loot drops. There is no loot conflict.
5. **World bosses scale** with the number of players nearby. A 4-player group faces a tougher boss than a 2-player group, but the loot quality also scales.

## Rare Encounters

These are low-probability events that can occur during normal exploration.

| Encounter | Location | Trigger | Reward |
|-----------|----------|---------|--------|
| Wandering Familiar | Any region, usually near water | Rare spawn (estimated 5% chance per zone visit) | High-stat familiar, rare color variant |
| Ethereal Rift | Timberline Basin caves | Random after defeating an Ethereal enemy | 3-5 Ethereal Dust, boss portal |
| Ancient Altar | Mira Isles | Interact with hidden altar | Permanent stat buff (+5 Max HP) |
| Crashing Supply Pod | Any region (sky flash + impact sound) | Random spawn — listen for audio cue | High-tier materials, rare recipes |
| Ghost Witch | Shuddersands | Night-only, random location | Unique cosmetic recipe |

**Tip:** The Crashing Supply Pod event plays a distinctive whistling sound followed by a ground-shaking impact. When you hear it, stop what you are doing and head toward the sound — the pod despawns after 5 minutes.

## Environmental Events

These are triggered by weather or regional conditions rather than enemies.

| Event | Region | Effect | Duration |
|-------|--------|--------|----------|
| Crystal Bloom | Timberline Basin | All Crystal Bud nodes double their yield | 10 min |
| Nectar Flood | Vyrs | Flower nodes produce 3x Nectar | 15 min |
| Mist Surge | Mira Isles | Reduced visibility, rare fish spawn in pools | 20 min |
| Ash Fall | Shuddersands | Fire damage +10%, water sources heal instead of harm | 15 min |

### How to Track Environmental Events

Open your map and look for a small icon next to the region name. A green leaf icon means an active beneficial event. A red weather icon means a hazardous event is in progress. Both types offer unique farming opportunities.

---

## Seasonal Festivals

Seasonal events run on a real-world calendar. These are the known seasonal events as of EA v0.1.1d:

| Festival | Time of Year | Activities | Rewards |
|----------|-------------|------------|---------|
| Harvest Moon Festival | September-October | Special Night Events, pumpkin farming | Seasonal hats, limited-time decorations, double Nectar yields |
| Frostfall | December-January | Snow-covered Vyrs, ice fishing, Winter Lumi boss | Frost-themed gear, seasonal furniture, unique building skins |
| Spring Equinox | March-April | Bloom bonuses, rare familiar spawn rate up | Floral cosmetics, pet growth boost items |

Seasonal festival items are **account-bound** and persist between wipes. If you plan to play long-term, prioritize seasonal events for their unique cosmetic rewards.

**Developer Note:** According to the official roadmap, additional seasonal events (Summer Solstice, Haunting Nights) are in development for post-EA releases.

## Event Preparation Checklist

- [ ] Potions: 4 Major Healing, 2 Major Mana, 1 Familiar Revival Tonic
- [ ] Gear: Repaired to 100%, best available weapons equipped
- [ ] Familiar: Full HP, Bonding Elixir applied if expecting a boss
- [ ] Inventory: 10+ free slots for event loot
- [ ] Co-op: Confirm group members are online and ready
- [ ] Map: Review event spawn locations before entering the region
