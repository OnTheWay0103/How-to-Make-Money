# Vyrs Region Guide — Full Exploration

> **Applies to EA v0.1.1d / Updated: June 29, 2026**

Vyrs is the starting region of Witchspire. Green fields, ruined structures, and low-level enemies (1-8) make it the safest place to learn the game. But even experienced players miss hidden chests and NPCs in Vyrs. This guide covers everything.

## Key NPC Locations

| NPC | Location | Services |
|-----|----------|----------|
| Mira the Vendor | Vyrs Crossroads (center) | Sells seeds, basic recipes, capture orbs |
| Old Finn | North of Hearth (farmhouse) | Gives fishing side quest, sells bait |
| Scholar Vex | Vyrs Eastern Ruins (library) | Lore items, maps of other regions |
| Traveler Kael | Vyrs South Passage gate | Explains region unlock mechanics |

**Talk to Scholar Vex before leaving Vyrs.** She sells the Timberline Basin map (500 gold) which reveals all Flight Pillar locations in the next zone.

## Points of Interest

### The Hearth Meadow
Your starting area. Flat, safe, ideal for base building. The Hearth crystal sits at the center of a stone circle.

### Vyrs Crossroads
Central hub with a vendor, a traveling merchant who rotates stock daily, and access to all Vyrs sub-zones.

### Vyrs South Cave
Iron ore hotspot. Enemies are level 3-4. Bring a torch (crafted at Workbench: 2 Wood + 1 Fiber). There is a hidden chamber behind a destructible wall on the right side at the midpoint.

### Eastern Ruins Arena
Large circular stone platform surrounded by ruined pillars. This is the Al'uunian Golem boss arena. Arrive prepared — entering the seal area locks you in until you defeat the boss or die.

### Vyrs Swamp (Southeast)
Small poison area with Pufffin (common Nox familiar). Nox clouds deal damage over time — come with healing potions or high poison resist.

## Vyrs Farming Route

Optimal loop for materials before tackling Al'uunian Golem:

```
Hearth → Vyrs Meadow (wood + fiber) → Crossroads (vendor) 
→ Vyrs South Cave (iron) → Swamp edge (nectar + Pufffin catch) 
→ Eastern Ruins (stone + chests) → Back to Hearth
```

Total time: ~20 minutes. Total haul: ~50 Wood, ~40 Fiber, ~15 Iron Ore, ~20 Stone, ~20 Nectar.

---

## Leaving Vyrs

When you are ready to leave, head to the **Vyrs South Passage** at the south edge of the map. You need the **Region Unlock Token** (from Al'uunian Golem) to open the gate to Timberline Basin.

**Don't rush out. Complete everything in Vyrs first:**
- [x] Catch at least 2 familiars
- [x] Upgrade Hearth to Tier 3
- [x] Craft your broom
- [x] Find all 6 hidden chests
- [x] Defeat Al'uunian Golem
- [x] Buy Timberline Basin map from Scholar Vex
