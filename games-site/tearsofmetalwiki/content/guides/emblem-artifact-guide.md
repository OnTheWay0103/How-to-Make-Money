# Tears of Metal Emblem and Artifact Guide — Complete Reference

> **Applies to Early Access v1.0 / Updated: July 23, 2026**

Emblems and artifacts are the core customization systems in Tears of Metal. Emblems grant elemental damage types and passive effects. Artifacts provide equippable bonuses with synergy potential. Understanding how these systems interact is the key to building powerful, consistent runs.

---

## Emblem System Overview

You can equip **two emblems** at a time — one Primary (determines your attack element) and one Secondary (bonus effects and synergies). Emblems are unlocked through gameplay progression and the Mystic Altar.

### Emblem Trigger Mechanics

- **Emblems trigger on hit** — Each successful attack has a chance to proc your equipped emblem's effect
- **Same-type stacking** — Equipping two emblems of the same type doubles the trigger chance but does not stack the effect
- **Different-type synergy** — Equipping different emblems unlocks combination effects (see Synergy Combinations below)
- **Trigger chance base** — Base trigger rate is approximately 15% per emblem. Attack speed increases total procs per second.

## Best Emblem Combinations

### Early Game (Act 1-2)

| Combination | Effect | Why It Works |
|-------------|--------|--------------|

| Fire + Poison | Double DoT; burns 8% HP/sec total | Melts bosses but requires Corr Resist |
| Regen + Corruption Resist | Passive healing + corruption protection | **Best for Act 3 survival** |

### Late Game (Act 3 Only)

| Combination | Effect | Why It Works |
|-------------|--------|--------------|
| Corruption Resist + Regen | Maximum survival | Non-negotiable for final boss |

### Synergy Pro Discovery

**Hidden interaction**: Lightning + Freeze does more than just chain and slow. When a frozen enemy is hit by a Lightning chain, the chain jumps to an additional target. This makes Lightning+Freeze the best emblem combination for crowd-heavy encounters throughout the game.

## Artifact System Overview

Artifacts are equippable items that provide passive bonuses. You can equip artifacts from the Armory between runs. The number of artifact slots depends on your Armory level.

### Rarity Tiers

| Rarity | Color | Stat Range | Special Effect |
|--------|-------|------------|----------------|
| Common | White | Baseline | None |
| Uncommon | Green | +20% over Common | Minor bonus effect |
| Rare | Blue | +50% over Common | Major bonus effect |
| Legendary | Orange | +100% over Common | Unique effect (build-defining) |

### Rarity vs. Synergy — Which Matters More?

**Synergy beats rarity in every case.**

A Common artifact that synergizes with your build outperforms a Legendary artifact that does nothing for your playstyle. For example:

- **Common Ironheart Pauldrons (+15% damage reduction)** on a Vanguard is better than
- **Legendary Ember-Touched Bracers (+20% attack speed while burning)** on a Vanguard that does not use fire damage

Prioritize artifacts that:
1. Match your hero's role (tank stats for Vanguard, damage for Berserker)
2. Synergize with your equipped emblems (elemental damage for emblem builds)
3. Cover your weaknesses (Corruption Resist for Act 3, Regen for sustain)

## How to Farm Artifacts

| Method | Efficiency | Notes |
|--------|------------|-------|
| Clear elite packs | High | Elites have 60% chance to drop artifacts |
| Break all destructibles | Medium | Crates, barrels, and urns can contain artifacts |
| Complete side objectives | Medium | Each act has optional artifact rewards |
| Boss kills | High | Bosses always drop 1-2 artifacts |
| Luck Emblem | Passive | +15% rare artifact chance while equipped |
| Co-op play | Higher drop rate | ~25% more artifact drops in multiplayer |

### Artifact Farming Route

1. Equip Gold Find + Luck emblems
2. Run Act 1 full clear (20-25 minutes)
3. Break every breakable object
4. Kill all elite packs
5. Sell unwanted artifacts for gold
6. Repeat until Armory is full of desired artifacts

## Build-Around Strategies

### Strategy 1: Emblem Proc Build

**Core**: Emblem-Weaver's Focus (artifact) + Elemental Mastery (emblem)
**Goal**: Maximize emblem trigger chance for constant elemental effects
**Best hero**: Marksman
**Artifacts**: Emblem-Weaver's Focus, Sage's Monocle, Thunder Core, Storm-Chaser's Quiver
**Emblems**: Lightning + Freeze
**Result**: Nearly every shot triggers at least one emblem effect

### Strategy 2: Tank-Buster Build

**Core**: Physical Damage + Critical Strike emblems
**Goal**: Maximize per-hit damage for burst windows
**Best hero**: Vanguard
**Artifacts**: Bloodied Claymore Grip, Warlord's Medallion, Ironheart Pauldrons, Highland Stone
**Emblems**: Physical Damage + Critical Strike
**Result**: Heavy swings hit critically for 2-3x normal damage

### Strategy 3: Burn Everything Build

**Core**: Dragon Stone Fragment + Fire emblem
**Goal**: Stack burn to maximum and keep it there
**Best hero**: Berserker
**Artifacts**: Dragon Stone Fragment, Ember-Touched Bracers, Phoenix Feather, Cinder Shard
**Emblems**: Fire + Attack Speed
**Result**: 10+ burn stacks on bosses dealing 15% HP/sec in passive damage
