## Map Overview

Mistfall Hunter currently has 5 maps, each with unique terrain, loot distribution, and boss encounters. Understanding the layout of each map is the single most important skill — it determines where you go, how you extract, and whether you survive.

| Map | Difficulty | Best Gear Score | Player Cap | Boss |
|----|-----------|----------------|-----------|------|
| Ruined Catacombs | Easy | Green+ | 12 | Returner Woodling |
| Forsaken Hollow | Medium | Green+ | 12 | Hollow Warden |
| Mistveil Forest | Medium | Blue+ | 10 | Verdant Ancient |
| The Bloodfen | Hard | Blue+ | 8 | Blood Matriarch |
| Obsidian Depths | Very Hard | Purple+ | 6 | Shattered King |

## 1. Ruined Catacombs

**Difficulty**: Easy | **Size**: Medium | **Time Limit**: 18 minutes

### Map Layout
Underground tomb complex with three main corridors connected by a central chamber. Tight corridors with limited sightlines.

### High-Value Loot Zones

| Zone | Value | Risk | What Drops |
|------|-------|------|-----------|
| Central Chamber (Boss Room) | High | Medium | Boss loot, rare chest |
| North Crypt | Medium | Low | Chests, Gyldenblood piles |
| South Corridor | Medium | Low | Common gear, materials |
| East Vault (Locked) | Very High | Low | Purple+ gear, but requires a Rusted Key |

### Extraction Points

| Point | Location | Type | Notes |
|-------|---------|------|-------|
| Exit A | North-east corner | Standard | 30s channel, exposed corridor |
| Exit B | South-west corner | Standard | 30s channel, behind pillar cover |
| Exit C | Central chamber (post-boss) | Conditional | Only opens after boss is defeated |

### Danger Zones

- **Central chamber**: High traffic. Everyone rotates through the center. Expect PvP.
- **East Vault corridor**: Campers common. Players will hide near the locked door to ambush key-holders.
- **South Corridor**: Dead end. If you get caught here, only one escape route.

### Recommended Route

**For loot**: North Crypt → East Vault (if you have a key) → Central Chamber (check boss first) → Exit A or B
**For quick extraction**: South Corridor → Exit B

## 2. Forsaken Hollow

**Difficulty**: Medium | **Size**: Large | **Time Limit**: 20 minutes

### Map Layout
Abandoned village with above-ground buildings and an underground arena. Verticality matters — buildings have 1-2 floors.

### High-Value Loot Zones

| Zone | Value | Risk | What Drops |
|------|-------|------|-----------|
| Village Center | High | High | Chests, Gyldenblood, consumables |
| Well (Underground Arena) | Very High | Very High | Boss loot (Hollow Warden) |
| Church (West) | Medium | Medium | Gear chest, materials |
| Blacksmith (East) | High | Medium | Weapon drops, upgrade mats |
| Abandoned Homes (3x) | Low-Medium | Low | Random loot, minor Gyldenblood |

### Extraction Points

| Point | Location | Type | Notes |
|-------|---------|------|-------|
| Exit A | North gate | Standard | 30s channel, open field |
| Exit B | South bridge | Standard | 30s channel, bottleneck |
| Exit C | Church basement | Conditional | Opens after Church zone is cleared |

### Danger Zones

- **Village Center**: Open courtyard with sightlines from all buildings. Sniper heaven.
- **Well entrance**: Narrow staircase. Ambush point for teams guarding the boss.
- **Church tower**: Best sniping position on the map. Contest it if you have a Blackarrow.

### Recommended Route

**For loot**: Blacksmith → Village Center (snipe from Church first) → Boss (if clear) → Exit A
**For safe extract**: Abandoned Homes → South Bridge → Exit B (avoid center)

## 3. Mistveil Forest

**Difficulty**: Medium | **Size**: Large | **Time Limit**: 22 minutes

### Map Layout
Dense forest with open clearings. Heavy fog reduces visibility to ~30 meters. Sound carries far.

### High-Value Loot Zones

| Zone | Value | Risk | What Drops |
|------|-------|------|-----------|
| North Clearing (Boss) | Very High | High | Boss loot (Verdant Ancient), rare herbs |
| Eastern Ruins | High | Medium | Ancient gear, rune fragments |
| Western Pond | Medium | Low | Materials, Gyldenblood |
| Central Crossing | High | Very High | Multiple chests, PvP hotspot |
| Hidden Grove (East-center) | Very High | Low | Requires pathfinding through bushes |

### Extraction Points

| Point | Location | Type | Notes |
|-------|---------|------|-------|
| Exit A | South path | Standard | 30s channel, tree cover |
| Exit B | North-west cliff | Standard | 30s channel, exposed on one side |
| Exit C | Hidden Grove | Conditional | Only opens if you discover the hidden path |

### Danger Zones

- **Central Crossing**: Three paths converge. Guaranteed player encounters.
- **Boss arena**: Sound of the boss fight attracts players from the entire map.
- **Eastern Ruins walls**: Ranged players on walls have clear shots into the ruins.

### Recommended Route

**For loot**: Eastern Ruins → Hidden Grove → Boss (late, after teams have fought) → Exit C
**For safe extract**: Western Pond → South Path → Exit A (avoid center at all costs)

## 4. The Bloodfen

**Difficulty**: Hard | **Size**: Medium | **Time Limit**: 18 minutes

### Map Layout
Swamp map with water zones (30% movement slow) and raised platforms. The water deals ticking damage during the boss's enrage phase.

### High-Value Loot Zones

| Zone | Value | Risk | What Drops |
|------|-------|------|-----------|
| Southern Chamber (Boss) | Very High | Very High | Boss loot (Blood Matriarch) |
| Northern Platform | High | Medium | Purple+ gear, rare mats |
| Central Island | Medium | High | Gyldenblood, chests (contest zone) |
| Eastern Cave | High | Low | Locked chest, requires key |

### Extraction Points

| Point | Location | Type | Notes |
|-------|---------|------|-------|
| Exit A | North-east | Standard | 30s channel on platform |
| Exit B | South-east | Standard | 30s channel in water (dangerous) |
| Exit C | Central Island | Conditional | Opens after water level drops (Phase 2 boss) |

### Danger Zones

- **Water areas**: Slow movement + potential DoT = death zone. Stay on platforms.
- **Central Island**: Elevated with clear sightlines. Anyone on this island controls the map.
- **Boss arena**: Tight space with water floor. Hard to maneuver.

### Recommended Route

**For loot**: Eastern Cave → Northern Platform → Boss (if you have a squad) → Exit A
**For safe extract**: Northern Platform → Exit A (skip boss and center)

## 5. Obsidian Depths

**Difficulty**: Very Hard | **Size**: Small | **Time Limit**: 15 minutes

### Map Layout
Volcanic cave system with a central pillar arena. Tight corridors with limited cover. The smallest map with the highest stakes.

### High-Value Loot Zones

| Zone | Value | Risk | What Drops |
|------|-------|------|-----------|
| Central Arena (Boss) | Legendary | Extreme | Shattered King boss loot |
| Obsidian Forge | Very High | High | Legendary weapon parts |
| Crystal Cavern | High | Medium | Rune fragments, Gyldenblood |
| Mini-boss Room (West) | Very High | High | Required kill to unlock boss portal |

### Extraction Points

| Point | Location | Type | Notes |
|-------|---------|------|-------|
| Exit A | North corridor | Standard | 30s channel, tight space |
| Exit B | South corridor | Standard | 30s channel, open to flank |

### Danger Zones

- **Everywhere**: This map has 6 player cap and no safe zones. Expect PvP in every room.
- **Mini-boss room**: Campers often wait here. Clear the room and check corners.
- **Forge**: High-value loot means high traffic.

### Recommended Route

**For loot**: Crystal Cavern → Mini-boss → Forge → Boss → Exit A
**For safe extract**: Crystal Cavern → Exit A (extract early, the map is lethal)

## Map Selection Strategy

| Your Goal | Best Map | Why |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| Learn the game | Ruined Catacombs | Lowest risk, forgiving layout |
| Farm Gyldenblood | Forsaken Hollow | Most loot per minute at low risk |
| Farm gear | Mistveil Forest | Best gear drop rates |
| PvP practice | The Bloodfen | Small map, forced engagements |
| End-game loot | Obsidian Depths | Legendary drops only |
| Quick extraction | Ruined Catacombs | 5-7 minute runs |

## General Map Tips

- **Sound is your best tool**: In all maps, sound travels. Learn the audio cues — footsteps on different surfaces, chest openings, boss aggro sounds. You can hear enemies before you see them.
- **The mist timer is real**: When the mist closure warning appears (5 minutes remaining), head toward an extraction point immediately. Do not engage fights near the edge — the mist kills fast.
- **Know multiple extraction points**: If your planned exit is camped, have a backup. Never commit to one extraction point early.
- **Use terrain to break line of sight**: Every map has pillars, trees, or walls. Use them when being chased by ranged attackers.
- **Watch choke points**: Corridors, bridges, and doorways are where 80% of PvP happens. Approach them carefully.
