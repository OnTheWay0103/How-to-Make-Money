## Why Play Mercenary?

Mercenary is the **best class for beginners** and remains competitive at all skill levels. It offers:
- 70% damage reduction on block
- Simple, effective combos
- Strong survivability in PvE and PvP
- Forgiving learning curve for extraction mechanics

## Core Mechanics

### Block and Counter

The Mercenary's shield is your primary tool. Blocking attacks:
- Reduces incoming damage by 70%
- Builds **Will** (a resource that empowers your next attack)
- Enables **Rising to the Challenge** (Shield Dash into stun)

The loop: **Block → Build Will → Shield Dash (stun) → Sword combo → Block again**

### Sword Tip Stacking

Landing consecutive sword hits stacks **Sword Tip** on enemies. At max stacks:
- **Blade Edge** deals massive bonus damage
- **Strong Edge** adds stagger and armor penetration

Master the rhythm: light attacks to stack → Blade Edge to burst → reset.

## Best Talents

### Core Talents (Take These First)

| Talent | Effect | Priority |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **Resilient Body** | Builds Will while blocking (faster resource generation) | Must-have |
| **Rising to the Challenge** | Shield Dash now stuns enemies for 1.5s | Must-have |
| **Stone Breaker Slash** | Next attack after blocking a heavy attack deals +40% damage | High |
| **Bulwark** | Block strength +15% (stacks with gear affixes) | High |
| **Shield Wall** | Unlocks AoE shield barrier for squad protection | Medium |

### Optional Talents

| Talent | Effect | When to Take |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Iron Footing** | Cannot be knocked back while blocking | PvP-heavy matches |
| **Vanguard's Charge** | Shield Dash travels 30% further | Open maps |
| **Last Stand** | When below 20% HP, block reduces damage by 90% | Clutch situations |
| **War Cry** | AoE taunt + team damage buff for 10s | Squad play |

## Best Affixes (Gear)

Prioritize these affixes on your gear:

| Affix | Why | Priority |
|-------|-----|----------|
| **Bulwark** | +Block strength | #1 |
| **Valor** | +Damage after blocking | #2 |
| **Fortitude** | +Max HP | #3 |
| **Iron Skin** | +Physical damage reduction | #4 |
| **Retaliation** | Reflect % of blocked damage | PvP only |

**Affix priority for PvE**: Bulwark > Valor > Fortitude > Iron Skin
**Affix priority for PvP**: Bulwark > Retaliation > Fortitude > Valor

## Build Variants

### PvE Tank Build

- **Focus**: Maximum survivability, consistent damage
- **Talents**: Resilient Body → Rising to the Challenge → Bulwark → Stone Breaker Slash → Last Stand
- **Affixes**: Bulwark, Fortitude, Iron Skin
- **Playstyle**: Pull aggro from Corroded, block everything, let teammates DPS safely

### PvP Brawler Build

- **Focus**: Burst damage after blocks, punishing aggressive players
- **Talents**: Resilient Body → Rising to the Challenge → Stone Breaker Slash → Retaliation → Iron Footing
- **Affixes**: Bulwark, Valor, Retaliation
- **Playstyle**: Let enemies swing first, block → stun → burst combo. Save Shield Dash for interrupts.

### Solo Extraction Build

- **Focus**: Balanced survivability and damage for solo play
- **Talents**: Resilient Body → Stone Breaker Slash → Rising to the Challenge → Bulwark → Last Stand
- **Affixes**: Bulwark, Valor, Fortitude
- **Playstyle**: Play slow, pick fights carefully, prioritize extracting with loot over PvP

## Gear Priorities

### Weapons

- **Sword**: Prioritize damage + Valor affix. Blue rarity is sufficient for most runs.
- **Shield**: Prioritize Bulwark affix. Rarity matters more for shields (higher block %).

### Armor

| Slot | Priority Stat |
|------|--------------|
| Helmet | Fortitude (HP) |
| Chest | Iron Skin (phys DR) |
| Gloves | Valor (damage) |
| Boots | Movement speed or stamina regen |

### Consumables

Always bring:
- **Healing potions** (at least 2)
- **Stamina potions** (at least 1)
- **Bandages** (for bleed removal)

## PvP Matchups

| Enemy Class | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|
| **Sorcerer** | Block spells, Shield Dash to close gap, burst in melee range. Do not chase at range. |
| **Shadowstrix** | Block their ambush opener. Once stealth is broken, they are fragile — punish hard. |
| **Blackarrow** | Use terrain to block line of sight. Shield Dash to close distance. |
| **Withered Knight** | Bait their parry. Strike after they miss. Avoid trading into their Reckoning burst. |
| **Seer** | Target the Seer first in squads. Without their support, the team crumbles. |
| **Mercenary (mirror)** | Whoever blocks first and counters wins. Patience beats aggression. |

## Quick Tips

- **Don't spam attacks** — a blocked Mercenary is a living Mercenary
- **Shield Dash is also a mobility tool** — use it to escape or reposition, not just offensively
- **Watch your stamina** — blocking costs stamina. If it depletes, you are dead
- **The best PvP strategy is patience** — let the enemy make the first move
