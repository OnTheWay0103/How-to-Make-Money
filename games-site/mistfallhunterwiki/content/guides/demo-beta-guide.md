## Current Playtest Status

Mistfall Hunter is currently in its **Playtest phase** on PC via Steam. The development team has announced plans for an Open Beta later in 2026, followed by the full 1.0 release. This guide covers how to access the current playtest, what content is available, and what to expect from future beta phases.

## How to Access the Mistfall Hunter Demo / Playtest

### Steam (PC) — Current Playtest

The playtest is available through Steam. Follow these steps:

1. Open the Steam client and navigate to the **Store** tab
2. Search for "Mistfall Hunter" or use the direct store page link
3. Scroll down to the **Request Access** section below the game description
4. Click **Request Access** — this sends a request to the developer
5. Wait for approval (approval times vary from same-day to 1-2 weeks depending on wave)
6. Once approved, the game appears in your Steam Library as "Mistfall Hunter Playtest"
7. Click **Install** and download (~15 GB estimated)

| Step | Action | Expected Time |
|------|--------|---------------|
| 1-3 | Find store page and request access | 2 minutes |
| 4 | Wait for approval | Same day to 2 weeks |
| 5 | Receive approval email | Notification via Steam |
| 6-7 | Download and install | 15-30 minutes (depends on connection) |

### Steam Next Fest Demo (Limited Time)

During Steam Next Fest events, Mistfall Hunter may offer a **timed demo** that anyone can download without requesting access. These demos:

- Are available for approximately 7 days during the event
- Do not require approval — install and play immediately
- Include a subset of the full playtest content (typically 1-2 maps, 3 classes)
- Are separate from the playtest — progress does not carry between them

## Progress Carry-Over and Wipes

### Will Playtest Progress Carry Over to Full Release?

**No.** The developers have confirmed that all playtest and beta progress will be wiped before the full 1.0 release. This includes:

- Character levels and skill points
- Camp upgrades
- Stash items and gear
- Gyldenblood currency
- Any unlocked cosmetics earned during playtest

### Why Wipes Happen

1. **Balance changes** — skills, gear stats, and upgrade costs change between builds
2. **Economy reset** — the extraction economy needs a fresh start for launch
3. **Data cleanup** — test accounts, bugged items, and exploit-generated wealth are cleared

### What May Carry Over

While full progress wipes are the norm, some developers offer **participation rewards** that transfer to the live game:

| Item | Likelihood of Carry-Over | Notes |
|------|-------------------------|-------|
| Playtest-exclusive cosmetic | Likely | Developers have mentioned "playtest rewards" |
| Account-bound titles | Possible | Common in competitive games |
| In-game currency | Very unlikely | Would unbalance launch economy |
| Gear and items | No | Confirmed wiped |
| Camp upgrades | No | Confirmed wiped |

The safest approach: treat the playtest as **practice and learning**. Experiment with classes and builds you would not normally try, because nothing is permanent.

## Known Demo Features and Gameplay Scope

### What You Can Expect to Do

During the playtest, you can:

1. **Extract 15-20 times per session** before the gear pool runs thin
2. **Try all 5 classes** — each with unique abilities and playstyles
3. **Learn both maps** — Ashfall Ruins (open outdoor) and Hollow Depths (tight corridors)
4. **Fight the Returner Woodling** — the only mini-boss in the current build
5. **Engage in full PvP** — 3-team, 2-player squad format
6. **Upgrade your camp to level 10** — unlock the first tier of all buildings
7. **Craft basic consumables** — health potions, bandages, weapon repair kits

### Playtest Limitations to Be Aware Of

| Limitation | Impact | Workaround |
|------------|--------|------------|
| Small player pool | Longer matchmaking (2-5 minutes peak, 10+ off-peak) | Play during evening hours in your region |
| Camp level 10 cap | Cannot unlock mid-tier upgrades | Focus on upgrading the Goddess Statue first |
| Only 2 maps | Repetition after 10+ hours | Alternate between maps each run |
| No ranked mode | No competitive ladder | Set personal goals (extraction streak, kill count) |
| Bugs and crashes | Playtest build has known issues | Save settings, verify files frequently |
| Occasional server downtime | Patches deploy weekly (usually Tuesdays) | Check the official Discord for maintenance announcements |

The Mistfall Hunter playtest is your opportunity to learn the game before launch — but do not expect progress to carry over. Console players should wishlist the game on their platform of choice and join the Discord for beta access announcements.
