## Why Play Withered Knight?

Withered Knight is the **high-skill, high-reward melee class** in Mistfall Hunter. It combines parry-based defense with sigil detonation burst damage. In the right hands, a Withered Knight can delete enemies faster than any other class. In the wrong hands, missed parries mean quick deaths.

## Core Mechanics

### Withering Sigils

Your attacks apply **Withering Sigils** to enemies (up to 5 stacks). Each sigil is a ticking time bomb — detonate them all at once with **Reckoning** for massive burst damage.

The loop: **Apply Sigils → Stack to 5 → Reckoning (detonate) → Burst damage + AoE CC**

### Parry System

Timed block just before an enemy attack lands. A successful parry:
- Negates all damage
- Applies 2 instant Withering Sigils (with **Riposte** talent)
- Opens a 2-second counter window

### Thorn Guide (Grapple)

Your gap-closer. Pull enemies toward you or pull yourself to terrain. Essential for closing distance on ranged classes.

## Combat Flow

### PvE
1. Thorn Guide → close gap
2. Light attack chain → apply sigils
3. Parry enemy counter-attack → +2 sigils (Riposte)
4. At 5 sigils → Reckoning → everything dies
5. Repeat

### PvP
1. Bait attacks — do NOT swing first
2. Parry their opener → +2 sigils + counter window
3. Heavy attack during counter window → +2 sigils
4. Thorn Guide to prevent escape
5. Reckoning at 4-5 stacks → kill confirm

## PvP Matchups

| Enemy Class | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|
| **Mercenary** | Bait Shield Dash. Parry it. They are dead in your counter window. |
| **Sorcerer** | Thorn Guide immediately. Stay in their face. Parry spells — yes, spells can be parried. |
| **Blackarrow** | Use terrain. Thorn Guide to close. One Reckoning burst kills them. |
| **Shadowstrix** | Listen for stealth sounds. Parry their ambush opener (predict the timing). If you miss, you die. |
| **Seer** | Easy if isolated. Kill before their team arrives. |
| **Withered Knight (mirror)** | Whoever parries first wins. Feint attacks to bait their parry. |

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Detonating at 2 sigils | Always wait for 4-5 stacks. Damage scales exponentially. |
| Swinging first in PvP | WK is a counter-fighter. Let them attack, you parry. |
| Missing Thorn Guide | It has travel time. Lead your target. |
| Over-relying on parry | Some attacks cannot be parried (red glow). Dodge those. |
| Forgetting Reckoning has AoE | The detonation hits all enemies near the target. Group fights are your strength. |

## Build Variants

### PvE Boss Killer
- **Talents**: Riposte → Shattering Reckoning → Withering Presence → Death's Patience → Grave Walker
- **Affixes**: Physical Might, Armor Rating, Cooldown Reduction
- **Playstyle**: Tank hits with parry, stack sigils fast, detonate for massive burst

### PvP Duelist
- **Talents**: Riposte → Shattering Reckoning → Iron Grip → Withering Presence
- **Affixes**: Physical Might, Impact, Armor Rating
- **Playstyle**: Counter-focused. Parry → burst → execute. Never fight fair.
