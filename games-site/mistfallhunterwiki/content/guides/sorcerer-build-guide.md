## Why Play Sorcerer?

Sorcerer is the **highest damage class** in Mistfall Hunter. If you want to delete enemies before they reach you, this is your class. It offers:
- Best-in-class AoE damage
- Strong crowd control with elemental reactions
- Teleport escape (Glacial Blink)
- Two viable specs: Elemental and Stardust

## Core Mechanics

### Elemental Cycling

The Elemental spec rotates between Fire, Ice, and Thunder spells. Each element applies a status, and switching elements triggers bonus effects:
- **Fire** → Burn DoT
- **Ice** → Slow + chance to freeze
- **Thunder** → Chain damage to nearby enemies

Cycling Fire → Ice → Thunder in sequence triggers **Elemental Resonance**, boosting all damage by 25% for 8 seconds.

### Stardust Burst

The Stardust spec focuses on instant-cast spells and massive single-target burst. Instead of cycling, you build Stardust charges and unleash them in a rapid barrage.

**Elemental = sustained AoE + control. Stardust = burst + instant casts.**

## Build Variants

### Elemental AoE Build (PvE + Team Fights)

- **Talents**: Elemental Resonance → Pyromancer's Fury → Frostbite → Elemental Barrier → Static Field
- **Skill rotation**: Fire (Burn) → Ice (Freeze) → Thunder (Chain) → Repeat
- **Affixes**: Spell Power, Cooldown Reduction, Mana Regen
- **Playstyle**: Stay behind your frontline. Cycle elements on cooldown. Use Glacial Blink to reposition if dived.

### Stardust Burst Build (PvP + Bosses)

- **Talents**: Celestial Alignment → Nova Burst → Astral Phase → Meteor Shower
- **Skill rotation**: Build Stardust charges → Full release for burst → Rebuild
- **Affixes**: Spell Power, Critical Strike Chance, Cast Speed
- **Playstyle**: Poke from range until Stardust is full. Find an opening. Release everything. If target survives, disengage with Glacial Blink and rebuild.

## PvP Matchups

| Enemy Class | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|
| **Mercenary** | Kite forever. Never let them get in melee range. Glacial Blink the moment they Shield Dash. |
| **Shadowstrix** | Stay in open areas. If you hear stealth, Glacial Blink immediately. You cannot survive their ambush combo. |
| **Blackarrow** | Close the gap. Your spells out-trade their arrows at close-mid range. |
| **Withered Knight** | Bait the parry. Cast a weak spell first. If they parry, punish with your real combo. |
| **Seer** | Focus the Seer. They cannot out-heal your damage if you target them directly. |
| **Sorcerer (mirror)** | Elemental beats Stardust in extended trades. Stardust beats Elemental if the burst lands. Who cycles better wins. |

## Positioning Tips

- **You are a glass cannon** — position like you have 1 HP at all times
- **High ground is your friend** — spells have no damage falloff over distance (arrows do)
- **Doorways and choke points** — your AoE spells are deadliest when enemies are funneled
- **Never be the first one into a room** — let your Mercenary or Withered Knight go first
- **Glacial Blink is NOT just an escape** — use it aggressively to reposition for better angles

## Quick Tips

- **Cycle elements even when no enemies are nearby** — maintaining Elemental Resonance uptime means you are always ready
- **Save Glacial Blink for Shadowstrix ambush** — if it's on cooldown, you are a free kill
- **Thunder Storm finishes low HP enemies** — chain damage secures kills that might otherwise escape
- **Don't overcommit to Stardust burst** — missing means you have nothing for 15+ seconds
