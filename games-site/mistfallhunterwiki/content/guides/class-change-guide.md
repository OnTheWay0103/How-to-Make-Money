## Changing Your Class

### Can You Switch Classes?

**Yes.** Mistfall Hunter allows you to switch classes freely between extractions. Unlike some RPGs where your class is locked at character creation, Mistfall Hunter is designed around class flexibility.

### How to Change Your Class

1. Return to your **Camp** (after an extraction or death)
2. Open the **Camp Menu** (default key: `B`)
3. Navigate to the **Class Selection** panel (the class emblem icon on the left side)
4. Select any unlocked class from the list
5. Confirm your choice — the class switches immediately
6. Equip appropriate weapons and gear from your warehouse before dropping in

You can change classes **as many times as you want** with no cooldown or cost.

### Class Unlocking

- **All 6 classes** (Mercenary, Sorcerer, Blackarrow, Shadowstrix, Withered Knight, Seer) are available from the start
- No class requires grinding or real-money purchase to unlock
- Some classes may be locked temporarily during **early access** or **beta events** — check official patch notes
- Future seasons may introduce **new classes** that may require battle pass progression or quest completion to unlock

### Do You Lose Progress When Changing Classes?

No. Each class has its own:

- **Skill Points** — earned separately per class, spent on class-specific talent trees
- **Progression** — class level and talents are saved individually
- **Build customization** — talent loadouts are stored per class

If you invest 20 skill points into Mercenary and then switch to Sorcerer, your Mercenary progress remains intact. You simply need to earn skill points for Sorcerer separately.

### Equipment Considerations

When switching classes, remember:

- **Weapons** are class-specific — your Mercenary's sword and shield cannot be used by your Sorcerer
- **Armor** pieces are generally interchangeable between classes (weight class permitting)
- **Consumables** and **trinkets** are universal
- Keep a basic gear set for each class in your warehouse to avoid dropping in naked after a class switch

## Changing Your Region

### Can You Switch Regions?

**Yes.** Mistfall Hunter lets you change your matchmaking region freely.

### How to Change Your Region

1. Open **Settings** (default key: `ESC`)
2. Navigate to the **Account** tab
3. Find the **Region** dropdown
4. Select your preferred region from the list:
   - North America (East / West)
   - Europe
   - Asia (East / Southeast)
   - Oceania
   - South America
5. **Save and restart** the game client for the change to take effect

### Region Change Costs and Restrictions

- **Free to change** — there is no currency cost
- **No cooldown** — you can switch regions as often as you like
- **Matchmaking only** — region selection affects who you queue with, not your account data
- **Progress is account-bound** — all your camps, gear, and class progress carry across regions

### When to Change Regions

- **Lower ping**: Select the region closest to your physical location
- **Playing with friends**: Change to match your party leader's region for stable connections
- **Off-peak hours**: If your home region has low population during certain hours, a different region may have faster queue times
- **Regional server events**: Some events may be region-specific

## Changing Your Server

### Can You Switch Servers?

This depends on the game's current infrastructure:

- **During full release**: Mistfall Hunter uses a **single global server infrastructure** per region (not traditional named servers). Your "server" is effectively determined by your **region selection**.
- **During beta / early access**: There may be **test servers** or **stress test servers** that are separate from the main build. Progress on test servers does **not** carry over to the live game.

### If There Are Multiple Named Servers

If Mistfall Hunter introduces named servers in the future:

- **Character progress may be server-specific** — check official announcements before switching
- **Server transfers** may require in-game currency or be limited to specific windows
- **Friends lists** may not carry across servers — you would need to re-add friends

### Currently: Change Region, Not Server

For now, changing your **Region** effectively changes your server. Read the "Changing Your Region" section above for the procedure.

## Beta vs. Full Release Differences

| Feature | Beta / Early Access | Full Release (Live) |
|---------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Class switching | Free, unlimited | Free, unlimited (confirmed) |
| Region switching | Free, unlimited | Free, unlimited (confirmed) |
| Class unlock | All 6 classes available | All 6 classes available; new classes may require unlock |
| Server separation | Test servers may exist | Single global infrastructure per region |
| Progress carryover | Beta progress may be wiped | Persistent account progression |
| Name reservation | Sometimes available | Standard character naming |

## Quick Reference

| Action | How | Cost | Limitation |
|--------|-----|------|------------|
| Change class | Camp → Class Selection | Free | None — switch anytime |
| Change region | Settings → Account → Region | Free | Requires game restart |
| Change server | Not directly supported | N/A | Use region selection instead |

## Key Takeaway

Mistfall Hunter makes class switching effortless — swap between all 6 classes at camp with no cost or penalty. For region changes, use the Account settings; this also serves as your server selection method. Each class retains its own skill point progression, so experiment freely without fear of wasting resources.
