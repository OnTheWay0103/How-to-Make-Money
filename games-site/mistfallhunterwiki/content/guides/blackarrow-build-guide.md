## Why Play Blackarrow?

Blackarrow is the **precision ranged class** in Mistfall Hunter. It rewards good aim, positioning, and patience with devastating long-range damage. It is the best PvE boss killer and excels on open maps. However, it struggles in close-quarters PvP and punishes missed shots severely.

## Core Mechanics

### Archer Spec — Precision Burst

Focused on charged shots and explosive damage:
- **Charged Shot**: Hold to charge for increased damage and accuracy. Headshots are guaranteed crits.
- **Explosive Arrow**: AoE damage on impact. Great for grouped Corroded.
- **Ice Bomb**: AoE slow + freeze. Setup for free charged headshots.

### Hunter Spec — Damage Over Time

Focused on sustained pressure through DoTs and crowd control:
- **Poison Arrow**: Applies stacking poison DoT. Each tick can crit.
- **Snare Trap**: Deployable trap that roots enemies. Triggers on proximity.
- **Tracking Mark**: Marked enemies take +15% damage from all sources and are visible through walls.

### Energy Loop

Landing shots generates energy. Missing shots breaks the energy loop and leaves you vulnerable. **Accuracy matters more than rate of fire.**

## Combat Flow

### PvE / Bosses
1. Open at max range with charged headshot
2. Tracking Mark the boss
3. Explosive Arrow → Ice Bomb → charged headshot on frozen target
4. Maintain distance. Your damage is highest at long range.
5. Repeat. Bosses with large heads (Returner Woodling) are easy targets.

### PvP
1. **Never be in melee range.** Your bow has damage falloff at close range.
2. Use Hunter spec — Snare Trap + Tracking Mark + poison pressure
3. Ice Bomb to create distance if someone closes on you
4. Reposition constantly. A stationary archer is a dead archer.
5. Target priority: Sorcerer > Seer > Shadowstrix > Withered Knight > Mercenary

## PvP Matchups

| Enemy Class | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|
| **Mercenary** | Kite forever. Snare Trap blocks Shield Dash. Poison + chip damage. |
| **Sorcerer** | You out-range them. Charged headshots while they cast. |
| **Shadowstrix** | Tracking Mark reveals them in stealth. Ice Bomb on their approach. |
| **Withered Knight** | Stay at max range. Thorn Guide can be sidestepped. |
| **Seer** | Priority target. Kill them before they support their team. |
| **Blackarrow (mirror)** | Whoever has better aim wins. Use cover between shots. |

## Positioning Tips

- **High ground is mandatory** — arrows have no damage falloff at long range, but drop over distance
- **Always have an escape route** — know the nearest cover, exit, or terrain break
- **Doorways and corridors are death traps** — stay in open areas
- **Sound is your early warning** — footsteps = reposition immediately
- **Pre-aim common approach angles** — hold angles where enemies are likely to appear

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Spamming uncharged shots | Charged shots do 3x the damage. Charge whenever safe. |
| Staying scoped too long | Tunnel vision gets you killed. Scan between shots. |
| Missing shots | If your aim is off, switch to Hunter spec (DoT is more forgiving). |
| Fighting in close quarters | Retreat to open space. You lose every close-range fight. |
