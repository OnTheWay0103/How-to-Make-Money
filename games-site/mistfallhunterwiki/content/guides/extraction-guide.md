## The Extraction Loop Explained

Extraction is the core mechanic of Mistfall Hunter. Everything you do in a raid leads to one goal: **escape with your loot**. Here is exactly how it works.

## Step-by-Step Extraction

### Step 1: Drop In

Select your class and loadout from your camp stash. Drop into the mist-shrouded ruins solo or with up to 2 squadmates. You spawn at a random entry point on the map.

### Step 2: Loot and Fight

Kill Corroded (AI enemies) for gear and Gyldenblood. Open chests. Avoid or engage rival players. The map is PvPvE — every sound you make can attract danger.

### Step 3: Find the Returner Woodling

The Returner Woodling is a **mini-boss** that spawns in high-value, contested areas of each map. It is your primary extraction objective because it drops the **Soul of Return**.

The Woodling is always in the same general zone per map, but its exact position varies. Look for:
- Marked ritual circles or corrupted tree formations
- Areas with higher Gyldenblood density
- Locations where you hear its distinctive chime/groan audio cue

### Step 4: Kill the Returner Woodling

The Woodling is not a pushover. It has:
- **AoE root attack** — dodge the vine eruption
- **Summon adds** — Corroded spawn during the fight at 50% HP
- **Charge attack** — telegraphed, dodge sideways

**Warning**: Attacking the Woodling signals other players. Expect PvPvE chaos. Either kill it fast or be ready to fight off third parties.

### Step 5: Secure the Soul of Return

The Soul of Return drops as a lootable item. It:
- Occupies one inventory slot
- Is **single-use** — one extraction attempt per Soul
- Is **lost on death** — if you die carrying it, it is gone

If you are in a squad, only **one person needs the Soul of Return** for the whole squad to extract together.

### Step 6: Find an Extraction Point

Extraction points are marked on your map. They are fixed locations per map. Travel to one with your Soul of Return.

### Step 7: Channel and Extract

Channel at the extraction point for approximately **30 seconds**. During this time:
- You are **vulnerable and visible**
- A loud audio cue plays, alerting nearby players
- If you take damage, the channel **does not reset**, but you can be killed
- If you move or attack, the channel **cancels**

Once the channel completes: **you and your squad extract successfully**. All carried loot is banked.

## Alternative Extraction Methods

Some maps have **fixed exits** that do not require a Soul of Return:
- May have longer channel times (45-60 seconds)
- May have limited uses (first-come-first-served)
- May require specific activation items
- Often in more dangerous or exposed locations

Learn these alternative exits — they can save a run if someone steals the Woodling kill.

## The Closing Mist

As the raid timer progresses, the **mist closes in** from the edges of the map:
- Deals increasing damage over time
- Forces players toward the center
- Creates natural choke points and final showdowns
- Full map coverage = death for anyone still inside

Always track the mist timer. Give yourself at least 2-3 minutes to reach extraction.

## Extraction Tips

### Risk Management

- **Extract early if you have good loot** — do not push deeper unnecessarily
- **A modest haul extracted = profit**. A great haul while dead = nothing.
- After killing a geared player: loot fast, reposition, decide immediately — stay and hunt or leave now?

### PvP Around Extraction

- Extraction points are the most dangerous locations on the map
- If you see combat at extraction, **wait** — let them fight, then extract after
- Bring smoke bombs or mobility skills to disengage if ambushed while channeling
- Squad strategy: one person channels, others guard

### Loot Priority During Extraction

When your inventory is full and you are heading to extract, prioritize:
1. **Gyldenblood** (primary currency)
2. **Epic/Legendary gear** (purple/orange)
3. **Quest items** (if active)
4. **Rare gear** (blue)
5. Everything else

Drop common items if you find something better on the way to extraction.

## Common Extraction Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Waiting until mist closes to start extracting | Plan your extraction route 3+ minutes before mist closes |
| Not clearing extraction area first | Scout the extraction point before channeling |
| Channeling immediately after getting the Soul | Other players know you just killed the Woodling — reposition first |
| Forgetting the Soul takes an inventory slot | Keep one slot free before engaging the Woodling |
| Assuming the Woodling is unguarded | It is a PvP magnet — approach with caution |
