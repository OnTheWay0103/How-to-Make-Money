## Understanding the Loot Economy

Everything in Mistfall Hunter revolves around loot. Extracted loot → Gyldenblood (sold or used) → camp upgrades + better gear → more successful extractions. Understanding where and how to farm is essential.

## Loot Quality by Zone

| Zone Type | Risk Level | Loot Quality | Player Density |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| Spawn areas (map edges) | Low | Common/Uncommon | Low |
| Mid-map ruins | Medium | Uncommon/Rare | Medium |
| Returner Woodling zone | High | Rare/Epic | High |
| Named locations/POIs | Very High | Epic/Legendary | Very High |
| Hidden stashes | Variable | Variable | Low (if you know where) |

## Best Farming Strategy by Risk Level

### Budget Run (Low Risk, Low Reward)

**Loadout cost**: <100 Gyldenblood equivalent
**Goal**: Extract with any profit
**Strategy**:

1. Spawn → immediately move away from spawn (hotspot)
2. Clear Corroded camps on the map edges
3. Loot every chest and corpse
4. Find a fixed exit (no Woodling required)
5. Extract as soon as inventory is full (8-10 items)

**Best class**: Mercenary (survivable) or Shadowstrix (avoid fights)
**Expected profit**: 50-200 Gyldenblood per run
**Time per run**: 5-8 minutes

### Standard Run (Medium Risk, Medium Reward)

**Loadout cost**: 200-500 Gyldenblood
**Goal**: Farm rare (blue) gear + Gyldenblood
**Strategy**:

1. Spawn → clear 2-3 Corroded camps on the way to mid-map
2. Scout the Woodling zone — if quiet, kill it
3. If contested, farm mid-map ruins for chests
4. Kill the Woodling if opportunity arises (third-party the fight)
5. Extract with rare+ loot

**Best class**: Sorcerer (fast clear) or Withered Knight (sustain)
**Expected profit**: 300-800 Gyldenblood per run
**Time per run**: 10-15 minutes

### High-Stakes Run (High Risk, High Reward)

**Loadout cost**: 800+ Gyldenblood
**Goal**: Epic (purple) or Legendary (orange) gear
**Strategy**:

1. Spawn → rush to named POI
2. Clear elite Corroded (they guard the best chests)
3. Secure the area against players
4. Loot the POI chests (guaranteed rare+, high chance epic)
5. Hunt the Woodling if you have not found good loot yet
6. Extract through any means necessary

**Best class**: Squad (Merc + Sorc + Seer)
**Expected profit**: 500-2000+ Gyldenblood per run
**Time per run**: 15-20 minutes

## Hidden Stash Locations

Hidden stashes contain guaranteed rare+ loot. They are in fixed locations per map but easy to miss:

| Stash Type | How to Spot | Typical Loot |
|-----------|-------------|--------------|
| Underwater cache | Bubbles on the water surface | Blue gear, Gyldenblood |
| Cliff ledge chest | Look up — chests above eye level | Purple crafting materials |
| Destroyable wall | Cracked wall texture | Blue/purple weapon |
| Behind waterfall | Waterfall with space behind it | Random rare+ |
| Grapple point stash | Requires Thorn Guide or high jump | Purple gear chance |

## Loot Priority (What to Keep)

When your inventory fills up, drop items in this order:

| Priority | Item Type | Reason |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| 1 (Keep) | Legendary (orange) gear | Endgame — never drop |
| 2 (Keep) | Epic (purple) gear | High value, competitive play |
| 3 (Keep) | Gyldenblood | Primary currency |
| 4 (Keep) | Quest items | Required for progression |
| 5 (Drop first) | Common (gray) gear | Worth almost nothing |
| 6 (Drop second) | Uncommon (green) gear | Replaceable via Shop |
| 7 (Keep if space) | Crafting materials | Used for camp upgrades |
| 8 (Keep if space) | Consumables | Useful, but not valuable |

## Corroded Farming Efficiency

### Enemy Value Ranking

| Enemy | Difficulty | Avg Loot Value | Efficiency |
|-------|-----------|---------------|------------|
| Elite Corroded (named) | Hard | 150-400 GB | High (if you can kill fast) |
| Corroded Captain | Medium | 80-150 GB | Best time/value ratio |
| Corroded Soldier | Easy | 30-60 GB | Good filler between captains |
| Corroded Spawn | Very Easy | 10-20 GB | Only if nothing else nearby |

### Farming Route: The Captain Loop

The most efficient PvE farm in the game:
1. Identify 3 Corroded Captain spawns on your map
2. Run a triangle route between them
3. Each circuit takes ~5 minutes
4. Captains respawn after ~8 minutes
5. Two circuits + extract = 500-800 GB minimum

## Gyldenblood Optimization

### What to Sell vs. Keep

| Item | Sell or Keep? | Why |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Duplicate gear | Sell | No benefit to having copies |
| Gear for classes you do not play | Sell | Inventory space is limited |
| Common crafting materials | Keep | Used for camp upgrades |
| Rare crafting materials | Keep | Required for high-tier upgrades |
| Healing items | Keep (3-5 max) | Essential for survival |

### Wealth Building Strategy
1. Run 5 budget runs → accumulate 500+ Gyldenblood
2. Upgrade Warehouse to 40+ slots (more gear storage = more flexibility)
3. Run 5 standard runs → accumulate blue gear sets
4. Run high-stakes runs once you have 3+ blue loadouts banked
5. Never risk more than 30% of your net worth on one run

## Timing Your Runs

| Time | Player Activity | Best For |
|------|----------------|---------|
| Peak hours (evenings/weekends) | High | PvP practice, high-stakes with squad |
| Off-peak (mornings/weekdays) | Low | Budget/standard farming, solo play |
| Immediately after patch | Very High | Avoid farming — everyone is playing |
| Late in wipe/season | Medium | Good mix of players and loot |

## Quick Tips

- **Check your corners** — the best loot is often in side rooms, not the main path
- **Listen for chest hums** — rare+ chests emit a faint audio cue
- **Break everything** — barrels, crates, pots. Some contain Gyldenblood.
- **Corpses of other players are loot piñatas** — one dead geared player = more than a full farm run
- **The Shop resets daily** — check for discounted purple gear
- **A full inventory of greens > an empty inventory and a dream of purples**
