## What Is Mistfall Hunter?

Mistfall Hunter is a dark fantasy PvPvE extraction ARPG. You drop into mist-shrouded ruins, fight Corroded (AI enemies) and rival players, scavenge Gyldenblood (the game's primary loot currency) and gear, and extract before the closing mist kills you. If you die, you lose everything you are carrying.

## The Extraction Loop in 30 Seconds

1. **Gear up** at your camp — choose a class and equip weapons/armor from your stash
2. **Drop in** solo or with up to 2 squadmates
3. **Loot and fight** — kill Corroded for drops, avoid or engage rival players
4. **Find the Returner Woodling** — a mini-boss that drops the Soul of Return
5. **Extract** — channel at an extraction point (~30 seconds, you are vulnerable)
6. **Survive and bank your loot** — everything in your warehouse is permanently safe

## Beginner's Priority Checklist

### 1. Pick the Right Class

For your first few extractions, play **Mercenary** (Sword & Shield). It is:
- The most forgiving class (70% damage reduction on block)
- Easy to learn extraction mechanics without dying constantly
- Effective in both PvE and PvP

Avoid Sorcerer and Shadowstrix until you understand the maps and extraction flow — they die fast if caught.

### 2. Bank Early, Bank Often

Any gear in your **warehouse** is safe forever. After every successful extraction:
- Deposit high-value items immediately
- Equip common/green gear for practice runs
- Save blue/purple gear for when you are confident

### 3. Learn the Map Before Pushing Deep

Spend your first 3-5 extractions just learning:
- Where extraction points are located
- Where the Returner Woodling spawns
- Common player pathing routes
- Safe zones with cover

Map knowledge matters more than gear.

### 4. Know When to Leave

The #1 beginner mistake: **getting greedy**. A successful extraction with modest loot is infinitely better than dying with a bag full of treasure.

- Extracted with 3 items? Win.
- Pushed deeper for one more chest and died? Loss.
- If you hear combat near extraction, wait or find another exit.

### 5. Upgrade Your Camp First

Between extractions, upgrade in this order:
1. **Goddess Statue** — unlocks core progression
2. **Gyldenblod Pool** — healing bonuses
3. **Shop & Warehouse** — more storage, better starting gear
4. **Tavern** — passive buffs

## Common Beginner Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Hurts |
|---------|-------------|
| Hoarding LP/Skill Points | Spending them early makes you stronger now — dead characters can't spend points |
| Ignoring stamina | Purple glow = depleted stamina = free kill for enemies |
| Fighting every player | Sometimes the best PvP is no PvP — extract with your loot |
| Not bringing healing items | Always carry potions and bandages |
| Standing still while looting | Loot fast, keep moving, check your surroundings |

## What to Do in Your First 5 Extractions

1. **Extraction 1**: Pick Mercenary. Find one extraction point. Die. Learn.
2. **Extraction 2**: Find the Returner Woodling. Watch someone else kill it. Learn the fight.
3. **Extraction 3**: Kill the Returner Woodling. Extract with common loot. Success!
4. **Extraction 4**: Bring a green weapon. Extract with green/blue loot.
5. **Extraction 5**: You know the basics. Now read specific class/build guides.

## Key Takeaway

Mistfall Hunter rewards patience and risk management over raw mechanical skill. Play slow, learn the maps, extract often, and the gear will accumulate naturally.
