## Camp Overview

Your camp is your persistent home base between extractions. Upgrading camp facilities unlocks core progression, improves your survivability, and gives you passive advantages in every raid. Spending resources wisely here is just as important as your in-raid decisions.

## Upgrade Priority Order

### 1. Goddess Statue — Unlock Core Progression

**Priority: Build Immediately**

The Goddess Statue is the most important camp facility. It:
- Unlocks your **talent tree** for every class
- Grants **blessing buffs** that persist across extractions
- Enables **class progression** — without it, you cannot unlock new abilities

**Upgrade path**:
- Level 1: Unlock basic talents (first 3 per class)
- Level 2: Unlock advanced talents (cost: 500 Gyldenblood + materials)
- Level 3: Unlock ultimate abilities (cost: 2000 Gyldenblood + rare materials)

Build Level 1 immediately. Level 2 after your first 5 successful extractions. Level 3 is a mid-game goal.

### 2. Gyldenblod Pool — Healing and Regeneration

**Priority: Build After Goddess Statue Level 1**

The Gyldenblod Pool provides:
- **Passive HP regeneration** between raids
- **Increased healing item effectiveness** in raids (+10/20/30% per level)
- **Reduced injury recovery time**

At Level 1, you heal 25% faster between extractions. At Level 3, you start each raid with a small HP regeneration buff.

### 3. Warehouse — Storage Capacity

**Priority: Build After Gyldenblod Pool Level 1**

The Warehouse expands your stash. Base storage is very limited (approximately 20 slots). Each Warehouse level adds 10 slots.

**Why this is important**: More storage = you can keep more gear sets, meaning fewer "budget runs" where you go in undergeared because you had to sell everything.

- Level 1: 30 slots (cost: 300 Gyldenblood)
- Level 2: 40 slots (cost: 800 Gyldenblood)
- Level 3: 50 slots (cost: 1500 Gyldenblood)

### 4. Shop — Better Starting Gear

**Priority: Build After Warehouse Level 1**

The Shop allows you to purchase gear directly instead of relying entirely on raid finds. Each level:
- Unlocks higher rarity items for purchase
- Reduces prices
- Adds rotating daily deals

- Level 1: Common (gray) and Uncommon (green) gear available
- Level 2: Rare (blue) gear available
- Level 3: Epic (purple) gear occasionally available

This is especially important after a series of losses — the Shop lets you re-equip without going in completely naked.

### 5. Tavern — Passive Buffs

**Priority: Build Last**

The Tavern provides:
- **Passive buffs** that apply to all raids (e.g., +5% movement speed, +3% loot quality)
- **Quest board** for bonus objectives with extra rewards
- **NPC companions** who provide lore and occasional item gifts

Build this after your core facilities are at Level 2+. It is nice to have but not essential for early progression.

## Upgrade Cost Reference

| Facility | Level 1 | Level 2 | Level 3 |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|
| Goddess Statue | Free (tutorial) | 500 GB + common mats | 2000 GB + rare mats |
| Gyldenblod Pool | 200 GB | 600 GB | 1500 GB |
| Warehouse | 300 GB | 800 GB | 1500 GB |
| Shop | 250 GB | 700 GB | 1800 GB |
| Tavern | 400 GB | 1000 GB | 2000 GB |

*GB = Gyldenblood. Costs are approximate based on beta data.*

## Resource Management Tips

- **Don't hoard Gyldenblood** — spending it on camp upgrades provides permanent benefits. Unspent Gyldenblood in your warehouse does nothing.
- **Prioritize one facility at a time** — spreading resources across all facilities slows down your power curve
- **After a loss streak, upgrade the Shop** — being able to buy gear prevents the "broke spiral"
- **The Goddess Statue is never a wrong investment** — talents benefit every class you play

## Early Game Upgrade Roadmap

| Milestone | Upgrade |
|-----------|---------|
| After your first extraction | Goddess Statue Level 1 |
| After 5 successful extractions | Gyldenblod Pool Level 1 + Goddess Statue Level 2 |
| After 10 successful extractions | Warehouse Level 1 + Shop Level 1 |
| After 20 successful extractions | All facilities Level 2 |
| Mid-game | Goddess Statue Level 3, then others to Level 3 |
