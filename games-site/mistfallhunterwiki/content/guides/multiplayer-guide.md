## How Multiplayer Works in Mistfall Hunter

Mistfall Hunter is a PvPvE extraction game that supports both **solo play** and **squad-based multiplayer**. You can team up with friends for co-op extraction runs or face off against them in PvP encounters. This guide covers everything you need to know about the multiplayer system.

## How to Invite Friends

### Via the In-Game Social Menu

1. Open the **Social Menu** (default key: `O` or click the friends icon in the top-right corner of the camp screen)
2. Click **"Add Friend"** and enter your friend's in-game username (case-sensitive)
3. Once they accept, right-click their name and select **"Invite to Party"**
4. Your friend receives a notification — they must accept to join your squad

### Via Steam Invites

If you and your friends are on Steam:

1. Open your Steam **Friends List** (Shift+Tab in-game overlay)
2. Right-click your friend's name
3. Select **"Invite to Game"**
4. Your friend will be prompted to join your party directly

Steam invites are the fastest method and work even if you haven't added each other in-game yet.

### Via Friend Code

Each player has a unique **Friend Code** (a 6-digit alphanumeric ID displayed in the Social Menu):

1. Ask your friend for their Friend Code
2. In the Social Menu, click **"Add by Code"**
3. Enter the code and send the request
4. After they accept, invite them to your party as normal

## How to Join a Friend's Party

### Accepting an Invite

When someone sends you a party invite:

- **In-game notification**: A popup appears on your screen — click **Accept** or press `Y`
- **Steam overlay**: Accept via the Steam invite notification
- **If you miss the notification**: Open the Social Menu → click the **Pending Invites** tab → find the invite → click Accept

### Joining Without an Invite

If you want to join a friend who is already in a raid:

- You **cannot** join mid-raid — you must wait until they extract or die
- Once they are back at camp, ask them to re-invite you

## Squad Size and Party Limits

| Mode | Max Players per Squad | Max Teams per Raid |
|------|----------------------|-------------------|
| Solo | 1 | ~8-12 |
| Duo | 2 | ~6-8 |
| Trio | 3 | ~4-6 |

- **Party leader**: The person who creates the party controls the raid settings (map, mode)
- **Spectating**: You can spectate your alive teammates after death
- **No friendly fire**: Damage does not affect your squadmates

## PvE vs PvP Multiplayer

### Cooperative (PvE-Focused)

You and your squad can focus on:

- Killing the **Returner Woodling** for the Soul of Return
- Clearing Corroded camps for Gyldenblood and gear
- Looting high-value chests together
- Extracting as a team

Only **one Soul of Return** is needed for the entire squad to extract. The squad leader should carry it while teammates cover the extraction channel.

### PvP Engagements in Squads

When fighting other teams as a squad:

- **Communication is essential** — call out enemy positions, targets, and cooldowns
- **Focus fire** — all attack the same target for quick kills
- **Role-based positioning** — tanks front, DPS mid, support back

For detailed squad PvP tactics, see the [PvP Guide](/guides/pvp-guide).

## Cross-Play and Platform Support

Mistfall Hunter supports **full cross-play** between PC (Steam) and consoles (when available):

- Cross-play is **enabled by default**
- You can disable cross-play in **Settings → Gameplay → Cross-Play** if you prefer to match only with your platform
- Voice chat works across platforms
- Friend requests and party invites work across platforms via in-game usernames or Friend Codes

## Common Multiplayer Issues and Fixes

### "Invite Not Sending" or "Invite Not Received"

- Both players must be on the **same game version** — check for updates
- Restart the game and try again
- Verify your Steam overlay is enabled (Steam → Settings → In-Game → Enable Steam Overlay)
- Check that neither player has their Social Menu privacy set to "Private"

### "Cannot Join Party" Error

- The squad might already be **full** (max 3 players)
- The party leader's session might be **locked** — ask them to unlock it
- One player might be in the middle of a raid — wait for them to finish
- Try having the other player invite you instead

### "No Voice Chat Audio"

- Check **Audio Settings → Voice Chat Volume** — it may be muted
- Ensure your microphone is selected in **Settings → Voice Chat → Input Device**
- Verify push-to-talk binding is set (default: `V`)
- Cross-platform voice chat requires both players to have voice enabled

### "High Ping / Lag in Party"

- The party leader's region determines the matchmaking server
- If friends are in different regions, try switching the party leader
- You can change your region in **Settings → Account → Region**
- Consider using a different squad composition if lag is persistent

### "Friend List Not Loading"

- Your internet connection may be unstable — check your connection
- The game servers might be under maintenance — check the official Discord or server status page
- Try restarting the game client

## Squad Composition Tips

A balanced squad generally includes:

- **1 Frontline** (Mercenary or Withered Knight) — absorbs damage and controls space
- **1 DPS** (Sorcerer, Blackarrow, or Shadowstrix) — deals damage
- **1 Support** (Seer) — heals and buffs the team

Teams without a Seer can succeed but must play more carefully — bring extra healing consumables and avoid prolonged engagements.

For optimal squad builds, see the [Team Composition Guide](/guides/team-composition-guide) and class-specific build guides.

## Key Takeaway

Mistfall Hunter's multiplayer system is straightforward: invite from the Social Menu or Steam, form a squad of up to 3, and drop in together. The game supports full cross-play, so platform doesn't matter. If you run into connection issues, check game version, restart the client, and switch party leaders to resolve most problems.
