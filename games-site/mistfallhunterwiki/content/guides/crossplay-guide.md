## Crossplay Guide — PC, Console, and Cross-Platform Play

Mistfall Hunter supports full cross-platform play across PC (Steam), PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox Series X|S, and Xbox One. This guide covers how to enable crossplay, how matchmaking works across platforms, performance differences, controller settings, and how to party up with friends on different systems.

---

## Crossplay Status by Platform

| Platform | Crossplay Available | Notes |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| PC (Steam) | Yes — all platforms | Full crossplay with all consoles |
| PlayStation 5 | Yes — all platforms | Crossplay with PC and Xbox |
| PlayStation 4 | Yes — all platforms | Same as PS5, lower performance |
| Xbox Series X|S | Yes — all platforms | Crossplay with PC and PlayStation |
| Xbox One | Yes — all platforms | Same as Series, capped at 30fps |
| Steam Deck | Yes (treated as PC) | Steam Deck users appear as PC players |

### Demo/Beta Crossplay

During the demo and playtest phases, crossplay was restricted to PC only. The full launch build enables crossplay across all listed platforms. If you played the demo on PC, your account and progress carry over to the full crossplay-enabled version.

## How to Enable Crossplay

### PC (Steam)

1. Open **Settings** from the main menu
2. Navigate to **Gameplay** tab
3. Find **Crossplay** toggle
4. Set to **Enabled**

> **Default:** Crossplay is enabled by default on PC. If you experience long queue times, verify crossplay is turned on.

### PlayStation (PS5 / PS4)

1. From the main menu, press **Options** button
2. Select **Settings** > **Online**
3. Toggle **Cross-Platform Play** to **On**
4. **Optional:** Link your platform account for friend list sync

**Note:** PlayStation players need an active PlayStation Plus subscription to play online, including crossplay sessions.

### Xbox (Series X|S / Xbox One)

1. Press the **Menu** button on the main screen
2. Go to **Settings** > **Account & Network**
3. Enable **Cross-Platform Play**
4. Ensure your Xbox privacy settings allow "Cross-network play"

**Note:** Xbox Game Pass Core (formerly Live Gold) is required for online play.

### Steam Deck

Steam Deck runs the PC version and follows the same PC settings. Crossplay is enabled by default. Performance defaults to "Steam Deck" preset, which caps at 40fps with medium-low settings for stable gameplay.

## Matchmaking and Crossplay

### How Matchmaking Works

Mistfall Hunter uses a skill-based matchmaking (SBMM) system that also considers platform connection quality.

| Matchmaking Factor | Weight | Notes |
|--------------------|--------|-------|
| **Skill Rating (MMR)** | High | Based on extraction rate, PvP kills, survival time |
| **Platform** | Medium | Prefers same-platform matches when possible |
| **Connection Quality** | Medium | Prioritizes low-ping lobbies |
| **Party Composition** | High | Matches full parties against other full parties |

### Input-Based Matchmaking

Mistfall Hunter does **not** use input-based matchmaking. Controller and keyboard-and-mouse players are placed in the same lobbies. This is an important consideration if you are concerned about competitive balance.

| Input Method | Advantages | Disadvantages |
|-------------|------------|---------------|
| Keyboard + Mouse | Precise aiming, faster camera control, quick inventory management | Less comfortable for extended sessions |
| Controller (console) | Analog movement, comfortable parry timing with triggers, vibration feedback | Slower camera turns, slower inventory navigation |
| Controller (PC) | Same as console + can mix with mouse for inventory | Must choose input mode at lobby screen |

### Crossplay Party Limits

| Party Size | Supported Across Platforms? | Notes |
|------------|---------------------------|-------|
| Duo (2 players) | Yes | Any platform combination |
| Trio (3 players) | Yes | Any platform combination |
| Solo | Yes | No restrictions |

## Adding Friends Across Platforms

### Using the In-Game Friend System

Mistfall Hunter uses an in-game friend code system that works across all platforms:

1. Open the **Social** menu (default: `O` on PC, touchpad on PlayStation, View button on Xbox)
2. Select **Add Friend**
3. Enter the player's **Friend Code** (found in their Social profile)
4. Send the request

### Friend Code Format

Friend codes are 8-character alphanumeric strings: `XXXXXXXX` (e.g., `MH3A9B2C`).

- Codes are **case-insensitive**
- Codes are **permanent** — they do not change between sessions
- You can find your own code in **Social > Profile > Friend Code**
- Codes are **not** tied to platform accounts — they are Mistfall Hunter-specific

### Voice Chat

Cross-platform voice chat is supported:

| Platform | Voice Chat Method | Notes |
|----------|-------------------|-------|
| PC | In-game voice (push-to-talk) + Discord | Discord is popular for PC groups |
| PlayStation | In-game voice + PlayStation Party | Party chat works during gameplay |
| Xbox | In-game voice + Xbox Party | Party chat works during gameplay |
| Cross-platform | In-game voice only | Party chat is platform-specific |

**Push-to-talk** is available on PC and highly recommended. Open mic can broadcast your position through audio cues.

## Performance Comparison

### Frame Rate and Resolution

| Platform | Target FPS | Resolution | Graphics Preset |
|----------|-----------|------------|-----------------|
| PC (High-end) | 120+ | 1440p-4K | Ultra |
| PC (Mid-range) | 60-90 | 1080p-1440p | High |
| PC (Low-end) | 30-45 | 1080p | Medium |
| PlayStation 5 | 60 (performance) / 30 (quality) | 1440p-4K | High |
| PlayStation 4 | 30 | 1080p | Medium |
| Xbox Series X | 60 (performance) / 30 (quality) | 1440p-4K | High |
| Xbox Series S | 60 | 1080p-1440p | Medium |
| Xbox One | 30 | 1080p | Low |
| Steam Deck | 40 | 800p | Steam Deck preset |

### Competitive Considerations

Console players face some inherent disadvantages against high-refresh-rate PC players:

1. **Aim precision**: Mouse aiming is more precise than stick aiming, especially at range
2. **Reaction time**: 60fps console vs. 120fps+ PC gives PC players a ~8-16ms visual advantage
3. **FOV**: PC players can set a wider FOV (up to 110) while console is capped at 90

**Mitigations for console players:**
- Use the **Aim Assist** setting (Medium strength recommended — High can fight your aim near multiple enemies)
- Set **Controller Sensitivity** to 5-6 (out of 10) for a balance of turning speed and precision
- Enable **Auto-Run** to free your left thumb for camera control while moving
- Consider the **Mercenary** class — its shield playstyle relies less on precise aiming

## Controller Settings for Crossplay

### Recommended Controller Configuration

| Setting | Recommendation | Why |
|---------|---------------|-----|
| Controller Sensitivity | 5 / 10 | Balance of precision and turning speed |
| Aim Assist | Medium | Strong enough for tracking, weak enough to not fight your aim |
| Vibration | Disabled | Reduces distraction in combat; enable for immersion in PvE |
| Button Layout | Default | Well-optimized; no need to rebind |
| Trigger Deadzone | 10% | Prevents accidental parries |
| Stick Deadzone | 10% | Prevents drift without losing responsiveness |
| Invert Y | Personal preference | Not recommended for competitive play |

### Keyboard vs. Controller Strengths

| Task | Better On | Reasoning |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Parry timing | Controller | Trigger buttons have satisfying tactile feedback |
| Sprint management | Keyboard | Shift hold is more reliable than toggle |
| Inventory sorting | Keyboard | Mouse drag-and-drop is faster |
| Ranged combat (Blackarrow) | Keyboard | Mouse precision for headshots |
| Melee combat (Mercenary/Vanguard) | Either | Playstyle preference |
| Quick item usage | Keyboard | Number row hotkeys are faster than D-pad cycling |

## Known Crossplay Issues (Playtest)

The development team has acknowledged these crossplay-specific issues from the playtest phase:

| Issue | Status | Workaround |
|-------|--------|------------|
| Voice chat crackling on cross-platform parties | Investigating | Use platform party chat or Discord |
| Friend requests delayed (up to 5 min) on Xbox-PS cross | Addressed in launch patch | Wait 5 min or restart the Social menu |
| Steam Deck players show as "PC - Unknown" in lobbies | Cosmetic — no gameplay impact | None needed |
| PS4 loading times longer than party members causes late spawn | Investigating | PS4 players should use an SSD |
