## PvP Fundamentals

Mistfall Hunter is PvPvE — you WILL fight other players. Whether you seek fights or avoid them, understanding PvP fundamentals determines whether you extract with loot or die with it.

### The Golden Rule of PvPvE

**The best PvP encounter is the one you choose.** If you are forced into a fight you did not initiate, you are already at a disadvantage. Control your engagements.

## When to Fight vs. When to Avoid

### Fight When
- You have numbers advantage (3v2, 2v1)
- You have gear advantage (purple vs green)
- You initiated (ambush, third-party)
- You must (they are between you and extraction)
- The reward justifies the risk (they clearly have good loot)

### Avoid When
- You are outnumbered
- You are carrying high-value loot (extract instead!)
- You are low on HP/consumables
- You hear multiple teams fighting (let them kill each other)
- You do not know where all enemies are
- The mist is closing and extraction is far

## Third-Partying

Third-partying is the most effective PvP strategy in extraction games.

### How to Third-Party
1. **Hear combat** → identify direction and distance
2. **Approach silently** → no sprinting, no gunfire
3. **Wait for the fight to end** → the winning team is low HP, looting, and distracted
4. **Strike when they are healing/looting** → maximum chaos, minimum resistance
5. **Kill the survivor(s)** → take all the loot from both teams

### Defending Against Third Parties
- After winning a fight: **heal → loot fast → reposition** (in that order)
- Do not linger at the fight location
- Assume someone heard your gunfire and is approaching
- The looting screen is when you are most vulnerable

## Team Fight Tactics

### Focus Fire
The most basic and most important team principle: **everyone attacks the same target.** Call your target: "Sorcerer low — finish Sorcerer."

### Target Priority (General)
1. **Seer** — kill the healer first and the team crumbles
2. **Sorcerer** — highest DPS, must die before they delete your team
3. **Shadowstrix** — if visible, they are vulnerable
4. **Blackarrow** — dangerous if left alone at range
5. **Withered Knight** — parry makes them hard to burst
6. **Mercenary** — kill last, they take forever

### Positioning
- **Frontline**: Mercenary or Withered Knight — between your team and the enemy
- **Midline**: Sorcerer or Blackarrow — damage from safety
- **Backline**: Seer — invisible, healing, CC
- **Flank**: Shadowstrix — circling for an ambush angle

### Communication
Good comms win fights:
- "Mercenary pushing left side"
- "Sorcerer low — half HP"
- "Shadowstrix stealth — watch backline"
- "I'm dead — two low, one full"
- "Third party from north — disengage!"

## Class-Specific PvP Tips

### Mercenary
- You are the anchor. Do not chase kills — protect your backline.
- Shield Dash the enemy Sorcerer to disrupt their casting.
- Your block can tank for teammates. Stand between the enemy and your DPS.

### Sorcerer
- Position like you have 1 HP. Because you basically do.
- Glacial Blink is your lifeline — save it for Shadowstrix ambush.
- Elemental cycle full before engaging. Do not start a fight without Resonance.

### Shadowstrix
- Your job: kill the Seer or Sorcerer. That is it. Do not trade with the Mercenary.
- Stealth → scout → call enemy positions to your team.
- After a kill: re-stealth immediately. Do not loot until the fight is over.

### Withered Knight
- Counter the enemy frontline. Parry their opener → burst them.
- Thorn Guide the enemy Sorcerer or Blackarrow to pull them out of position.
- Your Reckoning AoE can hit multiple enemies — group fights are your strength.

### Blackarrow
- Stay at max range. If you can see their faces clearly, you are too close.
- Tracking Mark the highest-priority target for your team.
- If anyone gets close, Ice Bomb → reposition → do not fight in melee.

### Seer
- You are the quarterback. Call targets, call cooldowns, call disengages.
- Bond your DPS. Heal your tank. CC divers.
- If you die first, your team loses. Position accordingly.

## Post-Fight Protocol

1. **Heal immediately** — do not loot first
2. **Reload/recharge** — all abilities ready before looting
3. **One person loots, others watch** — rotate who loots
4. **Take only high-value items** — Gyldenblood, purple/orange gear, quest items
5. **Leave within 30 seconds** — third parties are already on their way
6. **Do not celebrate** — the fight is not over until you extract

## Quick PvP Tips

- **Audio is information** — gunshots, spells, footsteps all tell you where players are
- **Purple glow = depleted stamina = free kill** — watch for it on enemies, prevent it on yourself
- **The element of surprise is worth a gear tier** — a green ambush beats a purple caught off-guard
- **Loser's bracket exists** — after multiple deaths, you face easier lobbies. Use it to rebuild.
- **Record your deaths** — watch what you could have done differently
