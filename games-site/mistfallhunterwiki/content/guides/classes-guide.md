## Class Overview

Mistfall Hunter launches with 6 classes, each with unique weapons, abilities, and playstyles. You can switch classes between extractions from your camp.

| Class | Role | Weapon | Difficulty | Best For |
|-------|------|--------|------------|----------|
| **Mercenary** | Frontline tank | Sword & Shield | Easy | Beginners, PvE, survivability |
| **Sorcerer** | Ranged DPS | Elemental Focus | Medium | Team fights, AoE damage |
| **Blackarrow** | Precision DPS | Longbow | Hard | PvE, open sightlines |
| **Shadowstrix** | Stealth assassin | Dual Daggers | Hard | Solo PvP, ambush |
| **Seer** | Support/Healer | Lantern & Catalyst | Medium | Organized squads |
| **Withered Knight** | Counter-fighter | Greatsword | Medium | Frontline disruption |

## 1. Mercenary — Best for Beginners

**Play if**: You are new to extraction games, want survivability, or prefer tanky melee.

**Core mechanic**: Block attacks to build defensive value, then Shield Dash to stun enemies, follow up with punishing sword combos. Sword Tip stacking into Blade Edge deals high burst damage.

**Pros**: 70% damage reduction on block, forgiving learning curve, strong in both PvE and PvP
**Cons**: Lower mobility, predictable playstyle at high levels

**Key talents**: Resilient Body (builds Will while blocking), Rising to the Challenge (Shield Dash stuns), Stone Breaker Slash (punish aggressive enemies)

## 2. Sorcerer — Highest Damage Ceiling

**Play if**: You want to deal massive damage from range and control the battlefield.

**Core mechanic**: Cycle between Fire/Ice/Thunder elements for damage reactions and crowd control. Stardust spec offers instant casts and burst damage. Elemental Resonance talent boosts damage when cycling elements.

**Pros**: Highest AoE damage, strong in team fights, versatile range
**Cons**: Extremely fragile when caught, positioning-dependent

**Key skills**: Flame Tempest (AoE fire storm), Glacial Blink (teleport + ice slow)

## 3. Blackarrow — Precision Marksman

**Play if**: You have good aim and prefer keeping distance.

**Core mechanic**: Charged precision shots with explosive arrows (Archer spec) or damage-over-time with crowd control (Hunter spec). Ice bomb stun into charged shot is the bread-and-butter combo.

**Pros**: Devastating at long range, strong in PvE boss fights
**Cons**: Miss a shot and your energy loop breaks, difficult in close-range PvP

## 4. Shadowstrix — Solo PvP King

**Play if**: You enjoy stealth, ambush tactics, and solo play.

**Core mechanic**: Open from stealth with massive ambush damage, execute burst combo, re-stealth or disengage. Dagger spec for spike damage, Dual-Blades for wound stacking and detonation.

**Pros**: Best solo PvP class, high skill expression, can 1v1 any class
**Cons**: Fragile, punished hard for failed ambushes, weak in extended team fights

**Key skills**: Viper Strike (poison DoT), Veil of Shadows (invisibility)

## 5. Withered Knight — Parry God

**Play if**: You enjoy counter-play, timing-based combat, and mid-range pressure.

**Core mechanic**: Apply Withering Sigils on enemies, then detonate with Reckoning for massive burst and CC. Thorn Guide (grapple) pulls enemies close. Timed parry into counter-attack.

**Pros**: Huge burst potential, strong crowd control, satisfying skill expression
**Cons**: Timing-dependent, punished for missed parries

## 6. Seer — Team Support

**Play if**: You play in a coordinated squad and enjoy enabling teammates.

**Core mechanic**: Heal, shield, and buff allies with Astral Bond (shared damage + HoT) and Celestial Tether (root enemies). Provides utility that no other class offers.

**Pros**: Essential in organized squads, can turn fights with well-timed heals
**Cons**: Weak solo, dependent on teammates, low personal damage

## Which Class Should You Pick?

| Your Goal | Pick |
|-----------|------|
| Learn the game | Mercenary |
| Deal the most damage | Sorcerer |
| Win solo PvP | Shadowstrix |
| Support a squad | Seer |
| High skill ceiling | Blackarrow |
| Counter-play style | Withered Knight |

For most players, **start with Mercenary** to learn extraction mechanics, then branch into other classes once you understand maps and flow.
