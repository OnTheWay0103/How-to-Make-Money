## Why Play Shadowstrix?

Shadowstrix is the **best solo PvP class** in Mistfall Hunter. If you enjoy playing alone, picking your fights, and outplaying opponents with stealth and burst damage, this is your class. It offers:
- Unmatched solo kill potential
- Stealth for picking (and avoiding) fights
- High skill expression — the best Shadowstrix players control entire lobbies
- Two viable weapon specs: Daggers (burst) and Dual-Blades (DoT pressure)

## Core Mechanics

### Stealth and Ambush

Shadowstrix revolves around **stealth**. Key mechanics:
- **Veil of Shadows** — enter invisibility for 8 seconds. First attack from stealth deals +60% damage (Dagger) or applies max wound stacks (Dual-Blades).
- **Ambush bonus** — breaking stealth with an attack grants bonus damage and briefly stuns the target (0.5s).
- **Re-stealth** — after a kill, Veil of Shadows cooldown is partially reset.

The loop: **Stealth → Find target → Ambush from stealth → Burst combo → Kill → Re-stealth → Repeat**

### Dagger Spec — Burst Assassin

Focuses on maximum single-target spike damage:
- Break stealth with a Dagger strike for massive ambush damage
- Follow with **Viper Strike** (poison DoT) to prevent healing
- Execute with **Shadow Rend** (bonus damage to poisoned targets)

Best for: killing one target fast, then repositioning or extracting.

### Dual-Blades Spec — Wound Stacker

Focuses on sustained pressure through wound mechanics:
- Each attack applies a **Wound** stack (up to 5)
- **Rupture** detonates all wounds for escalating damage per stack
- Longer kill time but more consistent pressure

Best for: extended fights, fighting multiple enemies, PvE bosses.

## Build Variants

### Solo PvP Assassin (Dagger Spec)

- **Talents**: Assassin's Mark → Deathblow → Shadow Dance → Venomous Blades → Cloak of Shadows
- **Combo**: Stealth → Ambush (stun) → Viper Strike → Shadow Rend → Kill confirmed → Stealth again
- **Affixes**: Agility, Armor Penetration, Critical Damage
- **Playstyle**: Hunt isolated players. Never take fair fights. Kill and move.

### PvE Boss Killer (Dual-Blades Spec)

- **Talents**: Deep Wounds → Hemorrhage → Blood Trail → Exsanguinate
- **Combo**: Stealth → Ambush (max wound stacks) → Rupture → Build wounds again → Rupture
- **Affixes**: Agility, Armor Penetration, Lifesteal
- **Playstyle**: Stack wounds, detonate at 7 stacks. Use lifesteal to sustain through boss mechanics.

## PvP Matchups

| Enemy Class | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|
| **Sorcerer** | Your best matchup. Stealth → ambush → they die before reacting. If they Glacial Blink away, Shadow Step to follow. |
| **Mercenary** | Wait for them to use Shield Dash offensively. Attack while it is on cooldown. Never ambush into their block — you lose the trade. |
| **Blackarrow** | Close the gap. Use terrain to block line of sight. Once in melee range, they cannot fight back effectively. |
| **Withered Knight** | Bait the parry. Feint an attack. If they parry air, you have a 2-second window to burst. |
| **Seer** | Free kill. They have no escape and no damage. Just do not get baited into their team. |
| **Shadowstrix (mirror)** | Whoever sees the other first wins. Use audio cues. Listen for stealth sounds. |

## Solo Extraction Strategy

Shadowstrix excels at solo extraction because you control engagements:

1. **Spawn** → immediately stealth and move toward the Returner Woodling zone
2. **Scout the Woodling** from stealth — is anyone fighting it? Are there corpses nearby?
3. **Decision point**:
   - Woodling unguarded → kill it, take Soul, extract
   - Players fighting it → wait in stealth, kill the survivor, take both their loot
   - Too many players → find an alternative exit or wait for the chaos
4. **After securing the Soul** → stealth immediately. Do not walk openly to extraction.
5. **At extraction** → scout from stealth. If clear, extract. If occupied, wait or find another exit.

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Ambushing the first player you see | Pick your targets. Ambushing a full-health Mercenary with cooldowns up is a waste. |
| Staying too long after a kill | Loot fast, stealth, and leave. Lingering attracts third parties. |
| Using stealth only offensively | Stealth is also your best escape tool. Save it if you might need to run. |
| Fighting fair 1v1s | You are an assassin, not a duelist. Ambush or avoid. |
| Forgetting to re-stealth between kills | Shadow Dance resets cooldown. Chain kills without breaking stealth uptime. |
