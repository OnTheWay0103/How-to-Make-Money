## Weapon Overview

Each Mistfall Hunter class uses a specific weapon type. Understanding your weapon's mechanics, strengths, and limitations is essential for success.

| Weapon | Class | Damage Type | Range | Attack Speed |
|--------|-------|------------|-------|-------------|
| Sword & Shield | Mercenary | Physical (Slash/Pierce) | Melee | Medium |
| Elemental Focus | Sorcerer | Elemental (Fire/Ice/Thunder) | Long | Variable |
| Longbow | Blackarrow | Physical (Pierce) | Very Long | Slow |
| Dual Daggers | Shadowstrix | Physical (Slash/Pierce) | Melee | Very Fast |
| Greatsword | Withered Knight | Physical (Slash) | Melee (Extended) | Slow |
| Lantern & Catalyst | Seer | Spirit (Heal/Buff) | Medium | Medium |

## Weapon Rarity System

| Rarity | Color | Relative Power | When to Use |
|--------|-------|---------------|-------------|
| Common | Gray | Baseline | First extractions, budget runs |
| Uncommon | Green | +15% stats | Practice runs, learning new maps |
| Rare | Blue | +30% stats | Standard loadouts |
| Epic | Purple | +50% stats + 1 extra affix | Competitive play |
| Legendary | Orange | +75% stats + 2 extra affixes | End-game, high-stakes raids |

**Rule of thumb**: A blue weapon with perfect affixes > a purple weapon with bad affixes.

## Sword & Shield (Mercenary)

### Mechanics
- **Block**: Hold to reduce incoming damage by 70%. Costs stamina.
- **Shield Dash**: Gap-closer that stuns with Rising to the Challenge talent.
- **Sword Tip stacking**: Consecutive hits build stacks. Max stacks → Blade Edge (bonus damage).

### Best Affixes
1. **Bulwark** — +Block strength
2. **Valor** — +Damage after blocking
3. **Fortitude** — +Max HP

### Upgrade Priority
Shield rarity matters more than sword rarity. A purple shield with Bulwark beats a legendary sword.

## Elemental Focus (Sorcerer)

### Mechanics
- **Elemental cycling**: Fire → Ice → Thunder for damage reactions
- **Charge time**: Some spells require charging; others are instant
- **No physical attacks**: Pure magical damage; ignores armor

### Best Affixes
1. **Spell Power** — Flat damage increase
2. **Critical Strike Chance** — High base crit multipliers
3. **Cooldown Reduction** — More spells per fight

### Upgrade Priority
Spell Power on weapon is the single most impactful stat for Sorcerer. Priority: Focus > Armor in all cases.

## Longbow (Blackarrow)

### Mechanics
- **Charge shots**: Hold to charge for increased damage and accuracy
- **Ammo types**: Standard, Explosive, Ice (from skills)
- **Damage falloff**: Arrows deal reduced damage at very close range

### Best Affixes
1. **Precision** — +Charge speed and accuracy
2. **Critical Damage** — Headshots are guaranteed crits
3. **Projectile Speed** — Harder for enemies to dodge

### Upgrade Priority
The bow's base damage is the most important stat. Even a green bow with high base damage outperforms a blue bow with low base damage.

## Dual Daggers / Dual-Blades (Shadowstrix)

### Mechanics
- **Daggers**: Fast attacks, ambush bonus damage from stealth
- **Dual-Blades**: Wound stacking (up to 5/7 stacks) → detonate for burst
- **Dual wield**: Hits twice per attack animation

### Best Affixes
1. **Agility** — +Attack speed and movement speed
2. **Armor Penetration** — Bypasses enemy defenses
3. **Critical Damage** — Ambush guarantees crit

### Upgrade Priority
Attack speed is king — more hits = more DPS and faster wound stacking. Agility is the #1 affix.

## Greatsword (Withered Knight)

### Mechanics
- **Slow, heavy hits**: Each swing deals high damage but has long recovery
- **Withering Sigils**: Apply on hit, detonate with Reckoning for burst
- **Parry**: Timed block that negates damage and opens counter-attack window

### Best Affixes
1. **Physical Might** — +Raw damage
2. **Armor Rating** — +Physical damage reduction
3. **Impact** — +Stagger on hit (interrupts enemy actions)

### Upgrade Priority
Base damage is everything for Greatsword. One good hit > three weak hits. Prioritize the highest damage-per-swing available.

## Lantern & Catalyst (Seer)

### Mechanics
- **Lantern**: Casts healing, shielding, and buff spells
- **Catalyst**: Casts debuff and crowd control spells
- **Shared resource pool**: Both use Spirit Power

### Best Affixes
1. **Cooldown Reduction** — More heals and buffs per fight
2. **Spirit Power** — +Heal and shield strength
3. **Cast Speed** — Faster spell delivery in clutch moments

### Upgrade Priority
Cooldown Reduction is the single most important stat. The difference between a 10s and 8s heal cooldown wins fights.

## Weapon Comparison: Which is Best?

| Criteria | Best Weapon | Why |
|----------|------------|-----|
| Highest single-hit damage | Greatsword | Slow but devastating |
| Highest DPS | Dual Daggers | Attack speed + wound detonation |
| Best for beginners | Sword & Shield | Block gives survivability |
| Best for solo PvP | Dual Daggers | Stealth + ambush burst |
| Best for team fights | Elemental Focus | AoE damage + CC |
| Best for PvE bosses | Longbow | Safe range + precision damage |
| Most versatile | Sword & Shield | Effective in all situations |

## General Gear Tips

- **Affixes > Rarity**: A green item with your 2 best affixes beats a blue item with useless affixes
- **Don't hoard**: If you are not using a piece of gear and it is not part of a specific build, sell it
- **Upgrade your weapon first**: Weapon upgrades provide the biggest power spike per Gyldenblood spent
- **Keep a budget set**: Always have a cheap green/blue loadout ready for practice or recovery runs
- **Check the Shop daily**: Rotating deals sometimes offer purple gear at steep discounts
