## Why Play Seer?

Seer is the **only support class** in Mistfall Hunter. In a coordinated 3-player squad with voice comms, a good Seer turns an average team into an unstoppable force. However, Seer is nearly unplayable solo — you have no escape, no burst damage, and no survivability tools for 1v1.

**Play Seer only if you have a regular squad.** Do not queue solo as Seer.

## Core Mechanics

### Astral Bond

Your primary ability. Tether to an ally:
- **Damage distribution**: 30% of damage your ally takes is redirected to you
- **Heal over Time**: Tethered ally receives constant HP regeneration
- **Range**: Medium (breaks if ally moves too far)

### Celestial Tether

Your crowd control. Root enemies in place:
- 2-second root on cast
- Can chain to nearby enemies (up to 3)
- Long cooldown — use strategically

### Lantern & Catalyst

- **Lantern**: Heals, shields, buffs — your primary tool
- **Catalyst**: Debuffs and CC — secondary, situational

## Best Talents

| Talent | Effect | Priority |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **Empowered Bond** | Astral Bond healing +40% | Must-have |
| **Shared Vigor** | Astral Bond also grants 10% damage reduction | Must-have |

| **Spirit Walk** | Gain 3s invisibility + movement speed after using Celestial Tether | PvP only |

## Healing Rotation

### Standard Engage
1. Astral Bond your DPS (Sorcerer or Shadowstrix)
2. Stay behind your frontline (Mercenary or Withered Knight)
3. Spam heals on cooldown
4. Celestial Tether any enemy that dives your backline
5. Re-apply Astral Bond if it breaks

### Emergency Rotation (Ally Dying)
1. Martyr's Resolve procs automatically — you now take 100% damage
2. Pop all healing items
3. Celestial Tether the attacker to root them
4. Call for your team to peel
5. Re-apply Astral Bond once safe

### Offensive Rotation (Your Team is Winning)
1. Astral Bond your DPS
2. Celestial Tether a priority target (enemy Seer or Sorcerer)
3. Use Catalyst debuffs on tethered enemies
4. Keep healing your team — winning fights still cost HP

## Squad Composition

### Best 3-Stack with Seer

| Role | Class | Why |
|------|-------|-----|
| Tank/Frontline | Mercenary | Soaks damage, you keep them alive forever |
| DPS | Sorcerer | Astral Bond lets them play aggressively |
| Support | Seer (you) | Heals, CC, damage distribution |

### How to Play with Each Class

| Teammate Class | Your Job |
|---------------|----------|
| **Mercenary** | Bond them. They are now unkillable. Focus heals on them. |
| **Sorcerer** | Bond them in team fights. They die fast if focused — Martyr's Resolve saves them. |
| **Shadowstrix** | Bond them before they engage. Break bond when they stealth (it reveals them). |
| **Withered Knight** | Bond them. Their parry timing + your healing = infinite sustain. |
| **Blackarrow** | Bond them. Celestial Tether anyone who closes on them. |

## PvP Matchups

| Enemy Class | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|
| **Anyone** | You cannot win 1v1. Do not try. Stay with your team. |
| **Shadowstrix diving you** | Celestial Tether → Spirit Walk away → call for peel |
| **Sorcerer focusing you** | Break line of sight. Your healing outpaces their poke if you use cover. |
| **Mercenary on you** | You are dead if your team does not help. Call it early. |

## What NOT to Do

| Don't | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Queue solo | You will die. You will not extract. You will hate this class. |
| Bond the wrong ally | Bond your DPS, not your tank. Your tank survives without you. |
| Waste Celestial Tether | Long cooldown. Save it for someone diving your backline. |
| Stand in the open | You are the #1 target for every enemy team. Use cover. |
| Forget to heal yourself | Martyr's Resolve redirects damage to you. If you die, your team dies. |
| Play without voice comms | Seer requires coordination. Randoms will not play around your abilities. |

## Quick Tips

- **You are the most important target on your team** — act like it. Position safely.
- **Your K/D does not matter** — your team's extraction rate does.
- **Call your cooldowns** — "bond up", "tether in 5", "I'm being dived"
- **The best Seer is invisible** — the enemy team should not know where you are
- **Accept that you will die sometimes** — a Martyr's Resolve that saves your DPS wins the fight, even if you go down
