## Why Team Composition Matters

In Mistfall Hunter, a coordinated squad can extract with 3x the loot of a solo player — but a badly composed team is worse than going solo. The right composition covers weaknesses, amplifies strengths, and gives you the upper hand in PvP encounters.

### Team Size Options

| Size | Best For | Challenges |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Solo | Stealth, speed, flexible pathing | No backup, full loot loss on death |
| Duo (2) | Fast extractions, split looting | Vulnerable to 3-player squads |
| Trio (3) | Max loot potential, boss farming | Slower movement, harder to hide |

## Duo Compositions (Best to Viable)

### S-Tier: Mercenary + Sorcerer

| Role | Player | Responsibilities |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| Frontline | Mercenary | Tank damage, control space, initiate fights |
| Backline | Sorcerer | AoE damage, clear adds, burst from range |

**Why it works**: The Mercenary creates space for the Sorcerer to free-cast. In PvP, the Mercenary blocks pushes while the Sorcerer AoEs grouped enemies. This is the most forgiving duo composition.

**Synergy talents**:
- Mercenary: Rising to the Challenge (Shield Dash stuns), providing easy targets for Sorcerer
- Sorcerer: Elemental Resonance (bonus damage on cycled targets)

**Weakness**: Both are weak to ambush. If the Mercenary gets caught off-guard, the Sorcerer dies shortly after.

### S-Tier: Shadowstrix + Withered Knight

| Role | Player | Responsibilities |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| Scout/Assassin | Shadowstrix | Flank, scout ahead, assassinate isolated targets |
| Follow-up | Withered Knight | Parry pressure, grapple follow-up, clean up |

**Why it works**: Shadowstrix scouts and softens targets. Withered Knight grapples fleeing enemies and provides the burst to confirm kills. This duo excels at ambushing other teams.

**Synergy talents**:
- Shadowstrix: Veil of Shadows (invisibility for scouting)
- Withered Knight: Thorn Guide (grapple) + Reckoning (burst detonation)

**Weakness**: Low sustain. If the first ambush fails, both classes struggle in extended fights.

### A-Tier: Blackarrow + Seer

| Role | Player | Responsibilities |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| DPS | Blackarrow | Ranged damage, zone control, boss DPS |
| Support | Seer | Healing, shielding, debuff cleansing, vision |

**Why it works**: The Seer keeps the Blackarrow alive through chip damage while the Blackarrow controls space with explosive arrows. Excellent for PvE boss farming — the safest duo for high-value loot.

**Synergy talents**:
- Blackarrow: Hunter spec (DoT + CC for prolonged fights)
- Seer: Astral Bond (shared damage reduction + HoT)

**Weakness**: Struggles against aggressive melee duos (Shadowstrix + Withered Knight). If a melee duo closes the gap, the Blackarrow cannot escape.

## Trio Compositions (Best to Viable)

### S-Tier: Mercenary + Sorcerer + Seer (The Trinity)

| Role | Player | Responsibilities |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| Tank | Mercenary | Engage, peel, absorb damage |
| DPS | Sorcerer | AoE damage, burst, add clear |
| Support | Seer | Heal, shield, cleanse, scry |

**Why it works**: The classic tank/DPS/support trinity covers every scenario. The Mercenary peels for both backliners. The Sorcerer outputs massive damage. The Seer keeps everyone alive through attrition.

**Teamfight strategy**:
1. Mercenary Shield Dashes into the enemy team
2. Sorcerer drops Flame Tempest on the engaged targets
3. Seer tethers the Mercenary (Astral Bond for shared damage reduction)
4. Enemy either burns to death or scatters — Sorcerer picks off runners

**Weakness**: Predictable positioning. Experienced enemies will try to flank the Seer.

### S-Tier: Shadowstrix + Withered Knight + Sorcerer (Execute Squad)

| Role | Player | Responsibilities |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| Scout/Initiator | Shadowstrix | Mark targets, soften, create chaos |
| Burst/CC | Withered Knight | Grapple isolated targets, detonate sigils |
| Clean-up | Sorcerer | AoE the pile, finish fleeing targets |

**Why it works**: This composition deletes single targets in under 3 seconds. Shadowstrix ambushes and applies pressure. Withered Knight pulls an exposed target. Sorcerer AoEs the area where the target lands.

**Teamfight strategy**:
1. Shadowstrix scouts and marks the enemy healer/carrier
2. Withered Knight grapples the marked target
3. Sorcerer drops instant-cast burst on the grappled target
4. 3v2 cleanup

**Weakness**: Fragile. If the initial burst doesn't kill a target, the team has no defensive options.

### A-Tier: Blackarrow + Mercenary + Seer (Siege Comp)

| Role | Player | Responsibilities |
|------|--------|-----------------|
| Zone DPS | Blackarrow | Long-range control, boss pressure |
| Bodyguard | Mercenary | Protect Seer and Blackarrow |
| Flex Support | Seer | Heal, cleanse, vision control |

**Why it works**: This comp controls engagement distance. The Blackarrow dictates where enemies can walk. The Mercenary bodyguards against divers. The Seer sustains through poke damage.

**Teamfight strategy**:
1. Blackarrow creates a danger zone with explosive arrows
2. Enemies must either stay back (losing map control) or push through (taking free damage)
3. If they push, Mercenary intercepts. If they stay back, Blackarrow picks them off.
4. Seer heals through poke wars.

**Weakness**: Low burst damage. Struggles against teams that can engage from multiple angles.

## Role Assignment Framework

Every team needs these three roles filled:

### 1. Initiator (Frontline)
- **What they do**: Start fights, scout, absorb first hit
- **Best classes**: Mercenary, Withered Knight
- **Secondary**: Shadowstrix (for ambush initiations only)

### 2. Damage (DPS)
- **What they do**: Kill things. Preferably quickly.
- **Best classes**: Sorcerer, Blackarrow, Shadowstrix
- **Secondary**: Withered Knight (burst damage role)

### 3. Sustain (Support/Control)
- **What they do**: Heal, peel, control space
- **Best classes**: Seer, Mercenary (peel support build)
- **Secondary**: Sorcerer (zone control with ice spells)

## Communication Tips

### Pre-Match
- **Call your route**: "I'm going north to loot, then rotating east"
- **Set extraction expectations**: "We extract by 12-minute mark"
- **Assign roles**: "I'll initiate. You follow up. You watch our back."

### During Combat
- **Target call**: "Focus the Sorcerer. One. Sorcerer."
- **Warning call**: "Third party incoming from south"
- **Retreat call**: "Reset. Back to the tunnel. Now."
- **Ability call**: "I'm stunning the tank in 3... 2... 1..."

### Post-Combat
- **Loot call**: "Who needs this ring?" — Share gear immediately
- **Next move**: "Clear rest of compound. Extraction north."
- **Heal check**: "I'm low on potions. Let's find a safe spot."

## Common Team Mistakes

| Mistake | Impact | Fix |
|---------|--------|-----|
| Splitting up without comms | One player gets 3v1'd | Stay in visual range |
| Both DPS fighting the same target | Wasted overkill damage | Communicate focus targets |
| Ignoring third parties | Wiped while looting | One player always watches the perimeter |
| Not sharing loot | Team with 3 decent kits > one player with a god kit | Share upgrade-relevant gear |
| Playing too slow | Mist closes, no extraction point left | Set a timer — 10 minutes max per map |

## When to Change Your Composition

Consider swapping your team composition when:

- **You lose 3 extractions in a row to the same comp** — your composition might be countered
- **Your team is all playing the same role** — triple DPS means no one peels
- **You're farming bosses** — bring a Seer and a tank
- **You're hunting players** — bring Shadowstrix and Withered Knight for burst
- **You have only 2 players** — avoid Sorcerer + Blackarrow (both fragile)
