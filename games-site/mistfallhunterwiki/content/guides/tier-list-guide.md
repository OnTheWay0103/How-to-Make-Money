## Tier List Methodology

This tier list is based on open beta performance data, community feedback, and competitive analysis. Rankings reflect:
- **Overall power** across PvE and PvP
- **Solo viability** (can you extract alone?)
- **Squad contribution** (do you make a team better?)
- **Skill floor/ceiling** (how hard to play vs. maximum potential)

**Important**: Mistfall Hunter launches July 29, 2026. This is pre-launch data. Balance patches will shift rankings.

## Overall Tier List

### S-Tier: Dominant

| Class | Why S-Tier |
|-------|-----------|
| **Sorcerer** | Highest damage output in the game. Elemental cycling deletes both PvE and PvP targets. Glacial Blink provides the escape tool that covers its only weakness (fragility). |
| **Shadowstrix** | Uncontested king of solo PvP. Ambush into burst combo kills most classes before they can react. Stealth re-engage allows hit-and-run play that no other class matches. |

### A-Tier: Strong and Consistent

| Class | Why A-Tier |
|-------|-----------|
| **Mercenary** | The most consistent class. 70% block DR makes it the tankiest option. Simple to play, hard to kill. Falls slightly behind S-tiers in damage output but makes up for it in reliability. |

### B-Tier: Viable with Practice

| Class | Why B-Tier |
|-------|-----------|
| **Withered Knight** | Devastating burst when sigil detonation combos land. Grapple is excellent for gap-closing. Held back by parry timing dependency — one missed parry can mean death. |

### C-Tier: Situational

| Class | Why C-Tier |
|-------|-----------|
| **Blackarrow** | Exceptional in PvE and open maps. Falls off hard in close-quarters PvP. Missing shots breaks the energy loop. Strong in the right hands but inconsistent. |

### D-Tier: Team-Dependent

| Class | Why D-Tier |
|-------|-----------|
| **Seer** | Fantastic in a coordinated 3-stack with comms. Healing and shielding can turn fights. But in solo queue or random squads, the lack of personal damage and self-peel makes Seer a liability. |

## Solo Play Tier List

For players queuing alone into PvPvE extraction zones:

| Tier | Classes | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| **S** | Shadowstrix | Stealth lets you pick fights on your terms. Ambush → kill → extract. |
| **A** | Mercenary, Sorcerer | Mercenary survives anything. Sorcerer kills everything before it reaches you. |
| **B** | Withered Knight, Blackarrow | Viable but harder to execute solo. |
| **C** | Seer | Do not play Seer solo. Seriously. |

## Squad Play Tier List

For coordinated 3-player squads:

| Tier | Classes | Best Team Comp |
|------|---------|---------------|
| **S** | Sorcerer, Seer, Shadowstrix | Sorcerer AoE + Seer support + Shadowstrix flank |
| **A** | Mercenary, Withered Knight | Mercenary frontlines, Withered Knight counters |
| **B** | Blackarrow | Strong PvE addition, weaker in team PvP |

**Best 3-stack comp**: Mercenary (tank/frontline) + Sorcerer (DPS) + Seer (support). Balanced, forgiving, effective at all ranges.

## Beginner Tier List

| Tier | Classes | Why |
|------|---------|-----|
| **Best** | Mercenary | Forgiving, tanky, simple combos. Learn extraction without dying constantly. |
| **Good** | Sorcerer | Ranged play is safer. Just stay far away and blast. |
| **Avoid** | Shadowstrix, Withered Knight, Blackarrow, Seer | These classes require game knowledge, map awareness, or team coordination that beginners lack. |

## Key Takeaway

**Tier lists are guidelines, not gospel.** Every class can extract successfully. Pick the class that matches your playstyle. A C-tier class you master will outperform an S-tier class you play poorly.
