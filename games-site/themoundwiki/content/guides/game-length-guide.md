# The Mound Game Length Guide — How Long to Beat & Content Overview

> Launch Version / Updated: July 14, 2026

**The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu** launched on July 15, 2026 with a substantial amount of content. This guide breaks down exactly how long you can expect to spend in the cursed forests of the New World, covering the main campaign, expedition durations, demo scope, and what to expect from post-launch updates.

---

## Main Campaign Length

The main story content of The Mound provides approximately **15-20 hours** of playtime for a typical first playthrough.

| Play Style | Estimated Time |
|------------|---------------|
| Story-focused (normal difficulty, co-op) | 15-20 hours |
| Completionist (all contracts, optional objectives) | 25-30 hours |
| Speed-focused (experienced players) | 12-15 hours |
| Solo playthrough | 20-25 hours |

### Factors That Affect Campaign Length

- **Team size:** Solo play is slower due to increased caution and fewer revive opportunities.
- **Difficulty:** Higher difficulty contracts take longer due to enemy density and objective complexity.
- **Exploration:** The jungle maps contain hidden areas, logbook entries, and optional objectives that add hours of content.
- **Deaths:** Each expedition failure means restarting from the deployment screen, losing any loot not yet extracted.

---

## Expedition Length Breakdown

Expedition length varies by contract type and play style. A single expedition lasts roughly **20 minutes**, but actual duration depends on the contract selected.

| Contract Type | Typical Duration |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Easy Salvage / Recon | 15-25 minutes |
| Medium Contract | 25-40 minutes |
| Hard Contract | 35-60 minutes |
| Story Contract | 30-60 minutes |
| Failed / Early Extraction | 5-15 minutes |

Most players find that a full successful expedition takes **30-45 minutes** on average. The hidden "forest awakening" timer adds pressure — the longer you stay, the more dangerous the environment becomes, encouraging efficient play.

## Free Steam Demo

A free demo is available on Steam for players who want to try the game before purchasing.

### Demo Scope

- **Limited expeditions:** The demo includes a curated selection of early-game contracts.
- **Session length:** A typical demo session lasts **20-30 minutes**.
- **Content cap:** The demo ends after completing a set number of expeditions.
- **Progress carry-over:** **No.** Demo progress does not carry over to the full game. This allows players to explore freely without affecting their final save.

### What the Demo Covers

- Tutorial expedition (guided)
- 2-3 standard Recon contracts
- Introduction to the Sanity system
- Basic weapon handling (matchlock, crossbow, melee)
- Solo play with AI companions

## Replayability

The Mound offers several systems that encourage repeated playthroughs:

- **Contract variety:** Multiple contract types with randomized enemy patrols and loot placement.
- **Rank system:** Individual player rank unlocks new tools, weapons, and cosmetic items.
- **Logbook collection:** Scattered entries reveal lore and backstory about the New World.
- **Difficulty scaling:** Higher difficulty contracts offer better rewards and new challenges.
- **Team composition:** Different loadouts and team strategies create varied experiences.
