# The Mound Extraction Guide — How to Get Out Alive

> Launch Version / Updated: July 14, 2026

Extraction is the most critical moment of any expedition in **The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu**. You can nail the entire run — perfect stealth, full loot, zero damage — and lose everything in the last five minutes if you mishandle the extraction phase.

This guide covers the hidden timer, extraction signals, route planning, and the most common ways players fail to extract.

---

## The Extraction Process

| Step | Detail |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Complete or abandon your Contract objective |
| 2 | Navigate to the marked extraction point on your map |
| 3 | Activate the extraction flare / signal |
| 4 | Survive the waiting period (typically 60-90 seconds) |
| 5 | Board the rowboat or climb the rope to the Galleon |
| 6 | Confirm extraction on the results screen |

---

## The Hidden Timer: "The Forest Awakens"

The Mound features a hidden timer mechanic that dramatically changes the difficulty of extraction. The game does not show you a countdown, but the world gives you clear warnings.

### Timer Trigger Points

| Event | Typical Time Elapsed | Warning Signal |
|-------|---------------------|----------------|
| Expedition start | 0:00 | Drop-in sequence |
| First warning | ~15-20 minutes | Wind picks up, ambient audio shifts |
| Second warning | ~25-30 minutes | Distant howls, screen vignette darkens |
| "Forest Awakens" | ~30-35 minutes | Enemies spawn at 3x rate, extraction point may shift |
| Critical phase | ~40+ minutes | Almost guaranteed enemy swarm at extraction |

### How the Forest Awakening Changes the Game

| Before Awakening | After Awakening |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Standard enemy patrol density | 3x enemy spawns |
| Enemies have predictable patrol routes | Enemies actively hunt player positions |
| Extraction point is static | Extraction point may relocate |
| Y'm-bhi behave normally | Berserkers become more common |
| Ghost Bats in fixed locations | Bats swarm more aggressively |
| Ox Cart path is safe | Ox Cart may be overrun |

## Signals That It Is Time to Extract

Knowing when to leave is a skill that separates experienced players from dead ones.

### Green Light (Keep Exploring)
- Plenty of time since deployment (<15 min)
- Squad Sanity is above 50% for all members
- No unusual environmental audio
- Contract objective not yet complete
- You have open inventory slots

### Yellow Light (Start Heading Toward Extraction)
- 20+ minutes elapsed
- Wind has picked up noticeably
- Any squad member has Sanity below 30%
- Contract objective complete
- Inventory is full or near full
- You heard a distant howl

### Red Light (Extract Immediately)
- 30+ minutes elapsed
- "Forest Awakens" audio cue has played
- Squad member is down and cannot be revived quickly
- Multiple enemies aggroed and pursuing
- Any squad member has critical Sanity (<15%)
- Extraction point marker appears unstable or you see a fake extraction point

## Common Extraction Failures

### 1. Waiting Too Long
**The problem:** Greed. One more chest. One more room. One more fight.
**The result:** The forest awakens, enemies swarm, extraction becomes impossible.
**The fix:** Set a mental timer. When you hear the first wind shift, start moving toward extraction. Do not deviate.

### 2. Splitting the Squad on the Way Out
**The problem:** Two players take a "shortcut" while the others take the main path.
**The result:** One group gets pinned. The other cannot reach them in time to help. Someone dies.
**The fix:** Stick together. A five-minute detour is better than a permanent death.

### 3. Not Accounting for the Ox Cart
**The problem:** The Ox Cart is still deep in the map with valuable loot when the team decides to extract.
**The result:** Either someone dies retrieving the cart, or the loot is abandoned.
**The fix:** Plan your route so the cart is always between you and extraction during the second half of the expedition.

### 4. False Extraction Points
**The problem:** A player with low Sanity sees a fake extraction marker and leads the team there.
**The result:** Wasted time, potential ambush, actual extraction point becomes unreachable.
**The fix:** Have a high-Sanity player confirm the extraction point. Cross-reference with the map. If no one else sees the marker, it is a hallucination.

### 5. Panic During the Waiting Period
**The problem:** The extraction flare takes 60-90 seconds. Players panic, break formation, and get picked off.
**The result:** Downed players cannot be revived during the final countdown.
**The fix:** Form a defensive circle. Assign watch directions. Stay calm. You have survived the whole expedition — you can survive 90 more seconds.

## The Perfect Extraction Checklist

- [ ] All squad members alive and above 30% Sanity
- [ ] Extraction point confirmed by multiple players
- [ ] At least one clear path to extraction identified
- [ ] No active enemy pursuit
- [ ] Ox Cart emptied of high-value items
- [ ] Waiting-period defensive positions assigned
- [ ] Backup extraction route known (in case primary is compromised)
- [ ] Flare or signal item ready in inventory
