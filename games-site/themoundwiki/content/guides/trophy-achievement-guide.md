# The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu — Trophy & Achievement Guide

> **Launch Version / Updated: July 18, 2026**

The Mound ships with 28 trophies/achievements: 1 Platinum (PS only), 5 Gold, 10 Silver, 12 Bronze. This guide covers every unlock requirement, missable warnings, and the most efficient path to 100% completion.

---

## Trophy Overview

| Tier | Count | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| **Platinum** | 1 | PS5/PS4 only. Steam equivalent uses same list without Platinum. |
| **Gold** | 5 | Story completion + high-difficulty challenges |
| **Silver** | 10 | Milestones, collectible thresholds, mid-tier challenges |
| **Bronze** | 12 | Tutorial completions, first kills, basic milestones |
| **Total** | **28** | No DLC trophies at launch |

**Estimated time to 100%:** 25 – 35 hours
**Missable trophies:** 2
**Online required:** No — solo play supported for all.

## Missable Trophies

| Trophy | Tier | Condition | How to Secure |
|--------|------|-----------|---------------|
| **Silent as a Shadow** | Silver | Complete any Contract without firing a shot | Solo Easy on Isle of Voices. Equip Crossbow + Machete only (Crossbow doesn't count as a firearm). Backstab all Y'm-bhi. Extract — unlocks immediately. |
| **No Man Left Behind** | Gold | Extract with full squad alive on Hard+ | Assign a medic (3+ Healing Salves). If someone goes down, fall back to cover revive. Pick a short Recon Contract — do not overstay. |

## Combat & Challenge Trophies

| Trophy | Tier | Condition | Difficulty |
|--------|------|-----------|------------|
| First Blood | Bronze | First Y'm-bhi kill | Easy |
| Backstabber | Bronze | 10 melee backstabs | Easy |
| Axe Artist | Silver | 3 kills with one Francesca Axe throw | Medium |
| Untouchable | Silver | Complete expedition without damage | Hard |
| Rain Dancer | Silver | Complete expedition in heavy rain | Medium |
| Pyromaniac | Bronze | 20 Oil Lamp fire kills | Easy |
| Headhunter | Silver | 50 ranged headshots | Medium |
| One Man Army | Gold | Solo Hard Contract | Hard |
| Full Arsenal | Silver | Extract with every weapon type in inventory | Medium |

**Axe Artist:** Lure 3 Y'm-bhi to a choke point with Medallion ping. Aim above center enemy — the axe arcs downward for collateral kills. Sunken Grotto entrance is a reliable farm spot.

**Untouchable:** Solo Easy with Cloaking Relic. Choose a landmarks-only Recon Contract (zero combat). Walk (don't sprint) near enemies. Avoid all hazards.

**One Man Army:** Crossbow + Machete + Oil Lamp + 3 Salves. Medium Recon on Isle of Voices. Clear from range, never engage 2+ enemies at once. Save one Salve for Sanity. Extract immediately after primary objective.

## Misc Trophies

| Trophy | Tier | Condition |
|--------|------|-----------|
| Setting Sail | Bronze | Complete first expedition (any outcome) |
| Galleon Rat | Bronze | Talk to all 7 NPCs in one Galleon visit |
| Weathered | Silver | Survive 50 expeditions |
| Rich Conquistador | Silver | Accumulate 10,000 Currency |
| Faction Favorite | Silver | Max reputation with any faction |
| Speed Runner | Gold | Complete Hard Contract in under 8 min |
| All Weather | Silver | Extract in clear, rain, and storm |

**Galleon Rat:** Visit Expedition Leader (central cabin), Armorer (lower deck), Scholar (starboard), Medic (port), Quartermaster (cargo), Navigator (upper), Boatswain (crew quarters). Then accept any Contract to trigger unlock.

**Speed Runner:** "Lost Patrol" on Hard with lightest loadout (Crossbow + Machete only). Sprint to logbook, skip all loot, sprint to extract. Goal: 5–6 minutes.

## Efficient Roadmap

1. **Play through the story** (10 – 15 hours, ~10 trophies earned naturally)
2. **Target missables** while still progressing
3. **Farm collectibles** (lore pages, blueprints, relics)
4. **Challenge trophies last** (Untouchable, One Man Army, Speed Runner)
