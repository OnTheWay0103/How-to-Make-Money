# The Mound Enemy Guide — All Foes and Combat Tactics

> Launch Version / Updated: July 14, 2026

The forests of the New World are teeming with horrors. Understanding each enemy type — their behavior, weaknesses, and tells — is essential to survival. This guide covers every confirmed enemy in **The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu**.

## Y'm-bhi (Undead Sentinels)

Y'm-bhi are the most common enemy in The Mound. These undead conquistadors patrol the forests, ruins, and caves. They are intelligent combatants with a complex behavior set.

### Behavior Patterns

| Behavior | Description | Counter |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **Patrol** | Walk set routes with predictable timing. | Use patrol windows to sneak past or set up backstabs. |
| **Feint** | Y'm-bhi fakes an attack to bait your dodge or parry. | Do not panic-dodge. Wait for the actual strike. |
| **Parry** | They can deflect your melee attacks. | Do not spam attack. Mix in feints or use ranged weapons. |
| **Disarm** | A special attack that knocks your weapon out of your hands. | Keep distance; this has short range. Use Machete as backup. |
| **Tentacle Grab** | A ranged tentacle attack that holds you in place. | Teammate must shoot/melee the tentacle to free you. |
| **Self-Immolation** | When low on health, a Y'm-bhi may light itself on fire and charge. | Run perpendicular. Do not try to block. The fire spread is short. |

### Y'm-bhi Difficulty Variants

| Variant | Appearance | Special Traits |
|---------|-----------|----------------|
| Sentry | Standard armor, patrols | Has all standard behaviors |
| Berserker | Larger, red-tinted eyes | More health, cannot be staggered easily, faster charge |
| Scout | Light armor, moves quickly | Detects players from farther away, calls reinforcements |
| Shaman | Robed, carries a staff | Can heal nearby Y'm-bhi, drains Sanity from range |

### Combat Strategy Against Y'm-bhi

1. **Prioritize stealth.** Avoid engaging more than one at a time.
2. **Backstab first.** If you can approach from behind, the kill is instant.
3. **Use light.** Oil Lamp or torch light stuns Y'm-bhi for 2-3 seconds — long enough for a follow-up strike.
4. **Watch for the feint.** Experienced players learn to read the shoulder twitch that precedes a real attack.
5. **The tentacle grab is a team emergency.** If grabbed, call it out immediately. Your teammates have about 4 seconds to free you.
6. **Berserker self-immolation:** Do not group up. Spread out so the fire does not chain.

## Ghost Bats

Ethereal bat-like creatures that patrol cave entrances and dark zones.

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Aggression | Patrol only — attack if you enter their territory |
| Damage | Moderate per hit |
| Light Weakness | Exposed to direct light = instant disintegration |
| Swarm Behavior | Multiple bats may appear together |
| Sound | High-pitched screech when aggroed |

### Strategy
- **Oil Lamp is your best friend.** A single flash of light kills Ghost Bats instantly.
- **Do not fire guns at them.** They dodge projectiles and you waste ammo.
- **Group up when swarmed.** Multiple bats attacking one player is dangerous only if you are alone.

## Vine Creatures

Plant-based ambush predators that blend into vegetation.

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Aggression | Passive until triggered by footsteps |
| Damage | Medium (grab attack) |
| Weakness | Fire — Machete to cut vines |
| Detection | Vibration through ground |

### Strategy
- **Watch the ground.** Vine-heavy areas have a distinct visual texture. Slow down.
- **Machete cuts through grab attacks.** If grabbed, spam the melee button.
- **Fire is permanent removal.** A lit Oil Lamp or torch burns vine patches, clearing the area permanently.
- **Avoid running through undergrowth.** Walking reduces the vibration trigger.

## Giant Centipedes

Massive burrowing arthropods that emerge from the ground.

| Property | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| Aggression | Territorial — attacks when you enter its burrow zone |
| Damage | High per bite |
| Weakness | Slow to turn — attack from the side |
| Special | Burrows underground, emerges underfoot |

### Strategy
- **Listen for the rumble.** Before a centipede emerges, the ground vibrates audibly.
- **Keep moving.** Standing still makes you an easy target for the emergence attack.
- **Circle strafe.** Centipedes turn slowly. Once it commits to a direction, move to its flank.
- **High-damage weapons only.** Crossbow headshots and Arquebus shots are most effective. Machetes struggle against the armored segments.
- **Watch the tail.** Some centipedes can whip their tail in a wide arc.

## Backstab Mechanic — Complete Guide

Backstabbing is the most efficient kill method in The Mound.

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Position | Directly behind the enemy (180-degree rear arc) |
| Weapon | Any melee weapon or unarmed prompt |
| Enemy Type | Works on humanoid enemies (Y'm-bhi variants) |
| Noise | Silent — does not alert other enemies |
| Animation | 2-second execution animation (you are vulnerable during this) |

### Backstab Tips
- **Crouch** to reduce footstep noise while approaching.
- **Patience.** Wait for the enemy patrol path to angle away from you.
- **Berserkers have a larger detection radius.** Approach from further away and time your sprint.
- **Do not backstab in a group.** If another enemy can see you, the backstab prompt may not appear.
- **The animation locks you in place.** Make sure no other enemy is nearby before committing.
