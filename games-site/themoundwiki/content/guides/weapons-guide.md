# The Mound Weapons Guide — All 6 Weapons & Best Loadouts

> **Launch Version / Updated: July 14, 2026**

The Mound features six distinct weapons. Your loadout defines your role — there are no classes. Understanding each weapon's traits is essential for surviving expeditions.

---

## Weapon Overview

| Weapon | Type | Silent? | Rain-Proof? | Best For |
|--------|------|---------|-------------|----------|
| Matchlock Arquebus | Firearm | No | **No** | Burst damage, armored enemies |
| Flintlock Pistol | Firearm | No | **No** | Sidearm, close-range backup |
| Crossbow | Ranged | **Yes** | Yes | Stealth kills, rainy conditions |
| Francesca Axe | Throwable | Yes | Yes | Precision throws, risk/reward |
| Machete | Melee | Yes | Yes | Reliable close combat |
| Oil Lamp | Utility | — | Flickers | Light source, throwable fire |

## Detailed Breakdown

### Matchlock Arquebus
- **Role**: Primary damage dealer
- **Strengths**: Devastating single shot, armor-piercing at close range
- **Weaknesses**: Long reload, can misfire, **fuse dies in rain**
- **Tactics**: Fire from shelter when possible. Time your shots — you won't get a second chance quickly. Pair with a Crossbow user for rainy conditions.

### Flintlock Pistol
- **Role**: Backup sidearm
- **Strengths**: Fast draw, decent close-range punch
- **Weaknesses**: Limited range, **rain-vulnerable**, low ammo capacity
- **Tactics**: Holster it. This is your "oh sh—" button, not your primary. Switch to melee after firing.

### Crossbow
- **Role**: Stealth marksman
- **Strengths**: **Silent**, rain-proof, armor-piercing bolts at close range
- **Weaknesses**: Slow deliberate shots, limited bolt capacity
- **Tactics**: The best weapon for controlling battlefield pace. Use for picking off Y'm-bhi before they alert others. **Essential in rain.**

### Francesca Axe
- **Role**: Precision throwable
- **Strengths**: Silent, retrievable, damage varies by throw accuracy
- **Weaknesses**: High skill ceiling, lost if missed into inaccessible areas
- **Tactics**: Aim for headshots. Practice the arc. Excellent for backstab alternatives when you can't close distance.

### Machete / Sailor's Knife
- **Role**: Reliable melee
- **Strengths**: Always works, no ammo, no weather effects
- **Weaknesses**: Short range, lower damage than specialized weapons
- **Tactics**: Everyone should carry one. It's your fallback when everything else fails. Backstab one-shots most Y'm-bhi.

### Oil Lamp
- **Role**: Utility / light source
- **Strengths**: Steady light (rain-proof), weakens light-sensitive creatures, throwable for fire burst
- **Weaknesses**: Flickers in moisture, limited fuel
- **Tactics**: Throw at Y'm-bhi carrying lamps — they self-immolate. Use to illuminate dark areas. Essential for teams without a dedicated light-bearer.

## Weather Consideration Table

| Weather | Firearms Work? | Oil Lamp? | Best Weapons |
|---------|---------------|-----------|--------------|
| Clear / Night | ✅ Yes | ✅ Steady | Matchlock + Flintlock |
| Light Rain | ❌ No | ⚠️ Flickers | Crossbow + Machete |
| Heavy Storm | ❌ No | ⚠️ Dim | Crossbow + Axe |

> **Golden Rule**: Always check the sky before choosing firearms. A sudden rainstorm mid-expedition turns your arquebus into a club.

## Recommended 4-Player Loadout

| Role | Primary Weapon | Secondary | Tool |
|------|---------------|-----------|------|
| **Marksman** | Matchlock Arquebus | Machete | — |
| **Scout** | Crossbow | Flintlock Pistol | Medallion |
| **Support** | Oil Lamp | Machete | Medallion |
| **Flex** | Francesca Axe | Crossbow | Mask of Nyarlathotep |

**Why this works**: The Marksman handles high-HP threats. The Scout remains silent for reconnaissance. Support provides light and enemy debuffs. Flex adapts to any situation — and the Mask lets them walk among Y'm-bhi undetected.
