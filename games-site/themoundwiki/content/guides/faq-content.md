# The Mound FAQ — 15 Most Common Questions Answered

> Launch Version / Updated: July 14, 2026

---

## Q1: What is The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu?

**A:** The Mound: Omen of Cthulhu is a 1-4 player cooperative PvE extraction horror game developed by ACE Team and published by NACON. Set in 1652 Chile during the conquest era, players take on the role of Spanish conquistadors exploring Lovecraftian-infested islands. The game blends extraction shooter mechanics with survival horror, featuring a unique Sanity system, spatial voice chat, and dynamic weather that affects gameplay.

---

## Q2: How much does The Mound cost?

**A:** The base game is priced at **$29.99 USD** on all platforms (PC, PS5, Xbox Series X|S). A **Deluxe Edition** is available for **$39.99 USD**, which includes additional cosmetic items and early access to certain in-game tools.

---

## Q3: What platforms is The Mound available on?

**A:** The Mound is available on:
- **PC** (Steam)
- **PlayStation 5**
- **Xbox Series X | S**

It is **not** available on PlayStation 4, Xbox One, or Nintendo Switch.

---

## Q4: Can I play The Mound solo?

**A:** Yes. The Mound fully supports solo play. When playing alone, you are accompanied by AI-controlled companions who can follow basic commands (follow, hold, attack, retreat). However, the game is designed with co-op in mind, and solo play is significantly more challenging due to the Sanity system (no one to confirm hallucinations) and the absence of human revival support.

For detailed solo play strategies, see the [Solo Guide](/guides/solo-guide).

---

## Q5: Does The Mound support crossplay?

**A:** Yes, The Mound supports full crossplay across all platforms. PC, PS5, and Xbox Series X|S players can squad up together. Cross-platform voice chat is supported. Note that crossplay parties are managed through the in-game friend system, not platform-specific invites.

---

## Q6: How many players can play together?

**A:** The Mound supports 1-4 players in a single expedition. There is no competitive multiplayer mode — the game is strictly PvE (Player vs Environment). The recommended squad size is 4 players for standard difficulty contracts.

---

## Q7: Does The Mound have cross-progression?

**A:** Yes. Your personal rank and unlocks are tied to your NACON account and carry across all platforms. Story progress is shared per squad but your individual character progression (rank, cosmetics, currency) is saved to your account and accessible on any platform.

## Q12: Is The Mound a live service game?

**A:** The Mound is a full-priced game with no battle pass or subscription. NACON and ACE Team have committed to post-launch support including:
- Free content updates (new contracts, enemies, and tools)
- Paid cosmetic DLC packs (optional)
- Bug fixes and balance patches

There are no microtransactions for gameplay advantages.

---

## Q13: Does The Mound have difficulty settings?

**A:** The Mound does not have traditional difficulty sliders. Difficulty is governed by:
- **Contract selection:** Easy, Medium, Hard contracts determine enemy density and objective complexity.
- **Team size:** Solo play is effectively harder than co-op play.
- **Weather conditions:** Dynamic weather can increase or decrease difficulty on the same contract.

The game's adaptive difficulty system adjusts enemy density based on team performance, but there is no explicit "Easy Mode."

---

## Q14: How long is a typical expedition in The Mound?

**A:** Expedition length varies by contract type and play style:

| Contract Type | Typical Duration |
|--------------|-----------------|
| Easy Salvage / Recon | 15-25 minutes |
| Medium Contract | 25-40 minutes |
| Hard Contract | 35-60 minutes |
| Story Contract | 30-60 minutes |
| Failed / Early Extraction | 5-15 minutes |

Most players find that a full expedition takes **30-45 minutes** on average.

---

## Q15: Will The Mound have mod support?

**A:** ACE Team has not announced official mod support for The Mound at launch. The game uses a proprietary engine, and modding tools are not currently planned. Community feedback may influence this decision post-launch.
