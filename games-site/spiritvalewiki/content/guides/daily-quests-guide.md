# SpiritVale Daily Quests Guide — Daily & Weekly Routines

**SpiritVale EA / Updated: July 23, 2026**

SpiritVale offers a rich system of daily and weekly repeatable quests that provide consistent experience, gold, reputation, and rare materials. This guide breaks down every repeatable quest, the optimal order to complete them, and how to maximize your rewards per minute.

---

## 1. Understanding the Quest Reset Cycle

SpiritVale operates on two reset cycles:

- **Daily Quests:** Reset at 05:00 server time every day. Each daily takes 5-15 minutes to complete.
- **Weekly Quests:** Reset on Monday at 05:00 server time. Weekly quests take 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on the objective.

Server time is displayed in the top-right corner of the game UI. Plan your sessions around these resets to avoid wasting potential rewards.

## 2. Daily Quest Guide

| Quest Name | NPC Location | Level Requirement | Time to Complete | Primary Rewards |
|------------|-------------|-------------------|------------------|-----------------|
| Bounty Board | Vale's Rest Bulletin Board | 15+ | 5 min | Gold, XP tokens |
| Material Delivery | Any Town Material Trader | 20+ | 3 min | Crafting materials |
| Dungeon Daily | Dungeon Entrance NPC | 25+ | 10-15 min | Equipment, dungeon tokens |
| Arena Daily | Colosseum Registrar | 30+ | 8-12 min | PvP tokens, gold |
| Gathering Run | Harvest Guild NPC | 10+ | 5 min | Herbs, ores, cooking ingredients |

### Optimal Daily Quest Order

For maximum efficiency, follow this route:

1. **Log in at Vale's Rest.** Pick up the Bounty Board quest (usually kill 10-15 mobs in a specific zone).
2. **Accept Gathering Run** from the Harvest Guild (located two buildings north of the Bulletin Board).
3. **Complete both in the same zone.** Choose a zone that has both the bounty targets and gather-able resources. Most zones overlap at least 70%.
4. **Return to town. Hand in both.** Turn around and accept Material Delivery — turn in 20 crafting materials you likely already have in your inventory.
5. **Queue for Dungeon Daily** and **Arena Daily** while in town. These require group matching; queue time is a good moment to organize your inventory or manage guild activities.

**Total time:** 25-35 minutes for all 5 dailies.

## 3. Weekly Quest Guide

Weekly quests offer significantly larger rewards and should be prioritized each reset.

### Main Weekly Quests

| Quest | Requirements | Time | Rewards |
|-------|-------------|------|---------|
| World Boss Weekly | Defeat 3 world bosses (any) | 30-60 min | Rare weapon box, 50,000 gold, XP elixir |
| Card Collector | Collect 50 cards (any rarity) | Variable (playtime) | Random Rare card, Card Dust x100 |
| Dungeon Marathon | Clear 5 different dungeons | 60-90 min | Epic equipment chest, 30 Dungeon Tokens |
| PvP Champion | Win 10 arena matches | 45-90 min | PvP weapon box, 50 PvP Tokens |
| Crafting Expert | Craft 100 items (any level) | Variable | Master Crafter title, 20 Material Tokens |

### Weekly Reputation Quests

Each major faction offers weekly reputation quests. Raising reputation unlocks exclusive gear recipes, cosmetics, and discounts:

| Faction | Quest | Rep Reward | Max Rep Unlock |
|---------|-------|------------|----------------|
| Vale's Rest Guard | Kill 50 enemies in Whisperwind Plains | 500 rep | Guard armor set (Honored) |

| Colosseum Committee | Win 15 PvP matches | 500 rep | Gladiator weapon skin (Exalted) |
| Merchant's Union | Sell 100,000 gold worth of items | 500 rep | Reduced auction house fees (Honored) |

## 4. Time-Efficient Routine Templates

### Casual Routine (30-45 minutes daily)

1. Bounty Board — 5 min (kill zone quest)
2. Gathering Run — combined with bounty zone — 5 min
3. Material Delivery — quick turn-in — 3 min
4. Queue Arena Daily while doing other activities — 10 min active

**Weekly target:** Complete World Boss Weekly + Card Collector + one reputation quest. Total weekly time: approximately 4 hours.

### Hardcore Routine (1-2 hours daily)

1. All 5 dailies in optimal route order — 30 min
2. One dungeon run (ticking Dungeon Weekly counter) — 15 min
3. 3-5 arena matches (ticking PvP Weekly counter) — 20 min
4. Crafting overflow (passive — craft while waiting for queues) — 10 min
5. Guild quests — 15 min

**Weekly target:** All weekly quests completed by Wednesday, reputation quests done by Friday, World Boss circuit on Saturday reset.

### Efficiency Tips

- **Stack quest zones.** Never accept a quest that sends you to a different zone than your other active quests.
- **Use Warp Scrolls.** Keep a stack of Warp Scrolls for rapid travel between towns. They're inexpensive from the Magic Shop.
- **Pre-craft.** If you know the Crafting Expert weekly is coming, stockpile simple crafts ready to turn in immediately after reset.
- **Party for world bosses.** World boss spawns are advertised in World Chat. Join a party for credit — soloing is inefficient.
- **Skip low-value dailies.** If a Bounty Board quest sends you across the map, abandon it and take a different one. You can reroll once per day for free.
