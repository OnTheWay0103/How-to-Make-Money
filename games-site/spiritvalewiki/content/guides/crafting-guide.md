## Gear Refinement

Refinement increases equipment base stats. The system uses a standard MMO upgrade ladder:

| Refine Level | Success Rate | Materials | Stat Increase |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|---------------|
| +1 to +3 | 100% | 1 Refine Stone per attempt | +5% base stats |
| +4 to +6 | 70% | 2 Refine Stones | +10% base stats |
| +7 to +9 | 40% | 3 Refine Stones + 1 Stabilizer | +15% base stats |
| +10 | 20% | 5 Refine Stones + 2 Stabilizers | +25% base stats |

### Refinement Materials

- **Refine Stone:** Drops from bosses, mining nodes, or purchased from player stalls
- **Stabilizer:** Increases success rate by 15% per stack (max 2 per attempt)
  - Crafted at Alchemy Station using 10 Refine Stones

### Important Notes

- Failed refinement does NOT destroy equipment (no "break" mechanic)
- Failed refinement reduces refinement level by 1 (minimum 0)
- Refinement effects transfer when upgrading gear to a higher tier (via Refine Transfer NPC in Ironforge Outpost)

## Player Trading & Economy

SpiritVale has a player-driven economy. Key points:

- **Stall System:** Set up a vendor stall in town squares (Vale's Rest, Ironforge Outpost)
- **No Auction House:** All player trading must be done via stalls or direct trade
- **Hot Items:** Refine Stones, rare cards, endgame crafted gear
- **Pricing:** Check multiple stalls before buying; prices vary widely
- **Tax:** 5% transaction tax on all stall sales

---

## Crafting Tips

1. **Mine while leveling** — Pick up ores as you pass through zones; you will need them at endgame
2. **Save all Refine Stones** — They are account-bound and needed for endgame gear
3. **Join a crafting party** — Group gathering trips to Soulfire Citadel yield more materials
4. **Craft for profit** — Mid-level gear (Level 30 Mithril sets) sells well through stalls to new players
5. **Don't refine past +7** until you have backup gear — the failure rate at +8 and above is punishing
