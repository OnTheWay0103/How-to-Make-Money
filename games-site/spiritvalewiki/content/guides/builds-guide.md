# SpiritVale Builds Guide — Paladin, Berserker, Archmage, Sniper

**SpiritVale EA / Updated: July 12, 2026**

Recommended endgame builds for the four most popular advanced classes. Each build includes attribute distribution, skill priorities, and card recommendations for optimal performance.

---

## 1. Paladin Tank Build

The go-to tank for all group content. Your job is to hold aggro, mitigate damage, and provide party survivability.

### Attribute Allocation

| Stat | Points (Level 50) | Why |
|------|-------------------|-----|
| VIT | 80 | Max HP and defense |
| STR | 40 | Enough for aggro generation |
| AGI | 20 | Attack speed for skill rotations |

Remaining points: dump into VIT.

### Skill Priority

1. **Holy Shield (Max)** — Main defensive skill, damage reduction
2. **Divine Strike (Max)** — Primary aggro generator
3. **Guardian Aura (Max)** — Party-wide defense buff
4. **Sacred Charge (1)** — Gap closer / interrupt
5. **Blessing of Light (Max)** — Self-heal over time

### Card Recommendations

| Slot | Card | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| Weapon | Iron Guardian Card | +Threat generation |
| Armor | Stone Shell Card | +Physical Defense |
| Accessory 1 | Heart of Oak Card | +Max HP % |
| Accessory 2 | Crystal Ward Card | +Magic Defense |

## 2. Berserker DPS Build

Highest melee burst damage in the game. Trade defense for raw offense.

### Attribute Allocation

| Stat | Points (Level 50) | Why |
|------|-------------------|-----|
| STR | 100 | Maximum melee damage |
| AGI | 30 | Attack speed for rage generation |
| VIT | 10 | Minimal — survive one hit |

### Skill Priority

1. **Rage Unleashed (Max)** — Core damage steroid
2. **Whirlwind Slash (Max)** — Primary AoE
3. **Blood Strike (Max)** — Self-healing, damage combo
4. **Furious Leap (1)** — Gap closer
5. **Berserker Fury (Max)** — Attack speed + damage buff

### Card Recommendations

| Slot | Card | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| Weapon | Raging Bull Card | +Critical Damage |
| Armor | Wolf Hide Card | +Attack Speed |
| Accessory 1 | Sharp Fang Card | +Critical Rate |
| Accessory 2 | Crimson Heart Card | +Lifesteal % |

## 3. Archmage (Wizard) AoE Build

The fastest PvE farmer. Screen-clearing AoE at the cost of being extremely fragile.

### Attribute Allocation

| Stat | Points (Level 50) | Why |
|------|-------------------|-----|
| INT | 100 | Maximum magic damage |
| DEX | 30 | Cast speed for faster rotations |
| VIT | 10 | Avoid one-shots |

### Skill Priority

1. **Firestorm (Max)** — Primary AoE damage
2. **Arcane Nova (Max)** — Burst AoE, short cooldown
3. **Ice Barrier (1)** — Emergency survivability
4. **Mana Surge (Max)** — SP regen + magic damage passive
5. **Blink (1)** — Teleport dodge

### Card Recommendations

| Slot | Card | Effect |
|------|------|--------|
| Weapon | Wildfire Card | +Fire Damage % |
| Armor | Silkweave Card | +Magic Defense |
| Accessory 1 | Arcane Orb Card | +INT % |
| Accessory 2 | Sages' Memory Card | +SP Regen |

## General Build Tips

- Visit the **Waybinder NPC** to respec freely — test different stat distributions
- Card bonuses stack — running duplicate card effects is viable
- Prioritize weapon upgrades over armor for faster clear times
- Builds above assume Level 50 with ~150 total stat points (Base Level 50 grants 150 points)
