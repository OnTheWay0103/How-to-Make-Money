# SpiritVale Classes Guide — All 15 Base & Advanced Classes

**SpiritVale EA / Updated: July 12, 2026**

SpiritVale features 7 base classes and 8 advanced classes obtainable at Job Level 50. This guide covers every class, its role, weapon, core stats, and viability.

---

## Base Level vs. Job Level

The two-level system is critical to understand:

| Stat | Base Level | Job Level |
|------|------------|-----------|
| Max cap | 99 | 50 |
| How to gain | Any XP gain | Killing monsters with class skills |
| What it unlocks | Attribute points (3 per level) | Skill points, Class Advancement |
| Respec cost | Free (Waybinder NPC) | Free (Waybinder NPC) |

At Job Level 50, you complete a Class Advancement quest to evolve into an advanced class.

## Advanced Classes (Job Level 50)

| Advanced Class | Base Prerequisite | Role | Weapon |
|----------------|-------------------|------|--------|
| Paladin | Knight | Tank / Support | Sword + Shield |
| Berserker | Warrior | Melee Burst DPS | Two-Handed Sword |
| Priest | Acolyte | Healer / Buffer | Mace, Staff |
| Wizard | Mage | AoE Magic DPS | Staff |
| Sniper | Scout | Ranged Single-Target DPS | Bow |
| Shinobi | Rogue | Stealth Burst DPS | Dagger |
| Necromancer | Summoner | Pet-based DPS / Debuffer | Tome |
| Gunslinger | Advanced (any base) | Ranged DPS | Dual Pistols |

**Note:** Gunslinger is unique — it does not evolve from a base class. Any class reaching Job Level 50 can change into Gunslinger, provided you complete the Gunslinger quest chain.

## Core Attributes Explained

| Stat | Effect | Priority Classes |
|------|--------|------------------|
| STR | Increases melee damage | Knight, Warrior, Paladin, Berserker |
| VIT | Increases HP and defense | Knight, Paladin (all classes benefit) |
| DEX | Increases ranged damage and cast speed | Mage, Scout, Wizard, Sniper |
| AGI | Increases attack speed and dodge | Rogue, Shinobi, Scout, Sniper |
| INT | Increases magic damage and SP regen | Mage, Acolyte, Summoner, Wizard, Priest, Necromancer |
| LUCK | Increases critical rate and critical damage | Rogue, Shinobi, Gunslinger |

## How to Respec

The **Waybinder NPC** is found in every major town (Vale's Rest, Ironforge Outpost, Silvertide Harbor). Speaking to them allows:

- **Reset all attribute points** — Redistribute STR/VIT/DEX/AGI/INT/LUCK freely
- **Reset all skill points** — Re-pick every skill in your current class tree
- **Change advanced class** — Switch between advanced classes (character must be Job Level 50+)

All respeccing is **completely free**. There is no gold cost, item cost, or cooldown. Experiment as much as you like.
