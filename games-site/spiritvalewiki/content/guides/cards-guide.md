# SpiritVale Cards Guide — How to Farm, Slot, and Combine Cards

**SpiritVale EA / Updated: July 12, 2026**

Cards are the primary horizontal progression system in SpiritVale. A well-optimized card setup can double your effective combat power. This guide covers everything about the card system — from basics to rare card farming routes.

---

## Card System Basics

Cards are equippable items that slot into your gear. Each piece of equipment has a set number of card slots.

| Gear Slot | Max Card Slots | Unlock Level |
|-----------|----------------|--------------|
| Weapon | 2 | 8 |
| Armor | 2 | 8 |
| Accessory 1 | 1 | 8 |
| Accessory 2 | 1 | 8 |

### Card Rarity

| Rarity | Color | Stat Range | Drop Rate |
|--------|-------|------------|-----------|
| Common | Gray | +1-3 | Very common |
| Uncommon | Green | +3-6 | Common |
| Rare | Blue | +6-12 | Uncommon |
| Epic | Purple | +12-20 | Rare |
| Legendary | Orange | +20-35 | Very rare |

## How to Obtain Cards

### 1. Monster Drops
Every monster in SpiritVale has a chance to drop its associated card. The drop rate varies by monster rarity:
- **Normal mobs:** ~1-5% drop rate
- **Elite mobs:** ~10-15% drop rate
- **Bosses:** ~25-50% drop rate (guaranteed minimum Uncommon)

### 2. Card Exchange NPC
Located in Ironforge Outpost. Trade 10 duplicate cards of the same rarity for 1 random card of the next rarity tier.

### 3. Crafting Station
The Card Forge (unlocked via quest at Level 20) allows:
- Combine 5 cards of same rarity → 1 random card of next rarity
- 100% success rate, no materials needed beyond the cards

### 4. Event Drops
Limited-time events and daily login rewards sometimes grant exclusive cards.

---

## How to Equip Cards

1. Open your **Inventory** (I) and select the **Cards** tab.
2. Right-click a card, then click **Slot into Equipment**.
3. Select the gear piece to install it.
4. Cards can be removed with a **Card Remover** tool (sold at General Goods NPC for 500 gold).

## Rare Card Farming Routes

### Whisperwind Plains (Level 15-25)
- **Iron Boar Card** — Common, starter ATK card
- **Wind Runner Card** — Common, movement speed
- **Queen Bee Card (Rare)** — Bee Hive area, ~5% drop rate

### Sunken Hollow (Level 25-35)
- **Minotaur Card (Rare)** — Minotaur spawn in central cave, ~8% drop rate
- **Lava Elemental Card (Epic)** — Lava pit area, ~3% drop rate
- **Cave Bat Card (Uncommon)** — Cave entrance, high density

### Soulfire Citadel Exterior (Level 42-50)
- **Void Mage Card (Epic)** — Citadel courtyard, ~4% drop rate
- **Arcane Wraith Card (Rare)** — Citadel perimeter, ~6% drop rate
- **Soulkeeper Card (Legendary)** — Citadel boss area, <1% drop rate
