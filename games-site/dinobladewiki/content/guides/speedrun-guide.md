# Dinoblade Speedrun Guide — Optimal Routes & Boss Rush Times

**Launch Version / Updated: July 23, 2026**

Dinoblade's punishing combat rewards mastery, and speedrunning takes that mastery to its logical extreme. Whether you are chasing a personal best or a top leaderboard spot, this guide covers the optimal routes, gear priorities, skips, and target splits for Any% and Boss Rush categories.

---

## 1. Speedrun Categories

| Category | Objective | Current World Record Estimate | Difficulty |
|----------|-----------|------------------------------|------------|
| Any% | Beat the final boss (Kira, the Exile) as fast as possible | ~45 minutes | Medium |
| Any% (No Major Skips) | Same goal, but no sequence breaks | ~58 minutes | Medium |
| Boss Rush | Defeat all bosses in sequence without exploration | ~22 minutes | Hard |
| 100% | All bosses, all upgrades, all campfires, all secrets | ~3 hours | Very Hard |
| True Ending | Reach the True Ending (see Endings Guide) | ~1 hour 15 minutes | Hard |

This guide focuses on **Any%** and **Boss Rush** — the most popular categories.

## 2. Any% Route — Optimal Path

### Overview
The Any% route is built around three principles: minimize backtracking, prioritize damage upgrades, and skip every optional encounter. The following route assumes intermediate skill with Dinoblade's parry mechanics.

### Segment 1: Jurassic Plains (Target: 0:00-12:00)

| Step | Action | Time Budget | Notes |
|------|--------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Sprint past all small raptors to the first campfire | 1:30 | Do not fight anything. Take the direct path northwest. |
| 2 | Loot the Fossil Greatsword from the hidden cave | 2:00 | Requires one jump over a root. See Secrets Guide for exact location. |
| 3 | Gather 3 Healing Herbs near the waterfall | 1:30 | Grab only the three closest to the path. Do not explore. |
| 4 | Run to the Plains Outpost, upgrade Fossil Greatsword to +1 | 3:00 | Spend Ancient Bones from the cave chest on weapon upgrade. |
| 5 | Cross the Plains Bridge to Crescent Canyon | 4:00 | Kill the Alpha Raptor blocking the bridge (2 charged heavies + 1 parry). |

**Segment checkpoint:** 12:00. If you are past 14:00, consider resetting.

### Segment 2: Crescent Canyon (Target: 12:00-25:00)

| Step | Action | Time Budget | Notes |
|------|--------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Ride the falling platform shortcut past the first gate | 1:00 | Jump onto the collapsing stone bridge, then drop to the lower path. |
| 2 | Fight Canyon Guardian (miniboss) | 3:00 | Parry its charge attack for the riposte window. 2 parries + 4 heavies kills it. |
| 3 | Collect the War Hammer from the Guardian's arena | 0:30 | Swap from Fossil Greatsword — War Hammer deals more stagger damage. |
| 4 | Run past the Pterosaur Nest (do not engage) | 2:00 | Sprint and roll through the narrow corridor. The pterosaurs will miss you. |
| 5 | Campfire at Canyon Summit | 0:30 | Rest, equip War Hammer, allocate stats. Prioritize STR (25) and VIT (18). |
| 6 | Approach Kira's arena | 1:00 | Optional: back to Plains Outpost to upgrade War Hammer to +2 if you have enough bones. |

**Segment checkpoint:** 25:00. On pace for sub-50.

### Segment 3: Kira the Exile (Target: 25:00-45:00)

| Phase | Strategy | Expected Time |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| Phase 1 | Parry her first 3 attacks for riposte damage. Stay aggressive. | 4:00 |
| Phase 2 (75% HP) | She summons shadow clones. Ignore them — focus Kira. Roll through clone attacks. | 5:00 |
| Phase 3 (50% HP) | Kira gains a ranged beam attack. Close distance immediately — she has no close-range defense. | 4:00 |
| Phase 4 (25% HP) | Enrage mode. Use all remaining Healing Herbs. Parry her final combo for the kill. | 7:00 |

**Target clear time:** 45:00. Good run. Sub-40 is possible with perfect parries and no mistakes.

## 3. Sequence Breaks and Skips

### Canyon Falling Platform Skip
Instead of taking the long path through Crescent Canyon's main gate, jump onto the cracked stone bridge near the entrance. It collapses after 3 seconds, dropping you onto the lower canyon path that skips approximately 4 minutes of platforming.

**Execution:** Sprint toward the bridge, jump at the last moment, and immediately roll when you land to cancel the fall stun. If you miss, you will take fall damage (about 30% HP) but can try again from the nearby campfire.

### Pterosaur Nest Sprint
The Pterosaur Nest is normally a combat encounter. Instead, sprint through without stopping. The pterosaurs' attack wind-up is long enough that if you maintain sprint, you will take at most 1-2 hits (about 40% total HP). Chug a Healing Herb after you clear the room.

### Kira Clone Skip
In Kira's phase 2, the shadow clones are designed to block your path. However, if you stay glued to Kira's hitbox, the clones spawn behind you and never block your attacks. You take damage from their swings, but you deal damage to Kira faster than the clones deal damage to you.

## 4. Boss Rush Route

Boss Rush mode pits you against every boss back-to-back with no exploration between fights. You keep your gear and HP between encounters.

### Boss Order and Target Times

| Boss | Target Time | Key Strategy |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| Canyon Guardian | 2:30 | Parry the charge, then 4 charged heavies. Do not bother with the adds — they despawn when the Guardian dies. |
| Alpha Raptor | 1:45 | Two parries kill it. Its leap attack has the most generous parry window in the game (7 frames). |
| Ancient Pterosaur King | 3:00 | Ranged boss. Use the War Hammer's charged heavy to stagger it out of the air. Grounded = free damage window. |
| Fallen Warrior | 2:15 | Slow but hard-hitting. Three parries, then riposte. Do not trade hits — you lose. |
| Kira the Exile | 5:00 | Same strategy as Any% but you must be more aggressive. No healing between phases. Eat all 5 Healing Herbs as soon as you drop below 50% HP. |
| T-Rex Prime (Boss Rush Exclusive) | 7:30 | The final boss of Boss Rush. Massive HP pool. Target the legs to stagger it, then attack the head for 3x damage. This fight is an endurance test — keep moving, never stand still. |

**Total target:** 22:00. World record pace is sub-18:00 with perfect play.

### Boss Rush Gear Strategy
You start Boss Rush with a base weapon. As you kill bosses, you collect their weapons. Swap gear strategically:

1. Start with the default **Greatsword** (balanced damage)
2. After Canyon Guardian, swap to the **War Hammer** (high stagger)
3. After Alpha Raptor, swap to the **Raptor Claws** (fast attack speed for Kira)
4. For T-Rex Prime, use whichever weapon has the highest upgrade level — the **War Hammer +3** is usually the best option

### Boss Rush Healing Management
You have exactly 5 Healing Herbs for the entire run. Use them sparingly:

- Canyon Guardian: 0 herbs (parry everything)
- Alpha Raptor: 0 herbs (parry everything)
- Ancient Pterosaur King: 1 herb (you will take unavoidable ranged damage)
- Fallen Warrior: 1 herb (his swings have generous parry windows but punishing damage)
- Kira the Exile: 2 herbs (phase 3 and phase 4)
- T-Rex Prime: 1 herb remaining

## 5. Stat Allocation for Speedruns

Speedrun stat allocation is different from a normal playthrough. Prioritize damage over survivability:

| Stat | Any% Target | Boss Rush Target | Reasoning |
|------|-------------|------------------|-----------|
| Strength | 30 | 35 | Maximum weapon damage for faster boss kills |
| Vitality | 18 | 15 | Just enough to survive one mistake per boss |
| Endurance | 15 | 12 | Enough stamina for 2 combos + a dodge |
| Dexterity | 10 | 10 | Minimum weapon requirements only |
| Spirit | 6 | 6 | Ignored — special skills are not used in speedruns |

**Stat order:** Level STR first (to 25), then VIT (to 15), then END (to 12), then STR to cap.

## 6. Practice Drills

To improve your speedrun times, practice these specific drills:

1. **Parry Drill (10 minutes):** Fight the Alpha Raptor in Jurassic Plains. Do not attack — only parry. Land 10 consecutive parries before moving on.
2. **Kira Phase 1 Drill (5 minutes):** Start a new save, rush to Kira, and fight only until phase 2 triggers. Practice the opening sequence.
3. **Boss Rush First Half (15 minutes):** Run Boss Rush through Canyon Guardian + Alpha Raptor + Ancient Pterosaur King. Reset after those three.
4. **T-Rex Prime Leg Targeting (10 minutes):** Practice staying behind the T-Rex's legs. The camera can be disorienting — learn to control it in this specific fight.
