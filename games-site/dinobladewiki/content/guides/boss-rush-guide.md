# Dinoblade Boss Rush Guide — S-Rank Strategies and Rewards

Boss Rush is Dinoblade's premier endgame challenge mode. After conquering Kasei the Tyrant and seeing the credits roll, this mode offers a rigorous test of everything you have learned. This guide covers how to unlock Boss Rush, the scoring system, optimal strategies for S-rank, and every reward you can earn.

---

## How to Unlock Boss Rush

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Prerequisite** | Defeat the final boss **Kasei the Tyrant** |
| **Difficulty** | Any (Normal, Hard, or NG+) |
| **How to access** | After beating the game, a new "Boss Rush" option appears on the main menu |
| **New Game+ note** | Boss Rush is also available from NG+ menus immediately after starting a new cycle |

There is no level requirement and no gear restriction — bring whatever you have. However, the mode scales to your current power level, so a New Game+ run will feature bosses with significantly more HP and damage.

---

## Boss Rush Format

### Core Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Boss order** | Fixed: Kira the Exile → Axe → Kasei the Tyrant (with optional sub-bosses depending on run modifiers) |
| **Healing** | Limited to 3 Estus flask equivalents for the entire run |
| **Checkpoints** | None. A death sends you back to the start of the current boss fight (not the beginning of the run). |
| **Timer** | Active from the moment you enter the first boss arena. Pauses during boss transition screens. |
| **Resting** | Not allowed. You cannot sit at a Roost between fights. |

### Scoring Breakdown

| Scoring Category | Weight | How to Maximize |
|-----------------|--------|-----------------|
| **Clear Time** | 40% | Finish the full run as fast as possible. Target: under 12 minutes for maximum time score. |
| **Damage Taken** | 30% | The less HP you lose across all fights, the better. Perfect runs (no hits) earn full points. |
| **Parries Performed** | 20% | Each successful parry adds to this score. Failed parries do not subtract. |
| **Deaths** | 10% | Subtract 2% per death (capped at 10% loss). Zero deaths = full score. |

### Score Ranks

| Rank | Score Required | Notes |
|------|--------------|-------|
| **S** | 90% or higher | Parry-heavy approach, no deaths, speed-focused routing |
| **A** | 75% to 89% | Moderate parry usage, 1-2 deaths acceptable |
| **B** | 60% to 74% | Survive each fight; speed is secondary |
| **C** | Below 60% | Completion-oriented. Focus on learning boss patterns. |

## S-Rank Strategies

Achieving S-rank in Boss Rush requires mastery of all three main bosses and a well-optimized build. Below is the recommended approach.

### Recommended Build

| Stat | Target | Reasoning |
|------|--------|-----------|
| **Dexterity** | 45+ | Fastest clear times with Claws; A/S scaling on dexterity weapons |
| **Endurance** | 30 | Enough stamina for sustained offense between pauses |
| **Vitality** | 20 | Survive one or two hits — enough margin without wasting points |
| **Spirit** | 15 | Allows two Alpha Roar uses per boss fight |
| **Strength** | Base (10) | Not needed for a DEX build |

**Best weapon:** Claws (Dexterity build). Their fast attack speed and low stamina cost allow the shortest kill times on all three bosses. Infuse with fire damage for bonus damage against Axe and Kasei.

**Backup weapon:** Fossil Greatsword (equipped in second weapon slot). Use only against Axe for the Fossilized slow debuff.

**Armor:** Light or medium. Must stay below 30% equip load for fast rolls.

### Optimal Boss Order and Strategies

The boss order is fixed, but your approach to each fight determines your score.

#### Boss 1: Kira the Exile

| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **1** | Parry her leaping bite. This is the single biggest parry score opportunity in the entire run. She uses this move at the start of the fight and whenever she backs away. |
| **2** | Stay aggressive. Kira has low poise and can be staggered repeatedly with Claws. |
| **3** | Dodge sideways (not backward) to avoid her tail swipe. |

**Target clear time:** Under 2 minutes. **Estimated parry opportunities:** 3-4.

#### Boss 2: Axe (Ankylosaur)

| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **1** | Switch to the Fossil Greatsword. Apply the Fossilized debuff with one or two hits. |
| **2** | Parry Axe's charge attack. The wind-up is long and telegraphed by a ground stomp. |
| **3** | After the slow debuff is applied, switch to Claws and target the legs. |

**Target clear time:** Under 4 minutes. **Key note:** Axe's club tail sweep has a deceptive hitbox; roll toward him (under the tail) to avoid it.

#### Boss 3: Kasei the Tyrant

| Priority | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **1** | Save both Estus charges for phase 2 and phase 3. Do not heal in phase 1 unless absolutely necessary. |
| **2** | Use Alpha Roar before the fight starts and again at the start of phase 2. |
| **3** | Parry Kasei's stomp attack (phase 1) and his bite rush (phase 2). Phase 3 has no parryable attacks — focus on dodging. |
| **4** | In phase 3, Kasei gains a fire breath AoE. Sprint perpendicular to the cone, then close distance during the recovery. |

**Target clear time:** Under 6 minutes. **Healing plan:** 1 Estus after phase 1 transition damage; 1 Estus in phase 3 if needed.

## Rewards

Each reward is unlocked cumulatively — you do not lose progress between runs.

| Reward | Requirement | Type | Description |
|--------|------------|------|-------------|
| **Ancient Crown** | S-rank once | Cosmetic headpiece | A crown of fossilized bone and crystal. Equippable on any character. |
| **Eternal Flame** | S-rank 3 times | Weapon enchantment | Applies permanent fire damage equal to 15% of base weapon damage. Stacks with other fire sources. |
| **Gold Trophy** | S-rank once | Steam achievement | "Boss Rush Champion" — Steam achievement with global leaderboard tracking. |
| **Brachiosaur Mount** | S-rank 5 times | Cosmetic mount | A majestic Brachiosaurus mount for roaming hub areas. The rarest cosmetic in the game. |

### Reward Farming Tips

- **S-rank once** for the Ancient Crown and Gold Trophy can be done on Normal difficulty with moderate parry practice.
- **S-rank three times** for Eternal Flame is easier with the fire-infused Claws build (the enchantment stacks).
- **S-rank five times** for the Brachiosaur Mount is a significant challenge. Consider doing these runs on NG+ after acquiring the Volcanic Hammer and Soul Reaper.

## Advanced Techniques

### Speed Runner Route

For players chasing leaderboard times:

1. **Alpha Roar** before entering each boss fog gate (the buff persists through the transition).
2. **Skip the opening cutscene** by pressing any attack key during the first frame.
3. **Use the Ancient Soul summon** on Kasei phase 2 for a massive damage burst that can skip half the phase.
4. **Do not loot drops** — no loot spawns in Boss Rush, so there is nothing to pick up.

### Safe Route (First S-Rank)

If you prioritize safety over speed:

1. **Two-hand the Fossil Greatsword** for the entire run. The slow debuff makes every boss more manageable, though kill times are longer.
2. **Play defensively in phase 3 of Kasei.** Wait for his bite attack (the safest punish window) rather than chasing him.
3. **Use all three Estus if needed.** The score penalty for healing is zero — only damage taken before healing matters.

### Practice Mode

You can practice Boss Rush bosses individually by starting a Boss Rush run and exiting after the first boss. Your progress toward the current boss does not save, but the practice helps. Alternatively, fight each boss in the main game on NG+ until you can beat them without taking damage.
