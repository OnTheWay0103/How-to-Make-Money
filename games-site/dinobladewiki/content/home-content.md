## What is Dinoblade?

Dinoblade is a dinosaur-themed Soulslike action RPG developed by Team Spino LLC. Step into the role of a dinosaur hunter, wielding weapons held in your dinosaur companion's mouth as you fight through five distinct prehistoric biomes. The game features stamina-based combat, light and heavy attacks, rolling with invincibility frames, a deep parry system, and massive boss encounters including Kira the Exile, Axe the Ankylosaur, and Kasei the Tyrant.

With over 500,000 wishlists on Steam, Dinoblade is one of the most anticipated indie action RPGs of 2026. The game launches on July 23, 2026 on PC (Steam) for $29.99.

## Key Systems at a Glance

- **6 Weapons** — Greatsword, Battle Axe, War Hammer, Scythe, Spear, Claws (plus 3 hidden weapons)
- **Soulslike Combat** — Stamina bar, light/heavy attacks, roll i-frames, parry with active frame windows
- **5 Biomes** — Jurassic Plains, Ancient Forest, Volcanic Wastes, Crystal Caverns, Summit
- **8 Bosses** — 6 story bosses + 2 optional secret bosses
- **Ex-Mod Upgrades** — Modify weapons with elemental damage, lifesteal, and special effects
- **Special Skills** — Unique per weapon (Meteor Slice, Whirlwind Spin, etc.) plus Alpha Roar buff
- **Boss Soul Summon** — Call defeated bosses for devastating attacks
- **2-Player Co-op** — Full campaign playable solo or with a friend
- **New Game+** — Increased difficulty with exclusive rewards
- **Boss Rush Mode** — Endgame gauntlet with S-rank scoring
