## Co-op in Sephiria

Sephiria supports **1-4 player online co-op**. In co-op, enemy health and damage scale with player count, but so do relic drops and gold rewards. Coordination separates a successful team from a wipe.

### How Co-op Scaling Works

| Players | Enemy HP | Enemy Damage | Loot Multiplier |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 100% | 100% | 1x |
| 2 | 160% | 140% | 1.5x |
| 3 | 220% | 180% | 2x |
| 4 | 280% | 220% | 2.5x |

## Optimal Team Composition

A well-balanced 4-player team covers all situations. The ideal composition:

### Recommended 4-Player Setup

| Role | Weapon | Build | Responsibility |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tank** | Sword (Paladin) | Defensive, healing auras | Draw aggro, protect allies, revive safely |
| **Support** | Spear (Phalanx) | Team buffs, enemy debuffs | Boost team damage, keep enemies at bay |
| **DPS** | Bow (Elemental) | Status effects, sustained damage | Consistent damage, apply debuffs |
| **Burst** | Staff (Fire/Arcane) | High AoE, room clear | Clear mobs, burst boss phases |

### 3-Player Alternate Composition

| Role | Weapon |
|---|---|
| Tank | Sword or Spear |
| DPS | Bow or Staff |
| Hybrid | Fist (aggressive) or Scythe (AoE) |

### 2-Player Recommended Pairs

| Pair | Synergy |
|---|---|
| Sword (tank) + Staff (AoE) | Tank holds aggro, staff AoEs freely |
| Bow (ranged) + Spear (reach) | Both keep distance, total battlefield control |
| Fist (rushdown) + Scythe (cleanup) | Fist stuns, Scythe finishes — high risk, high reward |

## The Revive System

When a teammate's HP hits zero, they enter a **downed state**:
- A **revive icon** appears above their body
- **Revive takes 3 seconds** of uninterrupted interaction
- **Enemies can hit you during revive** — the tank must clear space
- If fully killed (HP depletes during downed state), they are eliminated for the room

### Revive Priority

1. **Tank revives DPS** — Tank has the HP to tank hits during revive
2. **Support revives tank** — Support's reach keeps enemies back
3. **DPS covers the reviver** — Bow or Staff can fire while another player revives

## Relic Sharing in Co-op

Some relics provide **team-wide effects** — these are far more valuable in co-op than solo.

| Team Relic | Effect | Must Have? |
|---|---|---|
| **Courage Amulet** | Damage reduction for all nearby allies | Yes |
| **Guardian's Aura** | Share 15% of defense with team | Yes |
| **Banner of Unity** | 10% damage bonus when all 4 are alive | Highly recommended |
| **Sanctuary Seal** | Healing aura from any ally's block | Very strong |
| **Inspiring Melody** | Nearby ally kills restore 2% HP | Good sustain |
| **Shared Fortune** | Gold earned is shared at 120% | Economic boost |

## Communication is Key

### Pre-Run Planning

Before entering the tower, decide:

1. **Who carries the healing relics** — Designate 1-2 players for team sustain
2. **Who handles secret rooms** — Assign an explorer role
3. **Boss fight roles** — Who kites, who damages, who revives
4. **Loot distribution** — Who gets priority on weapon upgrades vs relics

### In-Run Callouts

Essential callouts in co-op:
- **"Down!"** — You're downed, need revive
- **"Secret room!"** — Hidden room found
- **"Elite!"** — Strong enemy spotted
- **"Vendor!"** — Shop found, check gold
- **"Boss room!"** — Last room, prepare

## Co-op Boss Strategies

| Boss | Co-op Strategy |
|---|---|
| Golemnir | Tank holds aggro, others attack arms from behind |
| Whisperia | Assign 1 player to hunt the real Whisperia while others clear clones |
| Bibliarch | One player focuses minions, three focus boss |
| Umbrawisp | Light sources from all players overlap, revealing boss constantly |
| Infernis | Spread out — don't cluster during lava breath |
| Sephiria | Assign phase roles — Light form team and Dark form team |
