## Speedrunning Sephiria

Sephiria's procedural tower layout and deep combat system make it an excellent speedrun game. A well-optimized run can clear all 6 chapters in under 15 minutes. This guide covers optimal routes, weapon choices, boss skip techniques, and the current speedrun meta.

## Recommended Speedrun Loadout

### Best Weapons for Speed

| Weapon | Speedrun Tier | Why |
|--------|---------------|-----|
| **Bow** | S-Tier | Best for speed. High damage from range, fast room clear with piercing arrows, safe boss fights. |
| **Spear** | A-Tier | Excellent crowd control, long reach clears rooms quickly. Slightly slower boss kill speed. |
| **Sword** | A-Tier | Balanced. Good for both room clear and boss damage. Requires more positioning skill. |
| **Scythe** | B-Tier | High damage but slow animations. Good for experienced speedrunners only. |
| **Fist (Gauntlets)** | C-Tier | Too dependent on building momentum. Inconsistent for speed. |
| **Daggers** | B-Tier | Fast but short range. Requires precise play and good relic RNG. |

**Recommendation:** Start speedrunning with the **Bow**. Its safety and room-clearing speed make it the most forgiving option while still achieving top-tier times.

### Essential Relics for Speed

Priority relics to look for during a speedrun:

1. **Feather Step** — Increases movement speed by 20%. The single best relic for speed.
2. **Berserker's Ring** — Damage boost when health is low. High risk, high reward.
3. **Haste Idol** — Reduces weapon charge time by 15%. Significant DPS increase.
4. **Glass Cannon** — Doubles damage, halves health. Speedrun staple for experienced players.
5. **Echo Stone** — Attacks have a 25% chance to chain to a second target. Excellent for room clear.

### Recommended Inscriptions

- **Agility** (movement speed) — Mandatory for speed
- **Ferocity** (crit damage) — Faster boss kills
- **Momentum** (damage after dodge) — Synergizes with movement-focused play

## Chapter Routing

### Route Philosophy

In a speedrun, **don't full clear**. Only visit rooms on the critical path. Learn to identify the boss room marker (a large door icon on the minimap) and navigate toward it directly.

### Chapter 1 — Rooted Depths (Target: 1:30–2:00)

- Skip the tutorial dialogue by pressing confirm immediately
- Take the first weapon offered — don't waste time rerolling
- Clear only the minimum rooms needed to reach the boss
- **Skip all chests** unless they're directly on your path
- **Boss:** Root Guardian. Stay close and strafe left. Weak to fire damage.

### Chapter 2 — Whispering Halls (Target: 2:00–2:30)

- Watch for the guaranteed relic room on the left path at the midpoint
- Ranged enemies here are the main time loss — use piercing attacks to clear groups
- **Skip the trap rooms** — the time investment isn't worth the relic reward
- **Boss:** Whisper Witch. Save your dodge for her AoE scream attack. Burst her down in phase 2.

### Chapter 3 — Mirrored Library (Target: 2:30–3:00)

- The layout has more branches. Memorize the left-path bias — the boss room most often spawns on the left side
- Don't fight the elite book guardians unless you need a relic upgrade for later chapters
- **Boss:** Archivist. His projectiles are predictable. Stay at mid-range and strafe.

### Chapter 4 — Crystal Spire (Target: 3:00–3:30)

- Enemy density spikes here. Don't stop moving — kiting is faster than fighting each group
- Use chokepoints to group enemies, then AoE them down
- **Boss:** Crystal Knight. Aggressive — use i-frames through his charge attack.
- Phase 2 is the danger zone. Save your burst cooldowns for this phase.

### Chapter 5 — Howling Abyss (Target: 3:00–4:00)

- The longest chapter. Enemy HP scales up significantly
- **Skip optional challenges** — the time penalty isn't worth the relic
- Gravity enemies can pull you into pits. Stay away from edges.
- **Boss:** Abyss Watcher. The hardest boss in a speedrun context. Learn his 3-attack combo and punish the recovery window.

### Chapter 6 — Spire Summit (Target: 3:00–4:00)

- Final chapter. Bring everything you have
- Elite enemies here are mandatory kills — they block progression doors
- **Final Boss:** The First Rabbit. Three phases. Phase 1 is free damage. Phase 2 introduces laser patterns. Phase 3 is a DPS check.
- **Speedrun tip:** Save your best relics and strongest weapon upgrades for this fight. If you have a Glass Cannon build, Phase 3 can be ended in under 10 seconds.

## Boss Skip Techniques

Some bosses have skip strategies used in competitive speedrunning:

- **Root Guardian skip:** If you have Feather Step + Haste Idol, you can burst the Root Guardian before it enters phase 2, saving ~20 seconds.
- **Whisper Witch phase skip:** All urns in the boss room can be destroyed before engaging the Witch. This skips her summon phase entirely.
- **Crystal Knight wall clip:** A precise dodge into the southeast corner can clip through to the exit. This is frame-perfect and not recommended for beginners.

## Practice Routines

1. **Chapter 1 alone** — Practice this until you can complete it in under 1:30 consistently
2. **Boss rush** — Use the practice mode to drill each boss's attack patterns
3. **Full run** — Once individual chapters are clean, link them together
4. **Review recordings** — Watch your runs and identify time loss (hesitation, wrong pathing, unnecessary fighting)
