## Hard Mode in Sephiria 1.0

Introduced with the **1.0 launch on July 31, 2026**, Hard Mode is Sephiria's ultimate challenge — designed for players who have completed the base game and want a significantly tougher experience. This guide covers every new mechanic and how to survive them.

## How to Unlock Hard Mode

Hard Mode unlocks after you complete your first full tower climb (defeat the First Rabbit on any difficulty). Once unlocked, you can select **Hard Mode** from the Chapter Select screen before starting a run.

## What Changes in Hard Mode

### Enemy Changes

| Change | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Enemy HP** | +150% base health |
| **Enemy Damage** | +100% base damage |
| **New Attack Patterns** | Every boss gains 1–2 new attacks |
| **Elite Spawn Rate** | Doubled — elites appear in every room |
| **Enemy Speed** | +25% movement and attack speed |
| **Armored Enemies** | New armored variants appear from Chapter 2 onward |

### Resource Changes

| Change | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Relic Drops** | Reduced by 50% — fewer relics per room |
| **Healing Item Drops** | Reduced by 60% — healing is scarce |
| **Gold Drops** | Reduced by 40% |
| **Weapon Upgrade Drops** | Reduced by 30% |
| **Inscription Drops** | Unchanged — the only reliable upgrade source |

### New Mechanics

**Corruption System** — A corruption meter fills as you take damage. At 50% corruption, your max health is reduced by 25%. At 100%, you begin taking damage over time. Corruption decreases slowly over time but never resets fully without visiting a cleansing shrine.

**Armored Enemies** — Introduced from Chapter 2 onward. These enemies have a blue armor bar that must be broken before they take health damage. Armor regenerates after 5 seconds of not being hit.

**Hard Mode Exclusive Boss Phases** — Every boss has a new phase 3 (previously max phase 2). These phases introduce entirely new attack patterns and arena hazards.

## Recommended Builds for Hard Mode

### The Tank Build (Recommended for First Hard Mode Clear)

**Weapon:** Spear (long reach keeps you safe)
**Relics (priority order):**
1. **Guardian's Shield** — Take 30% less damage while blocking
2. **Regrowth Seed** — Slowly regenerate health between rooms
3. **Iron Heart** — +40% max health
4. **Purifying Crystal** — Reduces corruption gain by 25%
5. **Steadfast Boots** — Cannot be staggered

**Inscriptions:** Fortitude, Vitality, Regeneration

**Strategy:** This build sacrifices damage for survivability. You'll clear rooms slower, but you'll survive the long haul. The Corruption mechanic makes chip damage punishing, so avoiding hits matters more than ever.

### The Glass Cannon (Experienced Players Only)

**Weapon:** Bow (maximum distance)
**Relics:**
1. **Berserker's Ring** — +50% damage when below 50% health
2. **Glass Cannon** — Double damage, half health
3. **Haste Idol** — 15% faster attacks
4. **Echo Stone** — 25% chain chance
5. **Feather Step** — 20% movement speed

**Inscriptions:** Ferocity, Momentum, Execution

**Strategy:** Don't get hit. Literally. With Glass Cannon active, most enemies will one-shot you. This build is for speedrunners and players who have memorized every attack pattern. The payoff is the fastest Hard Mode clears possible.

### The Corruption Resist Build

**Weapon:** Sword (balanced)
**Relics:**
1. **Purifying Crystal** — 25% corruption reduction
2. **Cleansing Heart** — Corruption decays twice as fast
3. **Serpent Ring** — +15% lifesteal
4. **Guardian's Shield** — 30% damage reduction while blocking
5. **Titan Blood** — +25% max health

**Inscriptions:** Vitality, Regeneration, Balance

**Strategy:** Specifically designed to counter the Corruption System. With both Corrupt-reducing relics, you'll rarely hit 50% corruption. This is the safest build for learning Hard Mode patterns.

## Chapter-by-Chapter Hard Mode Tips

### Chapter 1 — Rooted Depths

The difficulty spike is noticeable but manageable. Enemies have more HP, but their patterns are identical. Use this chapter to build your relic foundation. **Don't skip chests** — you need every relic you can find.

### Chapter 2 — Whispering Halls

Armored enemies debut here. The Spear's charged attack breaks armor in two hits. For the **Whisper Witch boss**, her new phase 3 attack spawns mirror images. Focus on the boss (the image without a reflection).

### Chapter 3 — Mirrored Library

Elite spawn rate doubles here. You should have 4+ relics by this point. The **Archivist** gains a new book-summoning attack in phase 3. Kill the books immediately — they buff the boss's damage.

### Chapter 4 — Crystal Spire

This is the Hard Mode wall for most players. Enemy density + elite spawn rate + armored enemies creates overwhelming situations. Use chokepoints aggressively. The **Crystal Knight's** phase 3 adds room-wide crystal shard rain — use the pillars for cover.

### Chapter 5 — Howling Abyss

Gravity enemies with increased speed are devastating. Do not stand near edges. The **Abyss Watcher's** phase 3 creates a darkness arena — your light radius shrinks to 30%. Listen for audio cues since you can't see attack tells.

### Chapter 6 — Spire Summit

The true Hard Mode test. The **First Rabbit** has a phase 3 with screen-wide laser patterns and summon phases. This is the longest boss fight in the game at around 8-10 minutes. Save all healing items. Memorize the laser rotation pattern — it cycles through 4 fixed configurations.

## General Hard Mode Strategies

1. **Prioritize survivability relics** — Damage is worthless if you're dead
2. **Learn armor break timings** — Armored enemies require sustained pressure
3. **Use cleansing shrines** — They fully reset corruption. Always path toward them.
4. **Never take unnecessary damage** — In Normal mode, minor hits are acceptable. In Hard Mode, every point of health is precious.
5. **Weapon upgrades > relics** — Upgrade your weapon at every opportunity. The +150% enemy HP makes raw damage essential.
6. **Co-op is viable** — Enemy HP scales per player, but having a revive option makes Hard Mode significantly more forgiving.
7. **Practice the new boss patterns** — Use Normal mode to practice phase 3 patterns before attempting Hard Mode.

## Rewards for Completing Hard Mode

- **Cosmetic:** First Rabbit's Golden Crown (account-bound cosmetic)
- **Achievement:** "Hardened" (Steam achievement)
- **Unlock:** Infinite Mode — an endless tower climb with scaling difficulty
- **Unlock:** Hard Mode exclusive relics (3 new relics added to the pool)
