## What Are Relics?

Relics are **passive items** that modify your stats, abilities, and interactions. You can carry up to **6 relics** at a time (upgradeable to 8 via inscriptions). They are found in chests, dropped by enemies, purchased from in-run vendors, or rewarded for completing challenge rooms.

### Rarity Tiers

| Rarity | Color | Drop Rate | How to Unlock |
|---|---|---|---|
| Common | White | Common | Found from Chapter 1 onwards |
| Uncommon | Green | Uncommon | Chapters 2+ |
| Rare | Blue | Rare | Chapters 3+, boss drops |
| Epic | Purple | Very Rare | Chapters 4+, secret rooms |
| Legendary | Orange | Extremely Rare | Chapters 5+, boss rush, secret bosses |

There are **over 150 relics** in the game. Below are the most impactful ones, tiered by general usefulness.

## How to Farm Relics

### Best Relic Farming Methods

| Method | Best Chapter | Relics per Run |
|---|---|---|
| Secret rooms | 4+ | 1-3 high-rarity |
| Challenge rooms | 3+ | 1 guaranteed |
| Boss kills | Any | 1-2 (higher rarity) |
| Vendor shopping | 2+ | Varies (gold cost) |
| Elite enemy farming | 3+ | Uncommon+ |

> **Pro tip:** Equip the **Explorer's Map** relic early in a run to find secret rooms, then swap it out once you've cleared the chapter.

### Unlocking All Relics

Some relics are locked until you complete specific objectives:

- **Chapter boss kills** — Each unique boss drops a specific relic on first kill
- **Achievement rewards** — 20+ relics are tied to achievements
- **Secret boss encounters** — 5 legendary relics come from hidden bosses
- **Difficulty milestones** — Higher difficulties unlock exclusive relic drops
