## What is Sephiria?

Sephiria is a **pixel-art action roguelite** developed by **Sephiria Studio** — a small independent team of 4. You play as a rabbit warrior climbing the endless Tower of Sephiria, fighting through 6 unique chapters against 10+ bosses. The game supports **1-4 player co-op** and has earned a **94% positive rating** on Steam during Early Access.

The **1.0 launch is July 31, 2026**. This guide covers everything you need to get started.

## Core Gameplay Loop

Every run in Sephiria follows the same cycle:

1. **Select a weapon** — Choose from 6 weapon types (each with 50+ upgrades)
2. **Climb the tower** — Fight through procedurally-arranged rooms across 6 chapters
3. **Collect relics** — Over 150 relics modify your stats and abilities
4. **Equip inscriptions** — Socket rune-like inscriptions into your gear for passive bonuses
5. **Defeat the chapter boss** — Each chapter ends with a unique boss encounter
6. **Die or reach the top** — Roguelite permadeath means you restart, but keep permanent upgrades

## Choosing Your First Weapon

As a beginner, weapon choice matters less than you think — all six are viable. However, these three are the most forgiving:

| Weapon | Difficulty | Why It's Good for Beginners |
|---|---|---|
| **Sword** | Easy | Balanced speed and damage. Straightforward combos, reliable block. |
| **Spear** | Easy | Long reach keeps you safe. Excellent for learning enemy spacing. |
| **Bow** | Medium | Ranged attacks let you observe patterns from safety. Resource management is the main challenge. |

Avoid the **Scythe** and **Fist (Gauntlets)** on your first few runs — they require precise positioning and aggressive play that rewards game knowledge.

## First Few Chapters: What to Expect

### Chapter 1 — The Rooted Depths
The easiest chapter. Enemies are slow, attack patterns are telegraphed, and relic drops are plentiful. Take your time exploring every room. Focus on collecting **2-3 relics** before the boss.

### Chapter 2 — The Whispering Halls
Introduces ranged enemies and traps. The difficulty step is moderate. This is where you should start thinking about your **build direction** — what kind of damage type or playstyle you're leaning into.

### Chapter 3 — The Mirrored Library
Boss difficulty spikes here. You should have at least **5 relics** and a **level 3+ weapon upgrade** before attempting the chapter boss. Enemy density increases substantially.

> **Tip for new players:** Don't be afraid to die and restart. Permanent upgrades (unlocked via Inscriptions and character progression) make subsequent runs significantly easier. Dying is learning.

## Essential Survival Tips

| Tip | Details |
|---|---|
| **Dodge, don't block** | Many new players try to block everything. Dodging through attacks gives invincibility frames and is safer. |
| **Explore every room** | Relics and weapon upgrades are hidden in side rooms and optional challenge chambers. |
| **Read relic descriptions** | Some relics have hidden synergies. A relic that seems weak alone might be S-tier with the right combination. |
| **Save healing items** | Don't chug potions after every hit. Save them for boss rooms and emergency situations. |
| **Teamwork in co-op** | In co-op, stick together. The game scales enemy health and damage per player. Splitting up makes dealing with packs much harder. |

## Permanent Progression

Even when you die, you keep:
- **Unlocked inscriptions** — Socket into weapons for passive bonuses
- **Character level** — Increases base stats across all future runs
- **Unlocked relics** — Once discovered, relics appear in future drop pools
- **Weapon mastery** — Using a weapon raises its mastery level, unlocking unique upgrades

This means every run, even failed ones, makes you stronger.
