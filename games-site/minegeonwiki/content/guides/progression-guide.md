## Progression Overview

MineGeon: Renegades progression follows a clear arc: survive your early runs, build up your base, tackle deeper biomes, and eventually conquer The Deep Core. This guide breaks down exactly what to focus on at each stage so you never waste resources or time.

## Stage 1: First Runs (Hours 1–5)

Your goal is simple: extract successfully and gather basic resources.

### Priority Checklist
- [ ] Complete your first extraction (any resources count)
- [ ] Unlock the Workbench and Crafting Station
- [ ] Upgrade your pickaxe to tier 2
- [ ] Build your first storage chest upgrade
- [ ] Try all 3 classes at least once

### What to Mine
Focus exclusively on **Iron, Copper, and Coal** from the Shallow Mines. These are the building blocks of every early upgrade. Ignore gems and rare ores — they're not useful until you have storage and crafting upgrades.

### Gear Targets
- **Pickaxe:** Tier 2 (Iron)
- **Weapon:** Any tier 1 weapon — don't waste resources upgrading weapons yet
- **Armor:** Not needed in early biomes

### Class Recommendation
Start with **Tank**. High health and simple mechanics make it the most forgiving class for learning enemy patterns and extraction timing.

## Stage 2: Base Building (Hours 5–15)

Now that you can extract reliably, shift focus to upgrading your underground HQ.

### Priority Checklist
- [ ] Unlock all 4 core rooms (Workbench, Furnace, Storage, Salvage)
- [ ] Upgrade Workbench to tier 3
- [ ] Upgrade Storage to tier 2
- [ ] Build the Conveyor Belt system
- [ ] Reach Biome 3 (Magma Chambers)
- [ ] Unlock your first class-specific upgrade

### What to Mine
Begin collecting **Silver and Gold** from Fungal Caverns and Magma Chambers. These are needed for mid-tier base upgrades and weapon crafting. Continue mining Iron and Coal — you'll need large quantities.

### Gear Targets
- **Pickaxe:** Tier 3 (Steel)
- **Weapon:** Tier 2 weapon matching your class
- **Armor:** Light armor set (crafted at Workbench)

### Key Milestone
Defeating the **Sporemother** (Biome 2 boss) unlocks the base expansion blueprint. Do this as soon as you have tier 2 weapons and a healing item stockpile of 5+.

## Stage 3: Mid-Game Push (Hours 15–30)

You're comfortable with extraction mechanics. Now it's time to push deeper and start specializing.

### Priority Checklist
- [ ] Unlock all 8 base rooms
- [ ] Upgrade Workbench to tier 4
- [ ] Upgrade Furnace to tier 3
- [ ] Reach Biome 5 (Abandoned Mineshaft)
- [ ] Defeat at least 4 bosses
- [ ] Reach level 15+ with your main class

### What to Mine
**Obsidian, Crystal, and Mythril** are your targets now. Biome 4 (Crystal Mines) and Biome 5 (Abandoned Mineshaft) contain the resources needed for top-tier gear. Gold remains valuable for base upgrades.

### Gear Targets
- **Pickaxe:** Tier 4 (Mythril)
- **Weapon:** Tier 3 specialized weapon
- **Armor:** Full armor set with set bonuses

### Tips for This Stage
- **Build a second storage room** — You'll need the capacity for diverse materials
- **Invest in movement upgrades** — Speed and dodge enhancements dramatically improve survival in Biomes 4–5
- **Start co-op runs** — Bosses in Biomes 4+ scale in complexity, not just stats. A partner makes a huge difference

## Stage 4: Late Game (Hours 30–45)

You've seen most of what MineGeon offers. Now it's about optimization and the final boss.

### Priority Checklist
- [ ] Reach The Deep Core (Biome 6)
- [ ] Upgrade all base stations to max tier
- [ ] Defeat the Core Entity
- [ ] Reach max level with your main class
- [ ] Complete all base upgrades
- [ ] Start a secondary class

### What to Mine
**Diamond and Void Crystal** from The Deep Core. These are used for max-tier upgrades and the best weapons in the game. They're rare — expect multiple Deep Core runs to gather enough.

### Gear Targets
- **Pickaxe:** Tier 5 (Diamond) — max tier
- **Weapon:** Tier 4 legendary weapon
- **Armor:** Max tier armor with optimal enchantments

### The Core Entity
The final boss requires flawless execution. Bring:
- 10+ high-tier healing items
- Full max-tier armor
- Your best weapon with optimal upgrades
- A co-op partner if possible

## Stage 5: Endgame (Hours 45+)

After defeating the Core Entity, the game opens up for completionists.

### Post-Game Goals
- **100% Achievement hunting** — 42 achievements total (see our Achievements Guide)
- **Max all classes** — Experience each class at full potential
- **Perfect your base layout** — Maximum efficiency rating
- **Speedrun optimization** — Minimize your extraction times
- **Help new players in co-op** — Many achievements require multiplayer

### Efficiency Tips for Endgame
- **Bulk craft consumables** — Save time by crafting 20+ healing items at once
- **Optimize your route** — Memorize extraction point locations for each biome
- **Specialize loadouts** — Create different gear sets for mining, boss fights, and speedrunning
- **Use the conveyor system** — Automated resource sorting saves enormous time
