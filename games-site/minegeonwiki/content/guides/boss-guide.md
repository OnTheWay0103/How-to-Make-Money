## Boss Overview

MineGeon: Renegades features **12+ unique bosses** spread across 6 biomes. Each boss has distinct attack patterns, weaknesses, and valuable loot drops. This guide covers every boss and how to beat them.

## Biome 1: The Shallow Mines

### Crystal Crawler Queen
- **Location:** Deep chamber in Biome 1
- **HP:** Medium
- **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ (Easy — tutorial boss)

**Attack Patterns:**
1. **Crystal Spray** — Fires crystal shards in a cone. Dodge sideways.
2. **Burrow Charge** — Burrows underground and charges at you. Watch for ground rumble indicator.
3. **Summon Crawlers** — Spawns 3-4 small Crawler adds. Kill them quickly.

**Strategy:**
- Stay at medium range to react to both charge and spray
- Clear adds immediately — they can overwhelm you
- Attack the glowing crystal on its back for bonus damage

**Drops:**
- Crystal Shard (crafting material)
- Crawler Carapace (armor crafting)
- Basic Health Module

## Biome 2: The Fungal Caverns

### Sporemother
- **Location:** Giant mushroom chamber, Biome 2
- **HP:** Medium-High
- **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐

**Attack Patterns:**
1. **Spore Cloud** — Releases a toxic cloud that fills half the room. Move to the clear side.
2. **Tendril Slam** — Slams 3 tentacles in sequence. Dodge roll between slams.
3. **Mushroom Minions** — Sprouts 5-6 exploding mushrooms. Shoot them from distance.

**Strategy:**
- Always know where the clear zone is during Spore Cloud
- The Sporemother takes double damage from fire weapons/abilities
- Destroy exploding mushrooms before they reach you

**Drops:**
- Fungal Core (rare crafting)
- Toxic Gland (poison weapon upgrades)
- Spore Cap (Engineer turret mod)

## Biome 3: The Magma Chambers

### Magma Construct
- **Location:** Lava forge, Biome 3
- **HP:** High
- **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Attack Patterns:**
1. **Lava Wave** — Sends a wave of lava across the arena. Jump over it.
2. **Hammer Smash** — Massive AoE slam. Get far away or behind cover.
3. **Heat Beam** — Continuous laser that tracks you. Sprint perpendicular.
4. **Enrage** — Below 30% HP, attacks speed up 50%.

**Strategy:**
- Bring fire resistance gear if available
- The Construct is weak to ice/water damage
- During Heat Beam, run in one direction — don't zigzag
- Save your burst damage for the Enrage phase

**Drops:**
- Magma Core (legendary crafting)
- Obsidian Shard (armor plating)
- Heat Sink (Engineer overclock module)

## Biome 4: The Crystal Mines

### Gem Guardian
- **Location:** Crystal chamber, Biome 4
- **HP:** Very High
- **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Attack Patterns:**
1. **Crystal Beam** — Fires a refracting beam that bounces off crystals in the room. Track the reflections.
2. **Gem Shield** — Becomes invulnerable; destroy 4 floating crystals to break shield.
3. **Shatter** — Explodes all crystals in the room. Hide behind pillars.
4. **Light Prism** — Splits into 3 copies. Only one is real (glows slightly brighter).

**Strategy:**
- Destroy wall crystals before the fight to limit beam bounces
- During Gem Shield, prioritize the crystals — don't waste ammo on the boss
- The real copy during Light Prism glows brighter — focus it
- Gunslinger's Deadeye is excellent for bursting the real copy

**Drops:**
- Pure Crystal (legendary crafting)
- Prism Shard (weapon mod)
- Guardian Core (Tank shield upgrade)

## Biome 5: The Abandoned Mineshaft

### The Foreman (Mini-Boss)
- **Location:** Random spawn in Biome 5
- **HP:** Medium
- **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐

**Attack Patterns:**
1. **Pickaxe Throw** — Throws a pickaxe that returns. Dodge twice.
2. **Cave-in** — Causes rocks to fall from the ceiling. Watch for shadows.
3. **Rally** — Buffs nearby enemies. Kill him fast.

### Drill Dozer (Main Boss)
- **Location:** End of Biome 5
- **HP:** Massive
- **Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Attack Patterns:**
1. **Drill Charge** — Charges across the arena with a giant drill. Dodge to the side.
2. **Rock Spray** — Drills into the wall, spraying rocks everywhere. Stay behind cover.
3. **Stomp** — 3 ground stomps creating expanding shockwaves. Jump each one.
4. **Berserker Mode** — Below 25% HP, attacks become faster and more frequent.
5. **Reinforcements** — Calls in 2 Elite Miners. Kill them or they'll overwhelm you.

**Strategy:**
- The Drill Dozer is the hardest boss in the game. Come fully prepared.
- Bring at least 8-10 healing items
- Tank should always keep aggro during Berserker Mode
- Focus Elite Miners immediately when they spawn
- Engineer turrets are extremely effective — place them before the fight

**Drops:**
- Drill Core (legendary crafting)
- Foreman's Key (unlocks secret room)
- Reinforced Plating (best armor material)
- Dozer Fragment (ultimate weapon component)

## General Boss Tips

### Preparation Checklist
- [ ] Full HP and at least 5 healing items
- [ ] Weapon at maximum durability
- [ ] Class abilities off cooldown
- [ ] Clear the area of regular enemies first
- [ ] Know the extraction route in case you need to retreat

### Co-op Boss Tips
- Tank should always initiate and maintain aggro
- Gunslinger focuses on consistent DPS from safe angles
- Engineer sets up turrets before pulling the boss
- Communicate during invulnerability phases
- Revive teammates during boss's recovery animations
