## Weapon System Overview

MineGeon: Renegades features a diverse arsenal across multiple weapon categories. Each class has weapon preferences, but many weapons can be used by any class with the right build.

## Weapon Types

### Melee Weapons

| Weapon | Class | Damage | Speed | Range | Special |
|--------|-------|--------|-------|-------|---------|
| **Pickaxe** | All | Low | Medium | Short | Also used for mining |
| **War Hammer** | Tank | High | Slow | Medium | AoE ground slam |
| **Battle Axe** | Tank | High | Medium | Medium | Bleed damage over time |
| **Power Fist** | Tank | Very High | Very Slow | Short | Charged punch, massive damage |
| **Wrench** | Engineer | Medium | Fast | Short | Repairs turrets on hit |

### Ranged Weapons

| Weapon | Class | Damage | Fire Rate | Range | Special |
|--------|-------|--------|-----------|-------|---------|
| **Revolver** | Gunslinger | High | Slow | Long | Headshot bonus damage |
| **Dual Pistols** | Gunslinger | Medium | Fast | Medium | Wider spread, good for crowds |
| **Rifle** | Gunslinger | High | Medium | Very Long | Scope for long-range shots |
| **Shotgun** | Gunslinger | Very High | Slow | Short | Massive close-range damage |
| **SMG** | Gunslinger | Low | Very Fast | Medium | High DPS, burns ammo fast |

### Special Weapons

| Weapon | Class | Damage | Special |
|--------|-------|--------|---------|

| **Tesla Coil Gun** | Engineer | Medium | Chains lightning between enemies |
| **Flame Thrower** | All | Medium DoT | Sets ground on fire, DoT damage |

## Best Weapons Per Class

### Tank — Best Weapons
1. **War Hammer** — Best all-around. Good damage + AoE crowd control.
2. **Battle Axe** — Bleed is excellent against high-HP bosses.
3. **Power Fist** — Highest single-hit damage but requires good timing.

### Gunslinger — Best Weapons
1. **Revolver** — Best for accurate players. Headshot bonus melts bosses.
2. **Rifle** — Safest option. Long range lets you avoid most damage.
3. **Dual Pistols** — Best for clearing rooms with many enemies.

### Engineer — Best Weapons
1. **Wrench** — Synergizes with turrets. Repair + damage in one tool.
2. **Grenade Launcher** — Area denial combos with traps.
3. **Tesla Coil Gun** — Chain lightning is devastating against grouped enemies.

## Weapon Upgrades

Weapons can be upgraded at the Anvil in your base:

| Upgrade Tier | Effect | Material Cost |
|-------------|--------|---------------|
| **+1** | +15% base damage | Iron ×10 |
| **+2** | +25% base damage, +10% attack speed | Silver ×15 |
| **+3** | +35% base damage, unlocks special effect | Gold ×20, Crystal ×5 |
| **+4** | +50% base damage, +20% attack speed | Obsidian ×10 |
| **+5** | +75% base damage, max special effect | Mythril ×5, Diamond ×2 |

### Special Effects (Unlocked at +3)
- **War Hammer:** Shockwave travels 50% further
- **Revolver:** Headshots refund 1 ammo
- **Rifle:** Pierces through 1 additional enemy
- **Wrench:** Turret repair also heals the turret
- **Grenade Launcher:** Leaves burning ground

## Ammo Management

### Ammo Types
- **Light Ammo** — SMG, Dual Pistols (common, cheap)
- **Medium Ammo** — Revolver, Rifle (moderate)
- **Heavy Ammo** — Shotgun, Grenade Launcher (rare, expensive)
- **Energy Cells** — Tesla Coil Gun, Ice Blaster (rare)

### Ammo Tips
- Always bring at least 50% more ammo than you think you need
- Engineer's Supply Drop refills ammo for the whole team
- Craft ammo between runs at the Workbench
- Conserve heavy ammo for bosses and elite enemies
