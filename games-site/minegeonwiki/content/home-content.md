MineGeon: Renegades is a **1-3 player co-op roguelite** developed by WTF Studios, releasing **July 28, 2026** on Steam. The game combines deep mining mechanics with fast-paced combat and a fully customizable base-building system.

Choose from 3 unique classes — **Tank**, **DPS Gunslinger**, and **Engineer** — and dig through 6 procedurally generated biomes filled with resources, enemies, and 12+ epic boss fights. Craft powerful gear, upgrade your base with 200+ decorations, and extract your loot before the mine collapses.

Whether you're going in solo or teaming up with friends, this unofficial wiki has everything you need: class builds, boss strategies, crafting recipes, biome guides, and mining tips to help you survive and thrive in the mines.
