# Skills & Raids Raid Progression & Level Guide — Full Walkthrough

**Skills & Raids Open Beta / Updated: July 18, 2026**

Not sure what level to attempt your first raid? Confused about unlock conditions for endgame content? This guide maps out the complete raid progression path — from your first Foothills run to the endgame Void Temple — including exact level requirements, gear score recommendations, and a preparation checklist for each tier.

---

## 1. Raid Unlock System Overview

Raids in Skills & Raids unlock progressively as you complete objectives and reach account milestones. You cannot rush to endgame — each raid tier has strict unlock conditions.

### Core Unlock Mechanics

| Requirement Type | Examples | How to Progress |
|-----------------|----------|-----------------|
| Account Level | Reach level 5, 10, 15, etc. | Complete raids, extract with loot |
| Prior Raid Completion | Clear the previous raid zone | Defeat the zone boss at least once |
| Skill Milestone | Equip X Rare skills or higher | Upgrade skills, open loot chests |
| Gear Score | Reach minimum gear threshold | Equip better gear, craft upgrades |

## 2. Complete Raid Tier Breakdown

### Tier 1: The Foothills (Beginner)

**Recommended Level:** 1-5 | **Gear Score:** None required | **Enemy Level:** 1-3

| Detail | Information |
|--------|------------|
| **Unlock Condition** | Complete the tutorial (automatic) |
| **Zone Length** | 4-6 encounter rooms |
| **Boss** | Bandit Leader (Level 5) |
| **Loot Drops** | Common (white) gear, Common/Uncommon skills |
| **Recommended Comp** | Balanced Trio (any Tank + Heal) |
| **First Clear Reward** | Uncommon DPS skill + 500 gold |

**Preparation Checklist:**
- [ ] Equip at least 3 Common skills per character
- [ ] Bring 2 healing consumables
- [ ] Learn extraction point locations on the map
- [ ] Set an inventory limit (don't overstay)

**Progression goal:** Clear the Bandit Leader boss to unlock Timberland. Farm Foothills until you have full Uncommon skill sets for your starter team.

---

### Tier 2: Timberland (Early Game)

**Recommended Level:** 5-10 | **Gear Score:** 50+ | **Enemy Level:** 4-7

| Detail | Information |
|--------|------------|
| **Unlock Condition** | Defeat Bandit Leader (Foothills boss) |
| **Zone Length** | 6-8 encounter rooms |
| **Boss** | Forest Troll (Level 8) |
| **Loot Drops** | Uncommon (green) gear, Rare (blue) skills (rare) |
| **Recommended Comp** | Balanced Trio with Cleanse |
| **First Clear Reward** | Rare Support skill + 1,000 gold |

**New mechanics:** Timberland introduces **poison debuffs** from Venom Spitter enemies. Your Support must have Cleanse equipped or you will lose HP every turn.

**Preparation Checklist:**
- [ ] All characters should have Uncommon skills in every slot
- [ ] Support must have Cleanse or Group Mend
- [ ] Tank should have Uncommon Shield Wall (minimum)
- [ ] Bring 3-4 healing consumables
- [ ] Craft at least one piece of Uncommon gear per character

**Progression goal:** Farm Timberland until your team has 2-3 Rare skills total. Defeat Forest Troll to unlock Dark Caverns.

---

### Tier 3: Dark Caverns (Mid Game)

**Recommended Level:** 10-15 | **Gear Score:** 100+ | **Enemy Level:** 8-12

| Detail | Information |
|--------|------------|
| **Unlock Condition** | Defeat Forest Troll (Timberland boss) |
| **Zone Length** | 8-10 encounter rooms |
| **Boss** | Shadow Wisp (Level 13) |
| **Loot Drops** | Rare (blue) gear, Rare/Epic (purple) skills |
| **Recommended Comp** | Control Composition (Tank + Controller + Support) |
| **First Clear Reward** | Epic Weapon skill + 2,000 gold |

**New mechanics:** Dark Caverns has **darkness zones** that reduce accuracy. Shadow Wisp summons adds that must be stunned or killed quickly. This is the first raid where the Control composition becomes strongly recommended.

**Preparation Checklist:**
- [ ] At least 3 Rare skills distributed across your team
- [ ] Controller with Stun and Slow (minimum Uncommon)
- [ ] Tank with Rare Shield Wall (absorbs 2x damage)
- [ ] Accuracy consumables for darkness zones
- [ ] Gear score minimum 100
- [ ] All characters level 10+

**Progression goal:** Farm Dark Caverns until your main team has 4-5 Rare skills. Defeat Shadow Wisp to unlock Dragon's Peak.

---

### Tier 4: Dragon's Peak (Late Game)

**Recommended Level:** 15-20 | **Gear Score:** 200+ | **Enemy Level:** 13-18

| Detail | Information |
|--------|------------|
| **Unlock Condition** | Defeat Shadow Wisp (Dark Caverns boss) |
| **Zone Length** | 10-12 encounter rooms |
| **Boss** | Ember Drake (Level 18) |
| **Loot Drops** | Epic (purple) gear, Epic/Legendary (gold) skills |
| **Recommended Comp** | Double DPS Speed Clear (Normal) / Turtle (Hard) |
| **First Clear Reward** | Legendary skill shard + 5,000 gold |

**New mechanics:** Dragon's Peak introduces **elemental damage** (fire). Boss applies a stacking burn debuff. This raid has two difficulty modes — Normal and Hard.

**Preparation Checklist (Normal):**
- [ ] Full Rare skill sets, at least 2 Epic skills
- [ ] Fire resistance consumables (crafted from Timberland materials)
- [ ] Gear score minimum 200
- [ ] DPS with Armor Break + Power Strike
- [ ] Support with Group Mend + Cleanse

**Hard Mode adds:**
- Gear score minimum 350
- 3+ Epic skills per character
- Turtle or Control composition strongly recommended
- Bring 6+ consumables per character

**Progression goal:** Farm Dragon's Peak (Normal) for Epic gear. Defeat Ember Drake to unlock Void Temple. Hard mode is optional but rewards Legendary skill shards.

---

### Tier 5: Void Temple (Endgame)

**Recommended Level:** 20-25 | **Gear Score:** 400+ | **Enemy Level:** 20-25

| Detail | Information |
|--------|------------|
| **Unlock Condition** | Defeat Ember Drake (Dragon's Peak boss) |
| **Zone Length** | 12-15 encounter rooms |
| **Boss** | Void Lich (Level 25) |
| **Loot Drops** | Epic/Legendary (gold) gear, Legendary skills |
| **Recommended Comp** | Control Composition (optimized) |
| **First Clear Reward** | Unique Legendary skill + Title unlock |

**New mechanics:** Void Temple has **phased boss fights** — the Void Lich transforms at 75%, 50%, and 25% HP, gaining new abilities each phase. Requires precise execution and full team coordination.

**Preparation Checklist:**
- [ ] Full Epic+ skill sets on all characters
- [ ] At least 1 Legendary skill in your main damage dealer
- [ ] Gear score minimum 400 (500+ recommended)
- [ ] All characters level 20+
- [ ] 8+ consumables per character
- [ ] Full synergy-optimized team composition
- [ ] Study boss phase transitions in advance

## 3. Difficulty Tiers Explained

Skills & Raids uses a hidden difficulty rating per raid zone. Here is how it maps out:

| Difficulty Label | Zones | Enemy Damage Multiplier | Loot Quality |
|-----------------|-------|------------------------|--------------|
| Easy (1 skull) | Foothills | 1.0x | Common/Uncommon |
| Medium (2 skulls) | Timberland | 1.3x | Uncommon/Rare |
| Hard (3 skulls) | Dark Caverns | 1.6x | Rare/Epic |
| Very Hard (4 skulls) | Dragon's Peak (Normal) | 2.0x | Epic |
| Extreme (5 skulls) | Dragon's Peak (Hard) | 2.5x | Epic/Legendary |
| Nightmare (5 skulls+) | Void Temple | 3.0x | Legendary+ |

**Difficulty tells:** The skull icon on the raid select screen is your warning. Never attempt a **2-skull jump** above your current gear score — you will get one-shot.

## 4. Gear Score Progression Path

Your gear score is the numerical shorthand for your character's overall power. Here is the optimal gear score progression:

| Raid Tier | Minimum GS | Comfortable GS | Optimal GS |
|-----------|-----------|----------------|------------|
| Foothills | None | None | 30+ |
| Timberland | 50 | 75 | 100 |
| Dark Caverns | 100 | 150 | 200 |
| Dragon's Peak (Normal) | 200 | 300 | 400 |
| Dragon's Peak (Hard) | 350 | 450 | 550 |
| Void Temple | 400 | 550 | 700+ |

**How to increase gear score:**
1. **Craft gear** using materials from raids (best source)
2. **Equip higher rarity skills** — skills contribute to effective gear score
3. **Upgrade existing gear** at the blacksmith
4. **Complete milestone achievements** for stat boosts

## 6. Common Progression Mistakes

| Mistake | Consequence | How to Fix |
|---------|------------|-----------|
| Skipping Timberland boss gear | Undergeared for Dark Caverns | Farm Timberland until comfortable |
| Rushing to Dragon's Peak at level 12 | Get one-shot by Ember Drake | Wait until level 15+ with gear score 200+ |
| Ignoring consumables | Boss DOT kills through heals | Always bring tier-appropriate consumables |
| Wrong comp for zone | Wipe on first adds | Check recommended comps above |
| Not farming previous tier | Starved for resources | Each tier is a resource gate, not just a boss gate |
