# Skills & Raids Crafting Guide — Full Crafting System Explained

**Skills & Raids Open Beta / Updated: July 14, 2026**

Crafting converts raw extracted materials into weapons, armor, consumables, and skill upgrades. Master it and you'll consistently outgear your enemies.

---

## Crafting Basics

Access the crafting menu from the main hub (between raids). You can craft:

| Category | Examples | Materials Needed |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| Weapons | Swords, Axes, Staves, Bows | Metal Ore + Wood + Rare Gem (for Rare+) |
| Armor | Helmets, Chestplates, Boots, Shields | Metal Ore + Leather + Cloth |
| Consumables | Health Potions, Elixirs, Buff Food | Herbs + Monster Parts + Water |
| Skill Upgrades | Upgrade skill rarity by 1 tier | Duplicate skill + Essence + Gold |

### Metal Ore (Weapons & Armor)
| Material | Rarity | Best Farm Location | Drop Rate |
|----------|--------|-------------------|-----------|

## Recipe Unlock System

Recipes unlock as you progress:

| Recipe Tier | Unlock Condition | Example Recipes |
|-------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Basic | Default unlocked | Iron Sword, Leather Armor, Small Potion |
| Improved | Craft 10 Basic items | Steel Sword, Chainmail, Medium Potion |
| Advanced | Kill first Boss | Mithril Sword, Plate Armor, Large Potion |
| Master | Extract from Crystal Depths | Orichalcum weapons, Elixirs |
| Legendary | Complete all raids | Legendary gear recipes |

## Maximizing Rarity Outcomes

When crafting gear, the outcome rarity depends on material quality:

| Materials Used | Outcome Rarity | Chance |
|---------------|----------------|--------|
| All Common | Common | 90% |
| All Common | Uncommon | 10% |
| 50% Uncommon + 50% Common | Uncommon | 70% |
| 50% Uncommon | Rare | 30% |
| All Rare | Rare | 80% |
| All Rare | Epic | 20% |
| All Epic | Epic | 90% |
| All Epic | Legendary | 10% |

**Pro tip:** Using even one Rare material in a Common recipe increases the Uncommon+ chance by ~15%. Always mix in your best materials.

## Crafting Priority (What to Make First)

| Priority | Item | Why |
|----------|------|-----|
| 1 | **Health Potions** | Mandatory for boss fights. Craft 5+ before attempting bosses. |
| 2 | **Tank Armor** | Keeping your tank alive keeps your whole team alive. |
| 3 | **DPS Weapon** | Faster kills = fewer enemy turns = less damage taken. |
| 4 | **Healer Armor** | Healer dying is a raid-ender. |
| 5 | **Elixirs** | Boss-specific buffs (Fire resist, Poison immune, etc.) |

## Gold Economy

Gold is used for crafting fees and skill upgrades:

| Action | Gold Cost |
|--------|-----------|
| Craft Common item | 50 |
| Craft Uncommon item | 200 |
| Craft Rare item | 800 |
| Craft Epic item | 3,000 |
| Upgrade skill to Uncommon | 500 |
| Upgrade skill to Rare | 2,000 |
| Upgrade skill to Epic | 8,000 |

**Best gold farm:** Speed-run Foothills with a Double DPS comp, extract in ~5 minutes, sell all Common loot.
