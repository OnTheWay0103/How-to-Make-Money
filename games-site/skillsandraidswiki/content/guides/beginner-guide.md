# Skills & Raids Beginner Guide — Getting Started

**Skills & Raids Open Beta / Updated: July 14, 2026**

Welcome to Skills & Raids, a team-based extraction RPG by solo developer Lootsurvivor. Build your squad, mix skills across 5 rarity tiers, fight through raid maps, and extract alive. This guide covers your first raids and everything you need to avoid wiping.

---

## 1. Core Gameplay Loop

Skills & Raids combines two genres:

- **Tactical Turn-Based Combat** — Position your team, chain skills together, exploit enemy weaknesses
- **Extraction Mechanics** — Enter a raid, loot enemies and chests, reach an extraction point. Die = lose most items

The loop: **Prepare Loadout → Deploy to Raid → Fight & Loot → Extract (or Wipe) → Upgrade → Repeat**

---

## 2. Building Your First Team

### Recommended Beginner Composition

| Role | Focus | Skill Types | Priority |
|------|-------|-------------|----------|
| Tank | Shield / Taunt / Defense | Protection | High |
| DPS | Damage / Crit / Penetration | Offense | Medium |
| Support | Heal / Buff / Cleanse | Restoration | Medium |

**Why a balanced trio?** A tank draws aggro, DPS eliminates threats, support keeps everyone alive. Synergy matters more than individual power — a coordinated Common-rarity team beats a mismatched Epic-rarity squad.

### First Skills to Equip

Start with Common (white) and Uncommon (green) skills. Focus on skills that **combo** — look for skill descriptions that mention "triggers synergy with [skill type]":

- **Shield Wall** (Tank) + **Holy Light** (Support) = Shield+Heal combo
- **Power Strike** (DPS) + **Mark Target** (Tank) = Damage amplification

## 3. Your First Raid Walkthrough

### Step 1: Preparation
- Equip at least 3 skills per team member
- Bring 1-2 healing consumables (crafted or purchased)
- Check the raid map for extraction point locations

### Step 2: Early Map (Foothills)
The Foothills is the starter raid zone. Enemies are level 1-3, with one mini-boss (Bandit Leader).

**Safe strategy:**
1. Clear the first 2-3 enemy groups to build confidence
2. Avoid the marked Boss area until you have better gear
3. Head to extraction when inventory is ~70% full

### Step 3: Extraction
Extraction points are marked on the map as green circles. Stand on the point for 2 turns to extract. **Enemies near extraction points will rush you** — clear them first.

## 4. Common Beginner Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Hurts | Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| No tank in team | DPS and Support get focused down | Always include a tank |
| Ignoring skill synergy | Skills without combos deal 40% less damage | Read skill descriptions, build combos |
| Overstaying in a raid | Greed = wipe. Inventory full? Extract. | Set a loot limit and stick to it |
| Skipping consumables | One heal potion can save a raid | Always bring 2+ healing items |
| Unequipped skills | Empty skill slots = dead weight | Fill all 3 skill slots per character |

## 5. Progression Path (First 10 Hours)

| Milestone | What to Do |
|-----------|------------|
| Raid 1-3 | Foothills — learn basics, extract with Common loot |
| Raid 4-6 | Foothills Boss — kill Bandit Leader, unlock Uncommon drops |
| Raid 7-10 | Timberland — second raid zone, Uncommon/Rare loot |
| Raid 10+ | Craft first Rare gear, start experimenting with skill combos |

---

## 6. Key Tips

- **Extract early, extract often.** Better to leave with 3 items than die with 10.
- **Save Rare+ skills** for boss encounters — don't waste them on trash mobs.
- **Craft between raids.** Raw materials are useless until processed.
- **Check skill synergy tags.** Skills with matching tags create combo effects.
- **The Open Beta has regular updates.** Follow the developer on Steam for patch notes.
