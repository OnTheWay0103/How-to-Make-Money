# Skills & Raids Best Skills & Builds Guide — Ultimate Optimization

**Skills & Raids Open Beta / Updated: July 18, 2026**

Not all skills are created equal. Some skills carry runs while others fill slots until you find better options. This guide ranks every major skill, provides optimized build templates for each role, explains upgrade priority, and covers how to respec and experiment effectively.

---

## 1. Skill Rarity and Power Scaling

Skills in Skills & Raids come in five rarity tiers. The difference between tiers is not just stat numbers — higher rarity skills unlock **bonus effects** and **extended synergy chains**.

| Rarity | Color | Stat Multiplier | Bonus Effects | Drop Source |
|--------|-------|----------------|---------------|-------------|
| Common | White | 1.0x | None | All content |
| Uncommon | Green | 1.3x | One minor bonus | Foothills+ |
| Rare | Blue | 1.6x | One major bonus | Timberland+ |
| Epic | Purple | 2.0x | Two bonuses | Dark Caverns+ |
| Legendary | Gold | 2.5x | Two bonuses + unique effect | Dragon's Peak+ |

**Stat multiplier example:** A Common Power Strike deals 100 base damage. An Epic Power Strike deals 200 base damage with two additional effects (e.g., +30% crit chance, +20% armor penetration).

## 3. Optimal Skill Combinations

### Best 2-Skill Combos

| Combo | Skills | Effect | Best On |
|-------|--------|--------|---------|
| Shield & Heal | Shield Wall + Holy Light | Shielded allies receive 2x healing | Tank + Support |
| Break & Strike | Armor Break + Power Strike | Ignore 50% remaining armor | DPS combo |
| Taunt & Counter | Taunt + Counter Stance | Retaliate on every hit taken | Tank |
| Mark & Crit | Mark Target + Critical Edge | Guaranteed critical hit | DPS burst |
| Slow & Stun | Slow + Stun | Enemies get fewer turns, then skip one | Controller |

### Best 3-Skill Rotation

The most powerful rotation in Skills & Raids for standard encounters:

1. **Turn 1:** Tank uses Taunt → DPS uses Armor Break
2. **Turn 2:** Support uses Haste on DPS → DPS uses Power Strike (with Armor Break active)
3. **Turn 3:** DPS uses Execute (enemy below 30%) → Tank uses Shield Wall (anticipating enemy attack)

**Result:** One dead enemy, aggro on tank, full team HP. This rotation clears most non-boss encounters in 3 turns.

## 5. Skill Upgrade Priority

You cannot upgrade every skill equally. Here is the optimal upgrade order:

### Overall Priority List

1. **Power Strike** (Tank version → DPS version) — Your primary damage tool
2. **Shield Wall** — Your primary survival tool
3. **Holy Light** — Your primary healing tool
4. **Armor Break** — Unlocks 50% more damage from all physical skills
5. **Taunt** — Aggro control
6. **Group Mend** — AOE healing
7. **Cleanse** — Debuff removal
8. **Fortify** — Passive defense
9. **Rest** — Fill as needed

### Upgrade Cost Scaling

| Upgrade Level | Cost | Materials Required |
|--------------|------|-------------------|
| Common → Uncommon | 500 gold | 3 Common essence |
| Uncommon → Rare | 2,000 gold | 3 Uncommon essence |
| Rare → Epic | 8,000 gold | 3 Rare essence + 1 Epic shard |
| Epic → Legendary | 25,000 gold | 3 Epic essence + 3 Legendary shards |

## 6. How to Respec and Experiment

### Respec Options

Skills & Raids offers two ways to change your skill loadout:

| Method | Cost | Effect | When to Use |
|--------|------|--------|-------------|
| Free Equip | 0 gold | Swap any equipped skill for another in your inventory | Always, whenever you want |
| Skill Extraction | Varies | Extract a skill from gear, destroying the gear | When you have spare gear |
| Full Reset | 5,000 gold | Reset all skill upgrades to Common, refund 80% of materials | When your build is outdated |

**Key insight:** You can swap skills freely between raids with no penalty. There is no reason to stick with a bad build. Experiment between every raid run.

### How to Test Builds

1. **Run Foothills** (low risk) with any new build to test synergy
2. **Check your extraction rate** — if it drops below 70%, the build needs work
3. **Record synergy triggers** — note which skill combos actually fire during combat
4. **Swap one skill at a time** so you know exactly what changed

## 8. Common Build Mistakes

| Mistake | Why It Hurts | Fix |
|---------|-------------|-----|
| Over-investing in Common skills | Wasted materials upgrading skills you'll replace | Save upgrades for Uncommon+ skills |
| Ignoring skill synergy | 30-40% damage loss from missed combos | Check synergy tags before equipping |
| Same skills on every character | Redundancy reduces team flexibility | Each role should have unique skills |
| No Cleanse in mid-game | Boss DOT destroys your team | Always bring Cleanse from Timberland onward |
| Rushing Legendary upgrades | Extremely expensive, slow progression | Upgrade core Rare skills first |
