## What is Echoes of Mystralia?

Echoes of Mystralia is an action-heavy spellcrafting ARPG roguelite developed by Borealys Games, the studio behind Mages of Mystralia (2017). You play as Mazarim, a Watcher protecting the Cycle of Memories from a mysterious Rift threatening to consume the world.

The game's defining feature is the spell crafting system — combine fire, ice, and lightning elements with Memory modifiers collected from defeated enemies. The positioning of components within a spell form's grid determines the output, enabling millions of unique combinations.

Each run sends you through procedurally varied regions filled with enemies, culminating in Guardian boss fights. Between runs, return to the Azimuth Temple to unlock permanent Lotus upgrades, customize your spell loadout, and interact with NPCs including a millennia-old goddess.

## Key Systems at a Glance

- **Spell Crafting** — 12 spell forms × 3 elements × 110 Memories = millions of combinations
- **110 Memories** — Common to Legendary modifiers dropped by enemies
- **30+ Relics** — Passive items with build-defining synergies
- **Lotus Progression** — Permanent upgrade system spanning 40+ tiers
- **3 Regions** — Ash Forest, Frozen Depths, Stormspire (Early Access)
- **3 Guardian Bosses** — Therakan + 2 more at EA launch
- **Elemental Rock-Paper-Scissors** — Fire beats Ice, Ice beats Lightning, Lightning beats Fire
- **Hybrid Spell Crafting** — Combine elements for Steam Burst, Plasma Storm, Frost Storm, Tempest
