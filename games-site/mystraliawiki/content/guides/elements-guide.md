# Elements Guide — Fire, Ice & Lightning Mastery

Echoes of Mystralia's combat revolves around three elements: **Fire, Ice, and Lightning**. Understanding their mechanics, interactions, and matchups is essential for mastering the game.

## Element Overview

| Element | Primary Effect | Secondary Effect | Best Role |
|---------|---------------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Fire 🔥** | Damage over Time (Burn) | Spreads between nearby enemies | Boss DPS, sustained damage |
| **Ice ❄️** | Slow → Freeze | Shatter (bonus damage to frozen) | Crowd control, survivability |
| **Lightning ⚡** | Chain between enemies | Brief stun on chain hits | AoE clear, speed farming |

---

## Fire 🔥 — Deep Dive

### Burn Mechanic

- Burn deals **8% of the spell's damage per second** for 4 seconds
- Burn **stacks up to 3 times** from different spells
- Burn **spreads** to nearby enemies when the burning target dies
- Ignite Memories increase burn damage and duration

### When to Use Fire

| Scenario | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Boss fights | Sustained burn damage adds up to significant bonus DPS |
| Ice enemies | Elemental advantage — 50% bonus damage |
| Tanky elite packs | Burn ticks while you dodge |
| Long fights | Burn's value increases with fight duration |

### Fire Memory Priorities

1. **Ignite** — increases burn chance and damage
2. **Amplify** — bigger hits = bigger burns
3. **Persist** — longer DoT duration
4. **Burst** — spreads burn on explosion

### Best Fire Spell Forms

- **Nova** — applies burn to everything around you
- **Vortex** — persistent fire tornado that stacks burn
- **Lance** — high single-hit damage = large burn ticks

## Ice ❄️ — Deep Dive

### Freeze Mechanic

- Each ice spell applies a **slow stack** (15% slow per stack)
- At **4 stacks**, the enemy is **frozen** for 3 seconds
- **Shatter:** Hitting a frozen enemy with a non-ice spell deals **200% bonus damage** and breaks the freeze
- Chill Memories increase slow amount and freeze duration

### Freeze Thresholds by Enemy Type

| Enemy Type | Stacks to Freeze | Freeze Duration |
|-----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Common | 4 | 3s |
| Elite | 6 | 2s |
| Mini-boss | 8 | 1.5s |
| Guardian | Immune to freeze (slow only) | 50% slow max |

### When to Use Ice

| Scenario | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Learning new content | Freeze gives you time to observe and react |
| Fire enemies | Elemental advantage — 50% bonus damage |
| High difficulty | Survivability is paramount |
| Elite packs | Freeze one, kill the rest |
| Boss fights | Slow is still effective even if freeze doesn't work |

### Ice Memory Priorities

1. **Chill** — increases freeze buildup speed
2. **Persist** — longer freeze duration
3. **Barrier** — survive while building freeze stacks
4. **Expand** — freeze multiple enemies at once

### Best Ice Spell Forms

- **Wall** — applies continuous chill to enemies walking through it
- **Shard** — piercing with chill application per hit
- **Nova** — mass slow/freeze in an emergency

## Lightning ⚡ — Deep Dive

### Chain Mechanic

- Lightning spells **chain to the nearest enemy** within 8m
- Each chain jump deals **70% of the previous hit's damage**
- Base chain count: **2 jumps** (hits 3 total enemies)
- Chain Memories increase jump count and range
- Chains have a **10% chance to stun** for 0.5s per hit

### Chain Damage Calculation

```
Primary target: 100% damage
Chain jump 1:   70% damage
Chain jump 2:   49% damage
Chain jump 3:   34% damage
Chain jump 4:   24% damage (with Chain IV)
```

### When to Use Lightning

| Scenario | Why |
|----------|-----|
| Speed farming | Clears groups faster than any other element |
| Dense enemy packs | Chain value increases with enemy density |
| Lightning enemies | Neutral damage (no advantage/disadvantage vs lightning) |
| Ash Forest farming | Neutral against fire, chains through ember wisps |

### Lightning Memory Priorities

1. **Chain** — more jumps = exponentially more damage
2. **Hasten** — faster casts = more chains
3. **Charge** — increases chain stun chance
4. **Amplify** — bigger first hit = bigger chains

### Best Lightning Spell Forms

- **Arc** — continuous beam that chains constantly
- **Wave** — wide cone applies chain to everything hit
- **Orb** — passive chain damage while you cast other spells

## Hybrid Element Combinations

### How Hybrids Work

Placing two different element Memories in adjacent slots creates a hybrid spell:

| Combo | Result | Effect |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Fire + Ice | **Steam Burst** | Explosion + vision-obscuring cloud (enemies miss more) |
| Fire + Lightning | **Plasma Storm** | Rapid bolts that chain AND burn |
| Ice + Lightning | **Frost Storm** | Slowing chains that freeze entire groups |
| Fire + Ice + Lightning | **Tempest** | Massive area damage with all status effects |

### Hybrid Crafting Tips

- Hybrid spells cost **50% more mana** than pure spells
- The **center slot element** determines primary damage type
- **Adjacent slot elements** determine secondary effects
- Only adjacent (touching) slots create hybrids — diagonal doesn't count

## Elemental Build Progression

```
Early Game (0–10 hrs):
  Pick ONE element → build around it
  Fire: Inferno Machine
  Ice: Blizzard King
  Lightning: Storm Weaver

Mid Game (10–25 hrs):
  Add a second element → hybrid builds
  Frostfire Hybrid (Fire + Ice) — safest progression

Late Game (25+ hrs):
  Master all three → Tempest God
  Requires Legendary Memories in all elements
```
