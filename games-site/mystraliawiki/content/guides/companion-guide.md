# NPC Guide — All Hub Characters, Quests & Interactions in Echoes of Mystralia

Between runs, you return to the **Azimuth Temple** — the central hub where NPCs offer upgrades, quests, lore, and services. This guide covers every character, what they offer, and how to unlock their full potential.

## Hub Overview: The Azimuth Temple

The Azimuth Temple serves as your home base. As you progress, more NPCs arrive and existing ones unlock additional services. The temple has 4 distinct areas: the **Main Hall** (merchants), the **Sanctum** (Lotus progression), the **Archives** (lore and Memory storage), and the **Courtyard** (quest givers and events).

## Main Hall Characters

### Elder Veylos — The Lotus Keeper

The Elder oversees the **Lotus progression system**. He is the first NPC you meet and remains the most important throughout the game.

**Services:**
- **Lotus Upgrades** — Spend resources to unlock permanent stat improvements (health, mana, spell power)
- **Memory Capacity** — Expand how many Memories you can carry per run
- **Spell Slots** — Unlock additional spell form slots (up to 6 at max)
- **Lotus Reset** — Reallocate your Lotus progression (costs 500 gold)

**Unlock Conditions:**
- Available from the start
- Unlock "Lotus Mastery" upgrade tier by spending 10,000 total gold on upgrades
- Lotus Reset becomes available after your first full region clear

**Tips:**
- Invest in health first (3 upgrades), then spell power (5 upgrades), then Memory capacity
- The Lotus Reset is cheap — don't hesitate to re-spec if your build direction changes

### Mira — The Memory Archivist

Mira catalogs and stores the Memories you collect. Her services are essential for managing your growing Memory collection.

**Services:**
- **Memory Vault** — View all 110 Memories you've discovered
- **Memory Equip** — Select which Memories to bring on your next run
- **Memory Fusion** — Combine 3 duplicate Memories to create a higher-tier version (unlocked after discovering 30 Memories)
- **Memory Lore** — Read lore entries for each Memory (completionist content)

**Unlock Conditions:**
- Arrives at the temple after your first successful Ash Forest run
- Memory Fusion unlocks when you have 30+ unique Memories discovered

**Tips:**
- Check the Memory Vault regularly to see what combinations you're missing
- Prioritize Memories that match your preferred element — don't spread too thin
- Fusion is best used on common Memories to create rare variants

### Kaelen — The Wandering Merchant

Kaelen travels between temples and brings rare goods. His inventory refreshes after every run.

**Services:**
- **Basic Goods** — Health potions, mana potions, scrolls (always available)
- **Special Stock** — Rotating selection of rare relics, Memory caches, and cosmetics
- **Warden's Compass** — One-time purchase, reveals hidden rooms on minimap (500 gold — essential for exploration)
- **Relic Shards** — Currency for upgrading relics at the Altar

**Unlock Conditions:**
- Arrives after 3 total runs completed
- Special Stock unlocks after you spend 1,000 gold at his shop
- Rare items require region progress (e.g., Frozen Depths access for ice-themed relics)

**Tips:**
- Always buy the Warden's Compass as soon as possible
- Check Kaelen's inventory before every run — his special stock can contain build-defining relics
- Save gold for rare items (1,500–3,000 gold range)

## Sanctum Characters

### The Guardian of the Lotus

A silent, armored figure who maintains the Sanctum. They offer **challenge runs** — modified runs with special conditions and better rewards.

**Challenge Types:**
- **Iron Will** — No healing items drop. Reward: "Unyielding" relic set
- **Elemental Surge** — Only one element type spawns. Reward: Element-specific Memories
- **Time Warp** — All enemies move 50% faster. Reward: "Temporal Shard" relic
- **Glass World** — Your health is halved, enemy health halved. Reward: Rare Memory cache

**Unlock Conditions:**
- Unlocked after clearing all 3 regions at least once
- Each challenge requires 500 gold to attempt

**Tips:**
- Challenge runs are the best way to farm specific relic sets
- Elemental Surge is the easiest challenge — pick your best element
- Iron Will is the hardest but rewards the best defensive relics

### Lysara — The Enchantress

Lysara enhances your Relics with additional properties.

**Services:**
- **Relic Enchantment** — Add a random bonus effect to a relic (costs Relic Shards)
- **Relic Purification** — Remove a negative effect from a Cursed relic (costs gold)
- **Relic Transmutation** — Convert 3 unwanted relics into 1 random new relic

**Unlock Conditions:**
- Arrives after you collect 10 unique relics
- Relic Purification unlocks after defeating a hidden boss

## Archives Characters

### Scholar Torvin — The Lore Keeper

Torvin catalogs the world's history. He offers lore fragments in exchange for Memories you're willing to sacrifice.

**Services:**
- **Lore Exchange** — Sacrifice a Memory to unlock a lore entry about Mystralia's history
- **Boss Lore** — Defeating bosses unlocks lore entries about their origins
- **World Map Fragment** — Watch the map expand as you explore new regions

**Unlock Conditions:**
- Arrives after you discover the Archives (post-Ash Forest clear)
- Lore Exchange available from the start of his arrival

**Tips:**
- Only sacrifice duplicate Memories to avoid losing build options
- Lore entries unlock hints about hidden areas and secret bosses

### Rina — The Courier

Rina delivers special requests from other NPCs, effectively serving as the **quest system**.

**Quest Flow:**
1. Talk to Rina to see available requests
2. Complete the objective (deliver an item, defeat a specific enemy, explore a hidden area)
3. Return to Rina to claim your reward

**Quest Types:**
- **Fetch quests** — Bring specific Memories or relics to NPCs
- **Exploration quests** — Discover specific hidden areas
- **Combat quests** — Defeat named elite enemies
- **Collection quests** — Gather a certain number of resources

**Unlock Conditions:**
- Arrives after you complete 5 runs total
- New quests unlock as you explore regions and meet NPCs

## Courtyard Characters

### The Mysterious Traveler

An enigmatic figure who appears randomly after runs. Offers cryptic hints about hidden areas, secret bosses, and lore.

**Services:**
- **Riddles** — Solve riddles for Memory and relic rewards
- **Prophecies** — Vague hints about future content and updates
- **Secret Boss Location** — After solving 3 riddles, reveals the location of a hidden boss

**Unlock Conditions:**
- Appears randomly after any run (approx. 25% chance)
- More likely to appear after you discover a hidden area

### The Pet Vendor

A cheerful vendor who sells cosmetic pets that follow you during runs.

**Available Pets:**
- **Ember Fox** — Leaves a faint fire trail (500 gold)
- **Frost Owl** — Perches on your shoulder (800 gold)
- **Storm Sprite** — Small lightning elemental that sparkles (1,200 gold)
- **Void Kitten** — Rare pet, appears only after beating a hidden boss (2,000 gold)

**Note:** Pets are purely cosmetic. They don't affect gameplay, but they're a popular gold sink for endgame players.
