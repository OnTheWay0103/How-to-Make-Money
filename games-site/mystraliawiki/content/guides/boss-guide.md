## Therakan, Ashen Warden — Complete Strategy Guide

Therakan is the first Guardian most players encounter, guarding the Ash Forest. A massive fire-aspected beast that controls the burning woodland.

### Stats (Normal Difficulty)

| Stat | Value |
|------|-------|
| Health | ~8,000 HP |
| Phases | 3 |
| Damage Type | Fire (80%), Physical (20%) |
| Weakness | Ice ❄️ |
| Resistance | Fire 🔥 (50% damage reduction) |

### Attack Patterns

#### Phase 1 (100%–70% HP)

| Attack | Tell | Response |
|--------|------|----------|
| **Flame Swipe** | Raises right claw, brief glow | Dodge sideways. Two-swipe combo. |
| **Ember Spit** | Head rears back, mouth glows | Strafe continuously. Projectile tracks slightly. |
| **Ground Stomp** | Stands on hind legs, pauses 1s | Jump or use Phase Memory. Shockwave travels along ground. |
| **Charge** | Lowers head, scrapes ground twice | Dodge sideways at the last moment. Long recovery window — punish here. |

**Best punish window:** After Charge — Therakan skids to a stop and is stunned for ~3 seconds. This is your primary damage window.

#### Phase 2 (70%–40% HP)

Therakan ignites the arena. Burning patches appear on the ground.

| New Attack | Tell | Response |
|-----------|------|----------|
| **Fire Breath** | Deep inhale, throat glows bright orange | Move to Therakan's side/rear. Covers 180° frontal cone. |
| **Meteor Rain** | Roars at the sky, 3 red circles appear | Watch ground indicators. Dodge out of circles. 3 waves. |
| **Flame Trail** | Drags claw along ground while moving | Don't stand in the fire trail. Deals rapid tick damage. |
| **Enraged Charge** | Faster version of Phase 1 Charge | Same dodge timing, but longer recovery. Still the best punish. |

**Phase 2 Strategy:** Manage arena space carefully. The burning patches persist and limit safe zones. Lure Therakan to clean ground before engaging.

#### Phase 3 (40%–0% HP)

Therakan enters **Berserk Mode**. All attacks are faster and hit harder.

| New Attack | Tell | Response |
|-----------|------|----------|
| **Inferno Nova** | Stands still, fire aura expands outward | Run to arena edge. Covers entire inner arena. |
| **Triple Charge** | Three rapid charges in sequence | Dodge #1, immediately prepare for #2 and #3. Don't get greedy between charges. |
| **Desperation Slam** | Below 15% HP — continuous ground stomps | Sustained DPS race. Pop all cooldowns. Use Ice Wall to block shockwaves. |

**Phase 3 Strategy:** This is a DPS race. Save your strongest spells and any damage Relics for this phase. Ice Wall is invaluable for blocking the shockwave spam.

### Recommended Spell Loadout vs Therakan

## General Boss-Fighting Tips

1. **Elemental advantage is everything** — using the right element can halve the fight duration
2. **Learn the attack rotation** — every Guardian has a fixed pattern; memorize it
3. **Phase Memory is S-tier for bosses** — invulnerability frames during casts save runs
4. **Don't hoard cooldowns** — use spells on cooldown unless saving for a specific punish window
5. **Positioning beats reflexes** — pre-position for known attacks rather than reacting
6. **Guardian re-fights scale** — each re-kill increases difficulty but improves drop rates
7. **Watch your mana** — running out mid-Phase 3 is a common cause of wipes
