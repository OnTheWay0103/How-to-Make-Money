# Spell Crafting Guide — Complete System Breakdown

The **spell crafting system** is the heart of Echoes of Mystralia. Unlike traditional RPGs where you select spells from a list, Echoes of Mystralia lets you *build* your spells from components. This guide covers every mechanic in detail.

## How Spell Crafting Works

The system has three layers:

1. **Spell Forms** (base templates) — determine *what kind* of spell you cast
2. **Elements** (damage type) — determine *what element* the spell uses
3. **Memories** (modifiers) — determine *how* the spell behaves

The unique mechanic: **the physical position of a Memory within the spell form's grid changes the spell's output.** Two identical sets of Memories arranged differently produce different spells.

## The Three Elements

### Fire 🔥

- **Effect**: Damage over time (burn)
- **Strength**: Highest raw damage output
- **Best vs**: Ice enemies, groups of weak enemies
- **Memory synergy**: Stack burn duration and spread effects
- **Ideal spell forms**: Nova, Vortex, Barrage

### Ice ❄️

- **Effect**: Slow and freeze
- **Strength**: Crowd control and survivability
- **Best vs**: Lightning enemies, fast-moving enemies
- **Memory synergy**: Freeze duration, shatter damage on frozen targets
- **Ideal spell forms**: Wall, Shard, Mine

### Lightning ⚡

- **Effect**: Chain between enemies
- **Strength**: Best AoE clear speed
- **Best vs**: Fire enemies, tightly grouped enemies
- **Memory synergy**: Chain count, bounce range, stun chance
- **Ideal spell forms**: Arc, Wave, Orb

## The Elemental Rock-Paper-Scissors

| Enemy Element | Use This Element | Reason |
|--------------|-----------------|--------|
| Fire enemies | Ice ❄️ | Freeze prevents their DoT application |
| Ice enemies | Fire 🔥 | Burn melts through their defense |
| Lightning enemies | Ice ❄️ | Freeze stops their chain attacks |
| Physical enemies | Lightning ⚡ | Chain clears groups fastest |

## Memory Modifiers

Memories are collected from defeated enemies and come in several categories:

### Offensive Memories
- **Amplify**: Increases base damage by a percentage
- **Chain**: Adds chain-jump behavior to any spell
- **Multishot**: Splits projectiles into multiple copies
- **Burst**: Adds an explosion on impact

### Defensive Memories
- **Barrier**: Adds a temporary shield on cast
- **Leech**: Restores health on-hit
- **Phase**: Adds brief invulnerability frames to the cast animation

### Utility Memories
- **Expand**: Increases area of effect size
- **Hasten**: Reduces cast time and cooldown
- **Persist**: Extends duration of AoE and zone spells
- **Attract**: Pulls enemies toward the spell's impact point

### Elemental Conversion Memories
- **Ignite**: Converts any spell to partial fire damage
- **Chill**: Converts any spell to partial ice damage
- **Charge**: Converts any spell to partial lightning damage

## Memory Positioning: The Grid System

This is where the system gets deep. Each spell form has a grid with slots. Where you place a Memory matters:

| Position | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **Center slot** | Memory applies at full strength |
| **Outer slots** | Memory applies at 50% strength but can stack |
| **Adjacent slots** | Same-element Memories adjacent to each other gain a 20% synergy bonus |
| **Opposite slots** | Opposite-element Memories create hybrid spells (Fire + Ice = Steam explosion) |

**Pro tip:** Place your primary damage Memory in the center and stack synergy Memories in adjacent outer slots for maximum output.

## Hybrid Element Spell Crafting

One of the most powerful techniques is combining elements:

| Combo | Element Mix | Result |
|-------|------------|--------|
| **Steam Burst** | Fire + Ice | Explosion that deals damage + creates a vision-obscuring cloud |
| **Plasma Storm** | Fire + Lightning | Rapid-fire bolts that chain AND burn |
| **Frost Storm** | Ice + Lightning | Slowing chains that freeze entire groups |
| **Tempest** | All three | Massive area damage with all status effects — but very hard to craft |

## Sample Spell Builds

### Beginner Fire Build
- **Form**: Bolt
- **Center**: Amplify (fire)
- **Adjacent**: Ignite x2
- **Outer**: Multishot
- **Result**: Fast, high-damage fire bolts that split into 3 projectiles

### Advanced Ice Control Build
- **Form**: Nova
- **Center**: Expand (ice)
- **Adjacent**: Chill + Persist
- **Outer**: Barrier + Leech
- **Result**: Massive frost nova that freezes everything around you, grants a shield, and heals

### Endgame Lightning Clear Build
- **Form**: Arc
- **Center**: Chain (lightning)
- **Adjacent**: Charge + Amplify
- **Outer**: Hasten x2
- **Result**: Incredibly fast-casting lightning beam that chains between enemies with high damage

## Spell Crafting Tips

1. **Save multiple loadouts** — create one per element for quick swapping between regions
2. **Test in the Training Area** — the Azimuth Temple has a practice dummy to test spell output
3. **Hybrid spells cost more mana** — balance damage output with mana sustainability
4. **Rarity matters** — Memories come in Common, Rare, and Legendary tiers with scaling effects
5. **Don't neglect cooldown reduction** — Hasten Memories are among the most valuable
